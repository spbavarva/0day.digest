The China-aligned espionage group Mustang Panda is running two campaigns
against Indian government and hydropower targets, deploying new malware and
using the legitimate cloud service Zoho WorkDrive as a command-and-control
channel.

Acronis's Threat Research Unit found active compromises inside Indian
government networks, including machines belonging to senior administrative
staff. The group continues a pattern of abusing trusted cloud platforms to
blend malicious traffic with legitimate use.
