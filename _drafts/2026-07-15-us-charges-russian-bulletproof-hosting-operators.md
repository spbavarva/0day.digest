US federal prosecutors have unsealed charges against three Russian nationals
accused of operating Media Land and sister company ML Cloud, a St.
Petersburg-based bulletproof hosting (BPH) service. Prosecutors allege the
operation knowingly provided infrastructure and technical support to
ransomware gangs, contributing to more than $62 million in damages to victims
worldwide.

The case is part of a broader push against hosting providers that shield
cybercriminal infrastructure from takedown.
