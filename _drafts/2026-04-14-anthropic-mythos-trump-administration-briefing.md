Anthropic co-founder Jack Clark confirmed at the Semafor World Economy summit that the company
briefed the Trump administration on Claude Mythos Preview's capabilities, even while Anthropic
has an active lawsuit against the U.S. government. Clark explained the decision reflects
Anthropic's view that responsible AI development requires engaging government stakeholders
regardless of adversarial conditions.

The briefing illustrates how advanced AI models with dual-use security capabilities have become
direct concerns for national security policy at the executive level. Both the DoD's investment
interest and now executive branch briefings signal that Mythos-class model capabilities are
being assessed for national security implications in real time.

The disclosure adds context to Anthropic's navigation of government relationships: simultaneously
a commercial AI company, a national security asset of interest, and a government critic.
