Kaspersky's GReAT team released Q1 2026 mobile threat statistics, highlighting updated variants of two notable malware families. SparkCat, an OCR-based infostealer that extracts cryptocurrency wallet recovery phrases from device screenshots, has new versions with updated obfuscation. Triada, a persistent Android banking trojan with deep system integration, also saw new variants targeting updated Android builds. Both families indicate sustained investment in mobile-targeting infrastructure by organized threat actors. Mobile device management policies and app-source controls remain the primary mitigations.
