Authorities across Europe and North America dismantled First VPN Service, a criminal VPN used by more than 25 ransomware groups for network reconnaissance, data theft, scanning, and denial-of-service attacks. The operation was led by France and the Netherlands, with support from several other nations following an investigation that began in December; the Netherlands seized 800 servers and arrested two men. The FBI notes First VPN was integral to ransomware group operational security. Law enforcement is sharing intelligence on service users with affected organizations.
