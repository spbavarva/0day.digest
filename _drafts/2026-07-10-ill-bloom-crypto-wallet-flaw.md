Security firm Coinspect disclosed a crypto wallet flaw it calls "Ill
Bloom": affected wallet software generated recovery-phrase seed material
using weak randomness, letting an attacker reconstruct the phrase and take
full control of the funds it protects. Coinspect confirmed one coordinated
sweep in May that has drained $3.1 million so far. Anyone who generated a
wallet with affected software should treat that recovery phrase as
compromised and migrate funds to a newly generated wallet.
