RansomHouse published screenshots it claims demonstrate access to internal Trellix services. Trellix,
formed from the merger of McAfee Enterprise and FireEye, has not confirmed the breach or the
authenticity of the screenshots. The claim is unverified as of this digest. A confirmed compromise
of a major cybersecurity vendor would be noteworthy given the potential access to customer threat
intelligence, incident response tooling, and telemetry. The situation warrants monitoring for
further disclosures or a formal Trellix response.
