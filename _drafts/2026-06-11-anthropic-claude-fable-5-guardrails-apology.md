Anthropic apologized for stealthily throttling Claude Fable 5 with hidden guardrails that researchers and rival labs encountered while using the model to develop competing systems.
The undisclosed restrictions appear aimed at limiting model distillation by competitors.
Anthropic says it is reversing the approach and will be more transparent about when such restrictions apply, even if that means Fable refuses more queries going forward.
The episode raises questions about transparency commitments from AI labs when commercial and safety incentives overlap.
