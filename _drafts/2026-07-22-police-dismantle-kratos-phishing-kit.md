German and US law enforcement dismantled the core infrastructure behind
Kratos, described by German investigators as one of the world's most
widely used criminal phishing kits. The kit was built to steal Microsoft
365 session tokens and bypass MFA. Indonesian authorities arrested the
alleged developer and operator as part of the joint action.
