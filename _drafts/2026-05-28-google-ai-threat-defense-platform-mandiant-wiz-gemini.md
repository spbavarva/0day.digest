Google unveiled its AI Threat Defense platform, integrating Mandiant threat
intelligence, Wiz cloud security data, and Gemini AI into a unified offering
designed to detect and respond to AI-powered cyberattacks. The platform positions
itself as using AI to fight AI — automating detection at the machine speed that
modern attacks demand. The announcement comes amid rising concern about AI-assisted
attacks from actors like GreyVibe. Security teams evaluating AI-native detection
platforms should compare Google's integrated approach against standalone point
products in their existing stack.
