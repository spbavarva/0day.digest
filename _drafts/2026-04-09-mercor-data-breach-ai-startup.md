Mercor, an AI-powered hiring platform valued at $10 billion, is facing lawsuits and reportedly
losing major customers following a data breach in which a hacker gained unauthorized access to
company systems. The specific data types compromised and total number of affected users have
not been disclosed in available reporting.

AI hiring platforms typically handle sensitive candidate data including resumes, work histories,
employment verification records, and potentially biometric or video interview data — all of which
represent high-value targets for both financially motivated actors and corporate espionage.
Companies using Mercor's platform for candidate data should review contractual data handling
obligations and assess their exposure.
