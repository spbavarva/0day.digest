Flashpoint has published a detailed breakdown of "The Com," a decentralized criminal ecosystem that combines hacking, extortion, and real-world violence to drive a pipeline of cyber fraud and adolescent exploitation. Unlike traditional organized crime, The Com operates through loosely affiliated online communities rather than hierarchical structures, complicating disruption efforts.

The ecosystem functions as a threat multiplier: members share tooling and victim lists, escalate from cyber fraud to physical violence as leverage, and actively recruit minors. Security teams investigating social engineering, SIM swapping, or extortion campaigns should factor in The Com's hybrid nature when building threat actor profiles and incident response playbooks.
