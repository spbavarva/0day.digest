Meta has disclosed that 20,225 Instagram users had their accounts hijacked after attackers abused Meta's AI-powered support system to reset account passwords. The incident is a concrete example of an AI-driven workflow becoming the weak link in an account-recovery process rather than a traditional credential attack. Instagram users who notice unexpected password-reset activity should check recent account access and re-secure with strong, unique credentials and 2FA.
