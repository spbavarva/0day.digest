Google announced an AI Threat Defense platform designed to help security teams detect and respond to AI-powered cyberattacks, combining capabilities from Mandiant threat intelligence, Wiz cloud security sensors, and Gemini large model reasoning. The platform is positioned as an AI-versus-AI defense tool — using Gemini's analysis to process signals surfaced by Mandiant and Wiz. Specific detection capabilities, integration depth, and pricing have not yet been disclosed. The announcement reflects the broader industry shift toward LLM-assisted SOC operations and signals Google's intent to compete directly in the AI security platform space.
