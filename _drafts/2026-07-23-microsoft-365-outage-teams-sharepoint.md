Microsoft Teams and several other Microsoft 365 services are experiencing an
ongoing outage, with users reporting problems accessing Teams, SharePoint,
Excel, and the Microsoft 365 Admin Center. No root cause had been disclosed
at time of writing. Organizations relying on these services for
business-critical workflows should monitor the Microsoft 365 admin center
service health dashboard for updates.
