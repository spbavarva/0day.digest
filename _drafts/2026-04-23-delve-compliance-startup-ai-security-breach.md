TechCrunch confirmed that Delve, an AI compliance certification startup, performed security
certifications for Context AI — the AI agent training company that disclosed a security incident
last week — making this at least the second Delve customer to experience a significant security
incident. Delve conducts compliance certifications that typically involve privileged access to
customer environments.

The pattern raises questions about whether third-party compliance and certification vendors in the
AI ecosystem represent an under-scrutinized supply chain risk. Organizations using compliance
vendors for SOC 2, ISO, or AI-specific certifications should scope the access granted to those
vendors using least-privilege principles and treat compliance vendor credentials as a potential
lateral movement path in their threat models.
