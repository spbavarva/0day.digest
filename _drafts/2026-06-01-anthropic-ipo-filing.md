Anthropic filed confidentially with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission to kick off the IPO process, reaching a key milestone in a race with OpenAI to go public. The filing sets the stage for what is expected to be a high-profile public offering given Anthropic's most recent fundraise, which established a $40 billion valuation led by Google.

Anthropic is the maker of Claude and operates AI safety research alongside its commercial API and consumer products.
