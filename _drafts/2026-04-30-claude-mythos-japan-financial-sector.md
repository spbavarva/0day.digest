Japan's financial services sector is expressing alarm over Anthropic's Mythos AI model, described in reporting as a "superhacker" AI capable of autonomous offensive operations. Cybersecurity experts cited in the piece are notably less concerned, characterizing the institutional reaction as disproportionate to Mythos's demonstrated and restricted capabilities.

This mirrors earlier panic seen in US and EU financial institutions following earlier Mythos coverage. The widening gap between practitioner assessment and board-level risk perception is itself a security risk — reactive posture shifts driven by headline fear rather than evidence can divert resources from real threats. Finance sector CISOs should prepare calibrated briefings addressing AI-assisted cyber threats with specifics rather than broad AI alarm.
