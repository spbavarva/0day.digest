A ransomware attack against ChipSoft, a Dutch healthcare software vendor, forced the
company to disable portions of its digital services relied upon by hospitals and patients
across the Netherlands. The national cybersecurity center for the Dutch healthcare sector
(Z-CERT) confirmed the disruption.

ChipSoft supplies software to a substantial share of Dutch hospitals, meaning the attack
has downstream operational impact across multiple healthcare institutions — a supply chain
risk materializing against critical-care infrastructure. TTPs, the responsible ransomware
group, and IOCs have not yet been publicly disclosed.
