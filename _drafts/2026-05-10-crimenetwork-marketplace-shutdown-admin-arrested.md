German authorities shut down a relaunched version of Crimenetwork, a
well-known criminal marketplace, and arrested its operator. The platform had
reportedly generated over €3.6 million before the takedown.

Crimenetwork had previously been dismantled; this action targeted a reboot
attempt by a new administrator. No technical details about the platform's
infrastructure or sold goods were included in the disclosure.
