The Vietnam-aligned threat actor OceanLotus has been linked to two campaigns deploying the SPECTRALVIPER backdoor against domestic targets.
One campaign ran a prolonged cyber-espionage operation against a Vietnamese infrastructure and transport construction corporation between mid-2024 and February 2026.
A second campaign targeted Vietnamese stock investors and reportedly involved a supply chain attack component.
Organizations in Vietnam's infrastructure and financial sectors should watch for SPECTRALVIPER indicators and review third-party software dependencies for tampering.
