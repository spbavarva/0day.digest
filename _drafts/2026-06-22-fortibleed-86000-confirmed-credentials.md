Fortinet has responded to the FortiBleed credential-harvesting campaign,
confirming that attackers built a database of more than 86,000 working
VPN credentials during the operation. The confirmation means the
previously reported leaked credentials are not just exposed but actively
valid, raising the risk of unauthorized access to FortiGate VPN
endpoints. Organizations using FortiGate VPNs should treat credential
rotation and authentication log review as urgent rather than
precautionary at this point.
