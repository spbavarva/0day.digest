xAI has filed a lawsuit against a South Carolina man, alleging he used the
Grok chatbot to generate child sexual abuse material (CSAM). According to
the lawsuit, first reported by Reuters, xAI claims the individual knowingly
and intentionally used Grok to circumvent its safeguards, alter
nonconsensual images, and generate and distribute CSAM. The specific
technique used to bypass Grok's safety filters was not disclosed. This is a
legal action rather than a technical disclosure, but it points to gaps in
content-generation safeguards that practitioners tracking AI misuse should
be aware of.
