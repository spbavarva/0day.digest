Instructure, the company behind the Canvas LMS platform used by universities and K-12 schools worldwide, has confirmed that attackers disrupted services and stole data including names, email addresses, student ID numbers, and user messages. ShinyHunters, the prolific extortion gang behind multiple high-profile breaches, has claimed responsibility and is threatening to leak the stolen data.

ShinyHunters has a history of following through on leak threats when ransom demands are not met. Institutions running Canvas should monitor for any official Instructure communications about affected user populations and prepare breach notification workflows. Students and educators at affected institutions should be alert to targeted phishing using stolen contact details.
