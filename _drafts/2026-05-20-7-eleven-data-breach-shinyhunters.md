7-Eleven has confirmed a data breach following claims by the ShinyHunters threat group. The company discovered the incident on April 8 and determined that attackers gained access to systems used to store franchisee documents. The scope of affected individuals has not been publicly disclosed.

ShinyHunters has been linked to numerous high-profile data extortion incidents. 7-Eleven franchisee operators should review any sensitive business documents stored on corporate systems for exposure. Notification obligations will depend on the content of the affected document systems and applicable breach notification laws.
