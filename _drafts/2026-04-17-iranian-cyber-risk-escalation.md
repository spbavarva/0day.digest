Unit 42 has published an updated threat brief on Iranian cyber operations, documenting escalating
phishing campaigns, hacktivist activity, and financially motivated cybercrime. The brief includes
direct analyst observations and specific defender recommendations.

Iran-linked actors have historically targeted critical infrastructure, defense contractors, government
agencies, and organizations in the Middle East and Western countries. This update reflects increased
operational tempo amid current geopolitical tensions.

Defenders should review Unit 42's published indicators and recommendations, prioritize email security
controls, and ensure monitoring covers both phishing delivery and hacktivist disruption patterns.
Organizations in sectors historically targeted by Iranian APTs — energy, government, financial —
should treat this as a prompt to review their detection coverage.
