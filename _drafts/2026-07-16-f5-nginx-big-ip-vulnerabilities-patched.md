F5 has patched multiple vulnerabilities across NGINX and BIG-IP products
that could let attackers modify configurations, terminate or restart
processes, cross security boundaries, leak memory, or execute code. Given
the widespread deployment of both product lines in production web and
application delivery infrastructure, administrators should prioritize
applying F5's updates.
