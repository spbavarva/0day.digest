Kaspersky researchers detailed a large-scale campaign distributing the
AsyncRAT trojan disguised as ScreenConnect, a legitimate remote access
tool, breaking down the infection chain and the campaign's C2
infrastructure. A related wave of the same operation uses SEO-poisoned
software download sites to distribute malicious installers masquerading as
popular tools like OBS Studio, DNS Jumper, DS4Windows, and Bandicam.
Defenders should treat unsolicited ScreenConnect installers and SEO-ranked
software download sites with added scrutiny.
