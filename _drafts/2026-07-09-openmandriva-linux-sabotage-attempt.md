The OpenMandriva Linux project says one of its contributors attempted to
sabotage the project following an internal dispute among contributors. The
incident underscores that insider-driven supply chain risk isn't confined
to large ecosystems like npm or PyPI — smaller open source distributions
with limited maintainer review capacity are exposed to the same class of
threat. No further technical detail on the attempted sabotage was
available in initial reporting.
