The hack-and-leak group FulcrumSec claims to have stolen 1.3 TB of data from Novo Nordisk, the pharmaceutical company behind Ozempic and Wegovy. The claim has not been independently verified and Novo Nordisk has not publicly confirmed a breach at time of reporting. FulcrumSec is a data-theft and extortion actor that does not rely on ransomware encryption, instead threatening public data dumps. If confirmed, the breach would represent a major exposure of pharmaceutical research, employee, and potentially patient data. Organizations should monitor for a public dump announcement or subsequent extortion escalation.
