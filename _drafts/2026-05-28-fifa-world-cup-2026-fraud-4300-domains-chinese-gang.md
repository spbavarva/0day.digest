A Chinese-speaking cybercriminal gang has registered more than 4,300 fraudulent
domains impersonating FIFA's official web presence since August 2025, ahead of
the 2026 World Cup. The campaign targets fans to steal personal and financial
information and sell fake tickets. The FBI separately issued a warning on fake
FIFA websites running similar fraud schemes. The scale — thousands of domains
sustained over months — indicates a well-resourced operation. Fans purchasing
World Cup tickets or travel packages should use only the official FIFA website
and verify URLs carefully before entering payment details.
