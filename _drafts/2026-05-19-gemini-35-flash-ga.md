Google released Gemini 3.5 Flash to general availability at Google I/O 2026, skipping the preview
stage and deploying directly across Gemini app, AI Mode in Google Search, Gemini API, Gemini
Enterprise Agent Platform, and Android Studio. Simon Willison notes the model is more expensive than
prior Flash variants, but Google is treating it as a production backbone across core products.

For developers, access is via Gemini API in Google AI Studio and Android Studio. Enterprises can
access it through Gemini Enterprise. The rapid cross-product deployment makes 3.5 Flash the effective
default Google AI model for the near term.
