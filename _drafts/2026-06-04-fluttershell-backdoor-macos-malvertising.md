Palo Alto Networks Unit 42 detailed Operation FlutterBridge, a macOS malvertising campaign distributing a new backdoor called FlutterShell through malicious ads impersonating legitimate software on Google and YouTube. The campaign is assessed as the next stage of JSCoreRunner/FileRipple activity first identified in August 2025. macOS users and enterprise security teams should update endpoint detection signatures for FlutterShell and advise users to download software only from official vendor sites, not ad-served search results.
