Microsoft's July Patch Tuesday resolved a record 570 security vulnerabilities
across its product line, which the company says was made possible in part by
AI-assisted vulnerability discovery. No single critical, actively exploited
flaw was highlighted in this release. The count marks a continued year-long
trend of surging disclosure volumes at Microsoft.
