Vidar infostealer has consolidated to the top of the criminal MaaS market following law enforcement disruptions of the Lumma and Rhadamanthys operations last year. Vidar targets browser-stored credentials, session cookies, crypto wallets, and saved passwords, and is distributed through affiliate networks.

Security teams should update their detection coverage for Vidar-specific indicators and behaviors — particularly credential harvesting from browser profile directories. The infostealer market reliably consolidates around survivors when competitors are disrupted; expect Vidar deployment volumes to remain elevated until the next law enforcement action or a credible competitor emerges.
