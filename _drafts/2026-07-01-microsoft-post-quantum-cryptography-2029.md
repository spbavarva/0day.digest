Microsoft is accelerating its post-quantum cryptography migration roadmap,
moving up its target for replacing current encryption standards to 2029.
Azure CTO Mark Russinovich cited advances in quantum computing research as
shifting the risk horizon sooner than previously projected. No specific new
vulnerabilities or exploits were disclosed — this is a roadmap and timeline
change. Organizations with long data-retention requirements should begin
inventorying where they depend on cryptography that PQC will eventually
replace.
