Chinese regulators have approved Apple Intelligence for launch in China
through partnerships with Alibaba and Baidu, a deal that had been rumored
for over a year. The approval marks a significant step for Apple's AI
ambitions in one of its largest markets, where foreign AI models face
regulatory restrictions and Apple has needed local partners to offer
compliant AI features.
