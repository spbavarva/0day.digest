OpenAI unveiled GPT-5.6 "Sol," which it describes as its most advanced
cybersecurity-focused AI model to date. The company claims Sol matches the
performance of competing systems, including Anthropic's Mythos Preview,
while using roughly a third of the output tokens. No further benchmark
detail, availability date, or pricing was included in the source summary.
