Additional cybersecurity vendors have disclosed exposure from the Klue
Salesforce integration breach, including HackerOne, Huntress, Jamf,
OneTrust, Recorded Future, Snyk, and Tanium. The growing list shows the
OAuth token abuse at Klue had a wider blast radius across the security
industry than initially understood. Organizations that used the Klue
integration should continue reviewing Salesforce access logs for
anomalous activity tied to the affected OAuth grants.
