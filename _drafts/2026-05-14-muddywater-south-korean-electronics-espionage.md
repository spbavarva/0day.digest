MuddyWater (also tracked as Seedworm and Static Kitten), an Iran-linked threat group, has launched
a broad cyber-espionage campaign targeting at least nine high-profile organizations across multiple
sectors and countries, with a major South Korean electronics manufacturer among the confirmed
targets.

MuddyWater historically relies on spear-phishing for initial access and frequently abuses
legitimate remote management tools — including Atera Agent and ScreenConnect — to maintain
persistence and blend into enterprise IT traffic. Organizations in government, defense, and
technology sectors should audit remote management tool deployments, tighten inbound email controls,
and review threat intel for current MuddyWater IOCs and TTPs published by Recorded Future and
Symantec.
