Fintech company Upbound Group disclosed that threat actors used data
stolen from a prior breach of its systems to generate $13 million in
fraudulent leases through its Acima subsidiary. Upbound has not detailed
the initial intrusion vector or how the stolen data enabled the fraudulent
lease applications. Acima customers should watch for unauthorized lease
agreements or unfamiliar accounts opened in their name.
