ShinyHunters has claimed a second successful attack against Instructure, the company behind Canvas LMS — a platform widely used across higher education and K-12 institutions worldwide. PII belonging to hundreds of millions of people is reportedly at risk as the edtech firm struggles to regain control of its systems.

This follows a prior ShinyHunters incident against the same target, suggesting the attacker maintains persistent access or has re-compromised the environment. Organizations relying on Canvas should monitor Instructure's official communications closely and review data-sharing integrations that could widen the exposure beyond the core platform.
