Dutch authorities have arrested two individuals who ran Dutch companies that allegedly provided bulletproof hosting to Russia-aligned threat actors. Bulletproof hosting underpins criminal and state-sponsored infrastructure by providing C2 servers, phishing sites, and exfiltration staging despite abuse reports.

The takedown represents a meaningful infrastructure disruption for Russia-aligned operations. Threat intelligence teams tracking affected actor clusters should monitor for migration to replacement hosting infrastructure in the coming weeks, as threat actors typically pivot rapidly after infrastructure seizures.
