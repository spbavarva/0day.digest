Unit 42 published research profiling The Gentlemen, a ransomware operation
whose growth is being driven by its affiliate program. The report examines
how affiliate recruitment and structure have fueled the group's rapid
expansion. Specific victim counts, TTPs, and IOCs were not included in the
available summary — see Unit 42's full report for technical detail.
Organizations tracking ransomware-as-a-service activity should be aware of
this emerging affiliate-driven group.
