Microsoft's July 2026 Patch Tuesday shipped 622 CVEs, more than triple June's
previous high of around 200. Two of the fixes close vulnerabilities that
attackers are already exploiting in the wild; Microsoft credited incident
responders with identifying both. Talos separately flagged CVE-2026-56155
among the batch's prominent fixes.

Practitioners should prioritize the two actively exploited bugs first before
working through the rest of the record-setting batch.
