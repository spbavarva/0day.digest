Anthropic announced Claude Science, described as an AI workbench for
scientists that pulls fragmented research tools and datasets into one
environment and can generate figures and visuals. The announcement came
at "The Briefing: AI for Science" event. The launch extends Anthropic's
focus beyond its existing coding tools and general-purpose models into
scientific research use cases. No technical or security details were
included in the announcement.
