China-nexus Tropic Trooper (also tracked as Earth Centaur / KeyBoy) is expanding operations to
include home and SOHO routers as attack vectors, alongside a new focus on Japanese organizations.
The group is known for rapid exploitation of newly disclosed vulnerabilities and has updated its
toolset and TTPs in this campaign. The shift to home router targeting mirrors a broader pattern
of state-sponsored actors using consumer networking gear as a persistent, low-visibility foothold.

Organizations in Japan and any environment with internet-exposed SOHO routers should review
Tropic Trooper indicators of compromise and prioritize router firmware updates. Defenders should
treat compromised home routers as potential network perimeter pivots, not isolated endpoints.
