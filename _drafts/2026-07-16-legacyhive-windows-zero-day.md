A researcher operating under the handle "Nightmare Eclipse" disclosed a
Windows zero-day dubbed LegacyHive. The proof-of-concept exploit was
deliberately stripped down before release to prevent immediate exploitation
of the underlying vulnerability. Details on the specific flaw and affected
Windows versions remain limited in initial reporting; organizations should
watch for an official Microsoft advisory and patch guidance.
