A campaign dubbed "The TFF Trap" combines fileless execution techniques
with loaders that have low antivirus detection rates. The chain is used to
deploy a mix of commodity RATs and credential stealers, including Agent
Tesla, Remcos, XWorm, and Best Private Logger. The combination of fileless
delivery and multiple payload options makes the campaign harder for
signature-based defenses to catch. Security teams should review email
gateway and endpoint detection rules for the named malware families.
