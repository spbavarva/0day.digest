A data breach at a third-party license vendor serving the Texas Parks
and Wildlife Department exposed personal information belonging to
approximately 3 million individuals. Hackers stole the data after
breaching the vendor's systems rather than TPWD's own infrastructure
directly. The exact categories of personal information exposed were not
specified in initial disclosures. Affected individuals should watch for
notification letters and consider credit monitoring as a precaution.
