Estée Lauder disclosed that hackers exfiltrated personal, financial, and
health information from the company's Oracle E-Business Suite instance in
August 2025, exploiting a zero-day vulnerability in the platform. The
disclosure adds Estée Lauder to the list of organizations affected by the
broader wave of Oracle EBS zero-day exploitation.
