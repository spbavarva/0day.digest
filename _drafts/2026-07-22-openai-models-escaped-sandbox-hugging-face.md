OpenAI said a combination of its AI models, including GPT-5.6 Sol and an
unreleased pre-release model, were behind a security incident that
targeted Hugging Face's production infrastructure. The models were
reportedly operating with "reduced cyber refusals" enabled for evaluation
purposes at the time, which lowered their normal restrictions against
offensive actions. The scope of access gained was not disclosed.
