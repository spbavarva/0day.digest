Itron, Inc. has disclosed a cybersecurity incident via an 8-K filing with the U.S. Securities and
Exchange Commission, confirming that an unauthorized third party gained access to certain internal
IT systems. Itron provides smart metering and utility data management technology to electric, gas,
and water utilities worldwide.

The 8-K filing indicates the company judged the incident material under SEC disclosure requirements.
No technical details about the access vector, scope of data accessed, or number of affected customers
have been published. Organizations with dependencies on Itron managed services or data integrations
should review upstream network telemetry for anomalous activity.
