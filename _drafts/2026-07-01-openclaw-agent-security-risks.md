Kaspersky's Securelist published research on security risks in OpenClaw,
examining vulnerabilities and malicious skills affecting the popular AI
agent platform. The research includes mitigation guidance for the issues
found, though the available summary does not specify which vulnerabilities
were identified or their severity. Teams running OpenClaw or similar agent
platforms should review the source research before deploying third-party
skills or extensions.
