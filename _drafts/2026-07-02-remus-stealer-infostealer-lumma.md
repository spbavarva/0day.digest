Flashpoint documented a new infostealer called Remus Stealer that shares
significant structural and behavioral similarities with the Lumma malware
family. The analysis suggests Remus may be a rebrand, fork, or close
derivative of Lumma rather than an entirely new codebase. Defenders who
already track Lumma IOCs and detection signatures should extend that
coverage to watch for Remus Stealer variants.
