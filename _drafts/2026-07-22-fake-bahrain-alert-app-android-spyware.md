A malicious Android app impersonating an official Bahrain alert service is
being distributed through phony Google Play-style sites, delivering a
four-stage surveillance spyware payload. The campaign exploits civilian
fear during Iranian missile strikes in the region to drive installs, then
progressively loads additional malicious stages after initial
installation. The specific spyware capabilities and threat actor behind
the campaign were not detailed in the available reporting.
