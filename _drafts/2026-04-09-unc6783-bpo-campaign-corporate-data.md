Google has identified a threat actor tracked as UNC6783 conducting a campaign against
business process outsourcers (BPOs) to exfiltrate corporate client data. The actor is
believed to be linked to "Mr. Raccoon," previously connected to alleged theft of Adobe
data obtained through a BPO.

BPOs are high-value targets because a single compromise can expose data belonging to
dozens of enterprise clients simultaneously. Organizations that outsource operations to
third-party BPOs should review vendor access controls, data classification policies,
and contractual obligations around breach notification.
