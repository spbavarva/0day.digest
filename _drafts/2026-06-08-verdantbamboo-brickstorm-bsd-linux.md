A China-nexus espionage group tracked as VerdantBamboo has been observed deploying a BSD variant of the BRICKSTORM backdoor, alongside two other malware families dubbed PLENET (aka GRIMBOLT) and AGENTPSD, to target Linux appliances. Volexity attributes the activity to a cluster overlapping with groups known elsewhere as Clay Typhoon. The BSD port suggests the group is broadening its targeting beyond standard Linux distributions to BSD-based network and security appliances.
