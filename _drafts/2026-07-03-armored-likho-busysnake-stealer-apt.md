Kaspersky's GReAT team detailed an active campaign by a previously
undocumented threat actor, Armored Likho, targeting government agencies
and the electric power sector in Russia, Brazil, and Kazakhstan. The
group uses spear-phishing, AI-generated loaders, and a new Python-based
stealer dubbed BusySnake to compromise targets, blending financially
motivated activity against individuals with targeted espionage against
organizations. Government and energy-sector defenders in the affected
regions should watch for spear-phishing lures and BusySnake indicators.
