Cisco Talos disclosed eight vulnerabilities in TP-Link routers, one in Adobe Photoshop, one in OpenVPN, and one in Gen Digital's Norton VPN.
All vulnerabilities have been patched by their respective vendors in adherence to Cisco's coordinated disclosure process.
Users and administrators should update TP-Link router firmware, Adobe Photoshop, OpenVPN client software, and Norton VPN to the latest available versions.
