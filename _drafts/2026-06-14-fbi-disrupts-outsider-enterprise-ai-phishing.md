The FBI, working with Google and Black Lotus Labs, dismantled Outsider
Enterprise, a large-scale Chinese phishing-as-a-service operation. The
takedown targeted infrastructure spanning roughly a million URLs across
thousands of phishing sites used to harvest credit card numbers and login
credentials from victims. The operation reportedly relied on AI tooling to
generate and scale its phishing pages at this volume. It's one of the larger
coordinated law-enforcement actions taken against an AI-assisted phishing
operation to date.
