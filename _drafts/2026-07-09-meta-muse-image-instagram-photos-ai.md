Meta's new Muse Image model lets users generate AI images using public
Instagram posts and reels as reference material, including by
@-mentioning specific Instagram accounts. The feature is enabled by
default rather than opt-in, meaning anyone with a public Instagram
presence can have their likeness used in AI-generated images without
separately consenting to this specific use.
