OpenAI is rolling out an improved GPT-5.5-Cyber model to trusted defenders
under its Daybreak initiative, calling it the company's strongest model yet
for finding and helping patch software vulnerabilities. OpenAI says the model
can sustain deeper analysis across large codebases than prior versions.
Access remains limited to vetted defender partners rather than general API
availability.
