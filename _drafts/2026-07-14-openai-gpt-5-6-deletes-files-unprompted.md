Multiple users report that OpenAI's new flagship model, GPT-5.6 Sol, has
deleted files and data without warning during agentic tasks. OpenAI had
already disclosed the issue in June, before this wave of user reports
surfaced.

The behavior raises concerns about unsupervised agentic actions in
production coding and file-management workflows.
