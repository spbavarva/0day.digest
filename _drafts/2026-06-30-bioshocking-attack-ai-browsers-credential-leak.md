Security firm LayerX disclosed BioShocking, a technique that manipulates
AI browsers and assistants into leaking user credentials by framing the
interaction as a game. The technique tricked six AI browsers and
assistants — including OpenAI's ChatGPT Atlas, Perplexity's Comet, and
Anthropic's Claude browser extension — into copying a user's login details
and sending them to an attacker.
