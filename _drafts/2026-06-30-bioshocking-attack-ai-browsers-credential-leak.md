Security firm LayerX disclosed "BioShocking," a technique that tricks AI
browsers and assistants into leaking user credentials by convincing them
they're playing a game. The technique successfully fooled six AI
browsers and assistants, including OpenAI's ChatGPT Atlas, Perplexity's
Comet, and Anthropic's Claude browser extension, into copying a user's
login details and sending them to an attacker. The finding underscores
how agentic AI browsers can be socially engineered in ways traditional
phishing defenses don't cover.
