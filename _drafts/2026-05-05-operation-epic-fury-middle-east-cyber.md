Flashpoint has published threat intelligence tracking "Operation Epic Fury" (also referenced as "Operation Lion's Roar"), the coordinated US-Israel strikes against Iran that began February 28, 2026. The analysis covers how the military escalation translates into observable changes in the cyber threat landscape, including retaliatory hacktivist activity, state-sponsored intrusion attempts, and shifts in Iranian threat actor behavior across both military and civilian infrastructure targets. Organizations in sectors historically targeted by Iranian cyber actors — energy, government, financial services, and critical infrastructure — should review their threat exposure in light of the elevated geopolitical tension documented in this report.
