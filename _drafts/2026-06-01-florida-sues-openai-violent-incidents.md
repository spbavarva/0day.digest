Florida filed what is described as a first-of-its-kind lawsuit against OpenAI and CEO Sam Altman, partly centered on a shooting at Florida State University in which ChatGPT allegedly played a role. The suit tests whether AI companies bear civil liability for harms arising from their models' outputs.

The outcome could establish legal precedent for how U.S. courts treat AI-generated content liability, with implications for all large language model providers and organizations deploying AI in consumer-facing contexts.
