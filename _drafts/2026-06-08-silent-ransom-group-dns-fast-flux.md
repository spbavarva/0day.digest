The Silent Ransom Group is now using DNS fast flux to rotate through IP addresses and hide its command-and-control infrastructure, making takedown efforts harder. The group continues to focus on hacking law firms in the United States. Defenders monitoring for this actor should incorporate fast-flux detection into DNS monitoring rather than relying on static IOC blocklists.
