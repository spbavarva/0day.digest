CISA has ordered federal agencies to prioritize patching two actively
exploited vulnerabilities in Fortinet's FortiSandbox threat detection
platform, with a deadline of this Sunday. The flaws are confirmed under
active exploitation, though the advisory summary does not specify CVE
identifiers or the attack technique involved. Organizations running
FortiSandbox should apply the vendor's patches immediately rather than
waiting on the federal deadline.
