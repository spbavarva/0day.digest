The U.S. Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) designated two
individuals and VPN provider First VPN Service (1VPNS) for enabling
ransomware actors and other cybercriminals, including attacks against
Americans. The designation blocks U.S. persons and entities from
transacting with the sanctioned parties and freezes any U.S.-based assets.
This marks the first time OFAC has sanctioned a VPN service specifically
for facilitating ransomware operations.
