HUMAN's Satori Threat Intelligence team disclosed Trapdoor, an Android-targeting ad fraud and malvertising operation involving 455 malicious apps and 183 attacker-controlled C2 domains.
The infrastructure generated up to 659 million fraudulent daily bid requests, making it a large-scale multi-stage fraud pipeline.
The malicious apps were distributed to Android device users and leveraged C2 infrastructure for coordinated fraud activity.
Users should avoid sideloading Android apps from unofficial sources; enterprises with mobile device management should audit installed app inventories against the disclosed indicator list.
