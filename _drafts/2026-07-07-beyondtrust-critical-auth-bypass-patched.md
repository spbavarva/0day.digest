BeyondTrust released patches for two critical vulnerabilities in its Remote
Support (RS) and Privileged Remote Access (PRA) products, including
CVE-2026-40138 (CVSS 9.2), a pre-authentication flaw. If exploited, an
unauthenticated attacker could take control of an affected device. No
active exploitation was reported at time of disclosure. Customers running
BeyondTrust RS/PRA should apply the patches immediately given the
unauthenticated attack path.
