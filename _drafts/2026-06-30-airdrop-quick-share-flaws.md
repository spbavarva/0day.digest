Two researchers found six security flaws in AirDrop and Quick Share, the
wireless features that beam files between nearby devices with no cables
or shared network. An attacker within wireless range, using just a laptop
and no prior connection, can crash the sharing service on a Mac or iPhone
set to receive from anyone, with no tap or prompt required. Related flaws
were also found in Quick Share.
