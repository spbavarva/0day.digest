Tencent's Hy Team released Hy3, an Apache 2.0-licensed mixture-of-experts
model with 295B total parameters and 21B active parameters, following up on
the Hy3 Preview launched in April. Tencent says post-training was refined
using feedback gathered from 50+ products and that the model rivals larger
flagship open-source models on benchmarks. The full-sized model weighs in
at 598GB. No security-specific claims were made in the release.
