OpenAI rolled out a capability update to GPT-5.5 Instant, its mid-tier model in the GPT-5 generation, delivered server-side with no version or API changes required for existing integrations. Alongside the update, OpenAI confirmed scheduled deprecations for several legacy models including o3 as the company consolidates around the GPT-5 model family. Full deprecation timelines have not been published in detail. Developers relying on o3 or other legacy endpoints should consult OpenAI's deprecation schedule and begin migration planning to GPT-5.5 or GPT-5 equivalents before the retirement dates take effect.
