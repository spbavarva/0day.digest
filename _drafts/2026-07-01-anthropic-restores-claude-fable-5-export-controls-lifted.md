The U.S. Commerce Department has lifted the export controls it imposed on
Anthropic's Claude Fable 5 and the more tightly controlled Mythos 5 roughly
two and a half weeks earlier, controls that had been linked to jailbreak
concerns. Anthropic says Fable 5 will return to users worldwide on
Wednesday, July 1, across Claude.ai, the Claude Platform, Claude Code, and
Claude Cowork, with access on AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft platforms to
follow. The reversal signals the earlier restriction has been resolved
rather than superseded by new safeguards.
