Healthcare testing and laboratory services provider Centers Laboratory
disclosed a data breach affecting 540,000 individuals. The WorldLeaks
extortion group claimed responsibility, stating it stole 720 GB of data
from the organization. The scale of the breach and sensitivity of
healthcare testing data make this a significant incident for affected
individuals and any organizations that relied on Centers Laboratory's
services.
