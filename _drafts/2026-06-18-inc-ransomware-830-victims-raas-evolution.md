Researchers have charted the rise of INC, a ransomware-as-a-service
operation that has claimed more than 830 victims since launching in
August 2023. According to Acronis, the disruption of LockBit and the
shutdown of BlackCat created an opening that INC capitalized on as
affiliates migrated to alternative RaaS platforms. The group's growth
shows how law enforcement takedowns of major ransomware brands can
redistribute, rather than eliminate, criminal capacity.
