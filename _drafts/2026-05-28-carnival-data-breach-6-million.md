Carnival Corporation confirmed a breach in which a threat actor compromised an
employee account last month, gaining access to a limited portion of its IT
environment. By the end of April, Carnival determined the attacker had copied
personal information belonging to nearly 6 million people. The employee account
vector is consistent with phishing or credential stuffing; no ransomware or
operational disruption was mentioned. Affected customers face elevated identity
theft risk and should monitor for fraudulent activity. Carnival is the world's
largest cruise company, making this a high-profile consumer data exposure.
