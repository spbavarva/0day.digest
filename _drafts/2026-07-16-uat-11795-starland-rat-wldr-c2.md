Cisco Talos disclosed UAT-11795, a Russian-speaking, financially motivated
threat actor that has been running a campaign against targets in the U.S.
and Europe since at least June 2025. The group deploys a novel Starland RAT
alongside a bespoke C2 implant dubbed WLDR, and has been observed
trojanizing legitimate software such as WebEx and Zoom installers to steal
credentials and cryptocurrency. The use of custom, purpose-built tooling
rather than off-the-shelf malware suggests a moderately sophisticated,
well-resourced operation.
