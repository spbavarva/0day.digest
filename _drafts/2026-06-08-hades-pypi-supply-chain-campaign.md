A campaign dubbed "Hades" has hit 37 PyPI wheels and 19 code packages in what researchers describe as a new evolution of the Shai-Hulud supply chain worm. The activity shows the persistent threat continuing to adapt its distribution technique against the Python package ecosystem. Teams pulling dependencies from PyPI should review recently added packages and pin known-good versions while indicators are confirmed.
