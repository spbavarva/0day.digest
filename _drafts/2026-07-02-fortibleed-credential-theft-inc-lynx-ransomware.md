The FortiBleed campaign, which harvested credentials from hundreds of
thousands of FortiGate firewalls, has been attributed to the INC and Lynx
ransomware operations. Researchers found an operator tied to FortiBleed's
infrastructure actively working negotiation panels for both groups, linking
mass FortiGate credential theft directly to follow-on ransomware
deployment. Given the scale of harvested credentials, organizations running
affected FortiGate appliances should assume compromise and rotate
credentials rather than wait for a breach notification.
