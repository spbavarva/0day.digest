The University of Nottingham confirmed a breach of its student records system after the ShinyHunters group claimed credit and leaked data.
The leak reportedly includes more than 450,000 email addresses and other personal information belonging to current students and alumni, including those at the university's Malaysia and China campuses.
The university says it is still working to determine the full scope of accessed data and has directly contacted affected students and alumni.
Affected individuals should watch for phishing attempts referencing university account details.
