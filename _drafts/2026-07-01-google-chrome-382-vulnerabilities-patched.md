Google patched 382 vulnerabilities in Chrome, including 15 rated critical
and 67 rated high severity. This is an unusually large batch even for a
routine Chrome update cycle. No active exploitation was reported for any of
the fixed issues. Given Chrome's install base, organizations should push
the update through standard patch management rather than waiting for the
next cycle.
