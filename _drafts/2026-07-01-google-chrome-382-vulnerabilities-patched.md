Google shipped patches for 382 vulnerabilities across Chrome, including 15
rated critical and 67 rated high severity. No detail was provided on
whether any of the flaws are under active exploitation. Given the scale of
the patch batch, users and enterprises managing Chrome fleets should
prioritize rollout of the update.
