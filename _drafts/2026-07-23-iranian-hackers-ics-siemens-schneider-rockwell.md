Federal agencies issued an updated advisory on Iranian state-linked
hacking activity targeting industrial control system devices, including
programmable logic controllers from Siemens, Schneider Electric, and
Rockwell Automation. The advisory outlines techniques used to compromise
PLCs at critical infrastructure operators, but does not name specific
victims or confirmed successful intrusions. Operators running affected PLC
models should review network segmentation and remote access controls
against the advisory's guidance.
