OpenAI announced it is joining the open C2PA (Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity) standard and adding Google's SynthID invisible watermarking technology to its products to help identify AI-generated imagery.
Simultaneously, Google announced it is expanding SynthID and C2PA content credential detection to Chrome and Google Search, making both systems accessible to regular users during normal browsing.
The coordinated announcements from the two largest AI image generators represent a meaningful step toward interoperable provenance metadata across platforms.
Organizations relying on image authenticity verification for trust and safety purposes should evaluate tools supporting both the C2PA and SynthID standards.
