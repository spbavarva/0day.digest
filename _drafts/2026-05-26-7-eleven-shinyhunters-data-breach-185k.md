The ShinyHunters extortion gang breached 7-Eleven's systems in April 2026, exfiltrating personal information belonging to over 183,000 individuals. Exposed data includes email addresses, names, physical addresses, and dates of birth, confirmed via Have I Been Pwned.

ShinyHunters has a consistent track record of high-profile retail and loyalty program breaches. Affected individuals should anticipate phishing attempts leveraging the leaked PII and update passwords on any 7-Eleven-linked accounts. The incident continues the group's pattern of targeting retail customer databases for extortion.
