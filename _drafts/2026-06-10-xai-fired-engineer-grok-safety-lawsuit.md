A former xAI engineer has sued xAI and SpaceX, alleging he was fired for raising safety concerns about Grok days before SpaceX's IPO.
The suit claims the dismissal was retaliation for flagging internal safety issues rather than for performance reasons.
The allegations haven't been independently verified, and xAI hasn't publicly responded in detail.
If substantiated, the case would add to a pattern of whistleblower disputes at frontier AI labs over how safety concerns are handled internally.
