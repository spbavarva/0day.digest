Rival ransomware groups 0APT and KryBit attacked each other's operations, leaking infrastructure details and operational data that provide rare visibility into how these groups organize and target victims. The inadvertent disclosures include internal tooling and infrastructure identifiers.

Threat intelligence teams should review the leaked data for extractable IOCs, command-and-control infrastructure patterns, and targeting criteria. This inter-gang conflict reflects continued fragmentation in the ransomware ecosystem following recent law enforcement disruptions, and may indicate deteriorating trust within criminal affiliate networks.
