JetBrains released Mellum2, a 12-billion-parameter Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) model focused on code completion and developer tooling, published as open weights on Hugging Face. MoE architectures activate only a subset of parameters per token, enabling larger effective model capacity at lower inference cost compared to dense models of equivalent parameter count.

Mellum2 is designed for integration into JetBrains IDEs and is available for self-hosted deployment.
