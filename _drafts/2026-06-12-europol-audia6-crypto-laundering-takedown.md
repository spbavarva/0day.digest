Europol announced the disruption of AudiA6, a cryptocurrency laundering
service used by ransomware gangs and other cybercriminal networks to wash
illicit proceeds.

The service is estimated to have laundered more than €336 million (about $389
million) since it began operating.

Europol described the takedown as cutting off "a key financial pipeline used
to wash hundreds of millions in illicit profits." No further technical
details about the service's infrastructure were provided in initial
reporting.
