Attackers discovered that Meta's AI support chatbot could be manipulated into resetting Instagram account passwords by asking it to change the email address associated with an arbitrary account. A tutorial demonstrating the technique circulated on Telegram and was used to briefly deface high-profile accounts — including the Instagram pages of the Obama White House and the Chief Master Sergeant of the U.S. Space Force — with pro-Iranian imagery.

The attack exploited a trust boundary failure: the AI assistant executed account modifications without proper authorization verification. Meta says the issue has been patched. The incident is a concrete example of AI-powered support systems being abused to bypass account security controls without direct credential access.
