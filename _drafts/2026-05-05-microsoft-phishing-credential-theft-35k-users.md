Between April 14–16, 2026, a multi-stage credential theft campaign targeted over 35,000 users at 13,000+ organizations across 26 countries. Attackers used code-of-conduct-themed lures delivered through legitimate email services to redirect victims to attacker-controlled domains and steal authentication tokens.

Routing phishing through legitimate sending infrastructure undermines reputation-based email filtering. Organizations should audit conditional access policies for anomalous token replay patterns and verify phishing-resistant MFA (FIDO2/passkeys) coverage across high-value accounts.
