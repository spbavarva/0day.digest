Kyrgyzstan-based cryptocurrency exchange Grinex has suspended all operations following a $13.7
million hack. The exchange is publicly attributing the theft to "Western intelligence agencies,"
a claim that has not been independently verified and for which no technical evidence has been
provided.

The shutdown leaves affected customers unable to access their funds while the investigation
continues. The lack of a disclosed attack vector or technical details makes independent assessment
of the breach difficult. Users should monitor official Grinex communications for recovery timelines
or legal recourse information.
