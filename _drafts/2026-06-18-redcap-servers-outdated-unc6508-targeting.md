SecurityWeek reports that a majority of internet-accessible REDCap
servers — widely used in clinical and academic research — are running
outdated versions. The China-linked threat actor UNC6508 has been
regularly targeting these servers for initial access and backdoor
deployment. Organizations running REDCap for research data collection
should confirm they're on a current, patched version and audit their
internet exposure.
