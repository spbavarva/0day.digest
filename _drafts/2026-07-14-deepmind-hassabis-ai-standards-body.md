Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis is calling for an independent body to test and regulate frontier AI models, drawing a comparison to FINRA's oversight role in financial markets. In a blog post, Hassabis argues the US should lead the effort, citing its position to set global standards for how frontier models are tested and released. The proposal comes amid growing calls from AI lab leaders for external safety oversight as frontier models operate with more autonomy and less direct human review. No concrete body, funding structure, or timeline has been announced.
