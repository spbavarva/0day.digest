BWH Hotels disclosed that threat actors maintained access to its reservation data systems
for approximately six months before discovery. Stolen data includes guest names and contact
information for an undisclosed number of affected guests. The extended dwell time indicates
the intrusion was not detected by standard monitoring controls for the duration. BWH Hotels
operates thousands of properties globally under brands including Best Western. Guests who
made reservations during the exposure window should remain alert to spear-phishing attempts
that reference their travel details.
