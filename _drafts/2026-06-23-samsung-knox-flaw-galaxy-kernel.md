Researchers disclosed an eight-year-old, high-severity use-after-free
vulnerability in Samsung's KNOX security framework. The flaw affected
Android-powered Galaxy devices spanning the S9 through the S25, exposing
millions of devices to kernel-level attacks. Galaxy device owners and fleet
administrators should apply Samsung's security patches as they become
available.
