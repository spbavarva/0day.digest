FBI and CISA issued a joint advisory warning that Iran-linked threat actors are
actively targeting U.S. water and energy sector organizations by exploiting
vulnerabilities in industrial programmable logic controllers (PLCs). The attacks
have caused operational disruption and financial losses.

Critical infrastructure operators in water and energy should review the advisory
for specific indicators of compromise and hardening guidance. Internet-accessible
PLCs and OT/ICS systems should be audited immediately and segmented from
corporate networks where possible.
