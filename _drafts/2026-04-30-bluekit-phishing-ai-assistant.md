A new phishing-as-a-service platform called Bluekit has emerged with over 40 templates targeting popular consumer and enterprise services, plus a built-in AI assistant for generating campaign lure content.

The AI component is the operationally notable feature: it removes the copywriting barrier for less-skilled operators, enabling convincing phishing drafts without manual effort. This continues the trend of AI integration into commodity attack tooling, lowering the entry bar across the threat landscape.

Security teams should update email filtering rules to account for AI-generated lure content that may not match known phishing signatures, and refresh user awareness training to address the improved plausibility of AI-crafted phishing messages.
