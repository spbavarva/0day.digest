The ShinyHunters cybercrime group breached Instructure's Canvas LMS and defaced login portals for
hundreds of colleges and universities across the United States. The group is threatening to leak
data belonging to 275 million students and faculty across approximately 9,000 institutions unless a
ransom is paid. The attack disrupted classes and coursework at affected schools today. Canvas is one
of the most widely deployed learning management systems in US higher education. Instructure has not
yet confirmed the breach vector or the claimed data volume. Institutions should monitor for
credential abuse, notify affected users, and watch for official guidance from Instructure.
