Discord has enabled end-to-end encryption by default for all voice and video calls on the platform,
protecting communications from server-side interception without requiring any user action. The rollout
is a meaningful security upgrade for a platform with significant security community, developer, and
enterprise presence.

E2EE applies automatically to all calls. Discord's move raises the baseline for communications
security across the platform and removes a historical gap that had been noted by privacy-conscious
users.
