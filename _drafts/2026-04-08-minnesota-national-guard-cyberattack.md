Minnesota Governor Tim Walz issued an executive order deploying National Guard cyber assets to Winona County after a cyberattack that began Monday disrupted critical county systems. The governor-level response indicates significant operational impact beyond a typical IT outage.

Local government attacks have escalated in frequency and severity. Winona County joins a growing list of municipalities requiring state-level intervention after cyberattacks. County and municipal IT teams should review backup integrity and incident response plans; the National Guard deployment model is increasingly becoming a standard state response posture.
