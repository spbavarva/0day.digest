New vulnerabilities have been identified in a Microsoft BitLocker
security wrapper used in ATM crypto software, putting both the affected
organizations and potentially the ATMs themselves at risk of compromise.
Full technical details have not yet been published. Organizations
operating ATMs or other systems that rely on this BitLocker wrapper
should watch for vendor advisories and apply patches once available.
