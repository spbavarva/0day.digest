Poland's security agency confirmed hackers breached industrial control systems at five water treatment
facilities, gaining the ability to modify operational parameters and creating a direct risk to the
public water supply. No service disruption was reported, but the unauthorized access to OT systems
represents a serious threat to critical infrastructure. Attribution has not been disclosed. ICS
operators in the water sector should urgently audit remote access controls and network segmentation
between IT and OT environments.
