Foxconn, the world's largest electronics manufacturer, confirmed a cyberattack
on its North American factory operations. The Nitrogen ransomware group claims
responsibility and alleges 8TB of data was exfiltrated, including confidential
documents. Affected factories are working to restore normal operations.
Nitrogen is a ransomware-as-a-service group with a history of targeting
manufacturing and logistics firms. No supply chain impact on downstream
hardware production has been publicly confirmed. Organizations in Foxconn's
supplier and partner ecosystem should monitor for secondary exposure from any
leaked vendor or contract data.
