The ShinyHunters extortion group claims to have hacked the Council of Europe and is threatening to leak roughly 297GB of data, including employee personal information.
The Council of Europe has not confirmed the breach or the scope of any data exposure.
The claim follows a string of recent ShinyHunters operations against other large organizations.
No technical details about the initial access vector have been disclosed.
