OpenAI has announced that GPT-5.5 Cyber—its cybersecurity-focused model—will roll out initially only to "critical cyber defenders," applying access restrictions nearly identical to those OpenAI had publicly criticized Anthropic for imposing on Claude Mythos.

The reversal is notable given OpenAI's prior public statements. It underscores the dual-use dilemma facing every AI lab shipping frontier models with verified offensive cyber capability: broad availability enables defenders but equally enables attackers.

Security teams that applied to early access programs should verify their status. Broader availability timelines were not announced. The policy alignment between Anthropic and OpenAI suggests informal coordination—or regulatory pressure—is beginning to shape access controls across the industry.
