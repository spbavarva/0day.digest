Basic-Fit, Europe's largest gym chain with locations across Belgium, France, the Netherlands,
Spain, and Luxembourg, has disclosed a data breach affecting approximately 1 million members.
Stolen data includes names, dates of birth, and bank account details.

The inclusion of bank account numbers significantly raises the risk of follow-on fraud and
targeted phishing campaigns against affected members, as bank account data combined with
real names and birthdates provides enough detail for account takeover attempts.

Affected members should monitor bank accounts for unauthorized transactions, alert their bank
to the potential compromise, and be alert to phishing messages claiming to be from Basic-Fit
or their bank. Consider requesting a new account number if your bank supports it without
disruption to existing payment arrangements.
