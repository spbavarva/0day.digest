Google DeepMind announced Gemini 3.5 Flash Cyber, a specialized model built
on 3.5 Flash designed to discover, validate, and patch vulnerabilities
quickly and efficiently. Per The Hacker News, the model will be available
exclusively to governments and trusted partners via CodeMender as part of a
limited-access pilot. Separately, Google Cloud says CodeMender — its
managed code security agent — is now in preview, offering code scanning and
remediation through the Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform or as a component
of AI Threat Defense.
