Microsoft confirmed an ongoing service outage preventing customers from enrolling new MFA devices or accessing the My Sign-Ins platform. No breach or unauthorized access was reported — this is a service availability incident. Organizations in the middle of MFA rollouts or onboarding new users will experience delays until the incident is resolved. Administrators should monitor the Microsoft 365 Service Health Dashboard for status updates and defer non-urgent MFA provisioning activities while the incident is active.
