Following speculation that Kimi K3's rapid capability jump came from
unauthorized distillation off Anthropic's Fable model, experts told
TechCrunch that the timeline and technique don't support that theory. One
expert said a model this strong, arriving this quickly after Fable's
release, is unlikely to be the product of straightforward distillation
alone. The piece doesn't identify a confirmed alternative explanation for
Kimi K3's performance, and no evidence of an actual extraction attack
against Anthropic's API was presented.
