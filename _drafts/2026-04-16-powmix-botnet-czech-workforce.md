Cisco Talos has disclosed "PowMix," a previously undocumented botnet that has been running
an ongoing malicious campaign against a broad workforce population in the Czech Republic
since at least December 2025. The botnet and its associated malware families were previously
unknown to public threat intelligence.

The targeting of a broad national workforce rather than specific high-value organizations
suggests the campaign may be opportunistic in scope, potentially used for credential
harvesting, proxy network building, or staging for more targeted intrusions. The multi-month
operational window indicates the operators have evaded detection at scale.

Full technical indicators, malware behavior, and detection guidance are available in the
Cisco Talos research post linked above.
