Varonis researchers found a flaw in Google's Dialogflow CX, dubbed "Rogue
Agent," that could have allowed data theft from AI chatbots built on the
platform. The flaw was reported to Google in late 2025 and has since been
addressed, with no evidence of in-the-wild exploitation. The disclosure is a
reminder for teams building on AI agent platforms to take a fresh look at
data access boundaries in their AI infrastructure.
