Eight major industrial control system vendors simultaneously released security advisories in
April's ICS Patch Tuesday: Siemens, Schneider Electric, Aveva, Rockwell Automation, ABB,
Phoenix Contact, Mitsubishi Electric, and Moxa.

The feed summary does not detail individual CVEs or severity ratings for each vendor's advisories,
but the breadth of vendors releasing on the same cycle underscores coordinated disclosure.
ICS/OT vulnerabilities carry elevated risk because patching windows are narrow and exploitation
can have physical consequences in manufacturing, utilities, and critical infrastructure.

OT security teams should pull each vendor's PSIRT advisory directly, prioritize internet-exposed
or DMZ-connected assets, and coordinate patch scheduling with operations to minimize downtime.
