OpenAI confirmed it is restricting release of a new AI model at the request
of the Trump administration, limiting access to government-approved
customers during an ongoing cybersecurity review. Anthropic is reportedly
subject to similar restrictions. The move ties frontier model access to
government vetting of customers, a notable shift in how AI deployment is
gated. Scope and duration of the review were not detailed in the source
summary.
