OpenAI and Dell have announced a partnership to bring Codex, OpenAI's AI coding agent, to hybrid and on-premise enterprise environments. The arrangement allows enterprises to run AI coding workflows on their own infrastructure, addressing data residency and compliance requirements that prevent sending proprietary source code to cloud-only AI services. The partnership lowers the barrier to AI-assisted development for security-conscious enterprises operating under strict data handling policies.
