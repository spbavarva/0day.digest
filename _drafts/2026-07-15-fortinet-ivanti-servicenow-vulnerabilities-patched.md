Fortinet, Ivanti, and ServiceNow patched multiple vulnerabilities this week.
The standout is a critical security defect in the ServiceNow AI platform that
could let remote attackers execute arbitrary code. No in-the-wild
exploitation has been reported; customers should apply the vendor patches.
