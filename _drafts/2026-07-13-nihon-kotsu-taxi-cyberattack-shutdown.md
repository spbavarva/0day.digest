Nihon Kotsu, Japan's largest taxi operator, shut down parts of its IT
infrastructure after detecting a cyberattack against its systems. The
company has not disclosed the attack vector, whether data was exposed, or
the full scope of the disruption. Further details were not available in
initial reporting.
