Splunk and Palo Alto Networks both shipped patches for severe vulnerabilities in their products this week.
The flaws could allow attackers to create or modify arbitrary files and to access or modify protected resources.
Both vendors' products are widely deployed in enterprise security operations, making prompt patching a priority.
No active exploitation has been reported yet; administrators should review the vendor advisories and apply the available updates.
