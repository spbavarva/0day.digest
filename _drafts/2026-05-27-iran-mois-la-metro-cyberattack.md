Gambit Security researchers have attributed the cyberattack on Los Angeles Metro
transit infrastructure to a group with direct ties to Iran's Ministry of
Intelligence (MOIS). The group had previously presented itself as a standalone
hacktivist crew.

The research identifies infrastructure overlaps linking the threat actor to
confirmed MOIS operations, revealing state-sponsored activity disguised under a
hacktivist cover identity. The attack on a major US transit system continues a
pattern of Iranian state actors targeting critical infrastructure under
deniable personas.
