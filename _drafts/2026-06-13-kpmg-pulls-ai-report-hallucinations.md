KPMG withdrew a recently published report on AI usage after it was found to contain apparent AI-generated hallucinations.
The retraction is another example of generative AI tools introducing fabricated claims or citations into professional research without adequate review.
Specific details on which parts of the report were inaccurate, or how the issue was identified, have not been disclosed.
