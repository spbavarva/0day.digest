Škoda Auto, a Volkswagen Group subsidiary, disclosed that attackers compromised its online
shop and exfiltrated personal information belonging to an undisclosed number of customers.
The breach affects data collected through the online vehicle ordering and configuration
platform. Škoda has not released the number of affected customers or the specific data
categories stolen beyond "personal information." Customers who interacted with the Škoda
online shop should be alert to targeted phishing or social engineering attacks that
leverage their vehicle purchase details.
