TikTok has begun testing an opt-in tool that scans for AI-generated
likenesses of creators and lets them report unauthorized deepfakes to the
company. The tool is currently being tested with a limited group of US
creators. It mirrors a similar likeness-detection effort YouTube has been
building. No technical details on the detection method have been published.
