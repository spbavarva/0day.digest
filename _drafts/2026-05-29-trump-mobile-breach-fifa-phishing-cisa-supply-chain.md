Three stories in brief from SecurityWeek's weekly roundup:

**Trump Mobile data breach:** Customer data from Trump Mobile was exposed in a breach; technical scope and record count were not disclosed in available reporting.

**FIFA World Cup 2026 phishing:** Threat actors are running phishing campaigns themed around the 2026 FIFA World Cup, targeting fans and presumably harvesting credentials and payment data ahead of the tournament.

**CISA supply chain response:** CISA issued a statement responding to recent supply chain attacks, though specific new guidance or IOCs were not detailed in the summary. Security teams should monitor the CISA website for any follow-on advisories.
