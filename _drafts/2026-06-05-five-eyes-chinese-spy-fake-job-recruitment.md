A joint Five Eyes advisory warns that Chinese intelligence officers are posing as
recruiters on professional networking platforms to target government and military
personnel with access to classified or privileged information. The operation solicits
sensitive disclosures under the cover of fake job opportunities and aims to establish
long-term collection relationships. Security teams supporting cleared or government
personnel should update awareness training to flag unsolicited recruitment contacts
from unverifiable profiles and remind staff not to discuss work scope or clearance level
with unknown contacts online.
