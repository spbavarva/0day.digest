OpenClaw removed five packages from ClawHub, its AI skills marketplace,
after discovering they bypassed marketplace security checks despite
containing infostealers and other malicious code. The incident underscores
the growing risk in AI agent "skill" ecosystems, where third-party
extensions can carry the same supply-chain risks as npm or PyPI packages
but with less mature vetting in place.
