Threat actors are selling investment-scam site templates built on the
legitimate DCloud Uni-App toolkit, a framework that researchers say now
powers roughly 200,000 scam sites. Abusing a legitimate, widely-used
development toolkit lets operators stand up convincing scam infrastructure
at scale while blending in with normal app traffic. Defenders monitoring for
investment-scam campaigns should be aware Uni-App-based sites are a known
pattern, not an isolated one-off.
