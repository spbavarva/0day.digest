Kaspersky's GReAT team identified a new global campaign, dubbed
StrikeShark, that delivers Cobalt Strike Beacon through a custom loader
called SharkLoader. The campaign represents continued reliance on Cobalt
Strike as a post-exploitation framework, fronted by purpose-built loaders
designed to evade detection during initial delivery.
