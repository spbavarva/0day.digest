Multiple Dashlane users were locked out of their accounts after brute force login attempts from distant locations and unknown devices were detected. Whether any accounts were successfully accessed has not been confirmed. Dashlane stores encrypted password vaults; a successful account compromise would expose credentials for all sites stored in the vault.

Affected users should rotate their Dashlane master password and verify that multi-factor authentication is enabled on their account. Users who received lockout notifications should contact Dashlane support to confirm whether their account was targeted.
