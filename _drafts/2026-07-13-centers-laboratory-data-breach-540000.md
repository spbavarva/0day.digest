The WorldLeaks extortion group claims to have stolen 720 GB of data from Centers Laboratory, a healthcare testing and laboratory services provider, affecting roughly 540,000 individuals. Healthcare data breaches of this scale typically involve patient records and test results, making them attractive for follow-on fraud and extortion.
