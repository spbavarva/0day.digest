Flashpoint intelligence tracks Rehub, a ransomware-as-a-service marketplace,
detailing its migration between infrastructure and the various RaaS groups
that sponsor and partner with it. The research frames Rehub as an
increasingly central hub in the ransomware ecosystem rather than a single
threat actor, illustrating how affiliate marketplaces are consolidating
around shared infrastructure and sponsorship arrangements.
