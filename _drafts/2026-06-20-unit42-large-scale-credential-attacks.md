Palo Alto's Unit 42 released a threat brief on large-scale credential
attacks, focused on recent campaigns targeting security vendors' own
devices. The brief provides guidance for preparing for and mitigating
this class of attack. No specific CVE, vendor, or victim count was
disclosed in the available summary. Teams running security appliances
should review credential hygiene and watch for anomalous authentication
attempts against management interfaces.
