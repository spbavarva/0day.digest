BlackFile is a newly tracked financially motivated threat actor active since February 2026,
conducting data theft and extortion attacks against retail and hospitality organizations. The group
uses vishing (voice phishing) as a primary initial access technique — calling employees while
impersonating IT staff or trusted parties to obtain credentials or MFA codes.

The retail and hospitality focus mirrors tactics used by Scattered Spider and similar social
engineering-heavy groups. Organizations in these sectors should reinforce identity verification
procedures for helpdesk and IT calls, implement callback verification for credential reset requests,
and ensure MFA fatigue attack mitigations are in place.
