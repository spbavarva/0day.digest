Google Mandiant and the Google Threat Intelligence Group have disclosed details of a financially motivated data theft and extortion campaign attributed to a threat actor tracked as UNC3753. Active between January and May 2026, the campaign targeted dozens of organizations across the professional services, legal, and financial sectors in the United States. The group combined vishing (voice phishing) with physical intrusions to gain initial access before stealing data and extorting victims. The blend of social engineering and in-person tactics highlights gaps that purely technical controls can miss.
