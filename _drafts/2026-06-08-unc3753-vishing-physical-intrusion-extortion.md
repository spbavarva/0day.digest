Google Mandiant and GTIG have disclosed a financially motivated data theft extortion campaign, attributed to a threat actor tracked as UNC3753, that combined voice phishing (vishing) with physical intrusions. Between January and May 2026 the group targeted dozens of organizations across professional, legal, and financial services in the United States. The blend of social engineering and on-site access represents a notable escalation beyond purely remote intrusion techniques for extortion-driven actors.
