Kaspersky's GReAT team published an in-depth analysis of Umbrij, a new
tool used by the ToddyCat APT group to compromise corporate email
communications in Gmail. The attack targets OAuth authorization tokens,
allowing the threat actors to gain persistent access to victims' Google
services without needing the account password.
