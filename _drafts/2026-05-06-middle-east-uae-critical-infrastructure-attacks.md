Breach attempts targeting the United Arab Emirates tripled within weeks as the conflict with Iran escalates, with a significant portion targeting critical infrastructure sectors. The pattern reflects the expanding cyber dimension of the Middle East conflict, with adversaries shifting attacks toward energy, utilities, and transportation targets.

Organizations with operations or dependencies in the UAE region should elevate their threat posture, review network segmentation between IT and OT environments, and ensure incident response plans account for coordinated, conflict-driven attack campaigns rather than opportunistic intrusions.
