International law enforcement agencies cleaned nearly 15,000 malware-
infected WordPress sites and took down more than 100 servers linked to
the SocGholish operation and the Evil Corp cybercrime group. SocGholish
has long been used as a fake-browser-update lure to gain initial access
for follow-on ransomware and other payloads. The takedown is a
significant disruption to one of the more prevalent initial-access
malware families currently in circulation.
