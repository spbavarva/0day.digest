OpenAI introduced Daybreak, a new initiative and toolset including Codex Security and a new model, GPT-5.5-Cyber, aimed at helping organizations find, validate, and patch vulnerabilities at scale. The launch puts OpenAI's models directly into the vulnerability-discovery and patching workflow rather than positioning them as a general-purpose coding assistant. Daybreak also includes the Patch the Planet program, which pairs frontier models with open-source maintainers to triage and fix bugs.
