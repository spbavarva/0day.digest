The UK and EU jointly imposed sanctions on Russian individuals and
entities for cyberattacks and disinformation campaigns targeting the
region — the first time the two have taken such action together. The
sanctions freeze assets and restrict dealings with the designated parties.
No new technical indicators or IOCs were disclosed alongside the action.
