Dozens of cybersecurity experts have urged the White House to lift export control restrictions placed on Anthropic's Fable 5 and Mythos 5 models.
The restrictions led Anthropic to take both models offline for all users worldwide after the directive required blocking access for foreign nationals, including its own employees.
The group argues the ban limits defenders' ability to use the models to secure their own software and products.
The protest is the latest development in the ongoing dispute between Anthropic and the U.S. government over the export controls.
