Google's Chrome 150 update patches 27 vulnerabilities, including 13
use-after-free bugs. Two of the fixed flaws are rated critical severity and
were discovered by Google's own researchers. No indication of active
exploitation was included in the disclosure. Users should update to the
latest Chrome build to receive the fixes.
