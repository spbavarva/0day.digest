Mandiant assisted California Water Service in investigating a cyberattack
attributed to Iranian hacker group Handala, which had publicly claimed it
could disrupt the utility's water supply. The investigation found no
evidence that operational technology (OT) systems were breached or that
any disruption capability was achieved, despite the group's claims. The
incident is a reminder that nation-state-linked groups frequently overstate
OT access in public claims, and that rapid third-party investigation
remains the most reliable way to confirm or refute such claims.
