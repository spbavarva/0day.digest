Law enforcement dismantled "AudiA6," a cryptocurrency laundering service allegedly used by ransomware operators and other cybercriminals.
The service is estimated to have laundered more than $380 million in illicit funds.
The takedown disrupts a key piece of financial infrastructure that ransomware affiliates relied on to cash out extortion payments.
Further details on the operators and jurisdictions involved have not yet been disclosed.
