The Department of Homeland Security is investigating a cyberattack that
compromised the Homeland Security Information Network (HSIN), a sensitive
information-sharing platform used by federal, state, local, and
private-sector partners. DHS has confirmed the intrusion but has not
disclosed the attacker's identity, the entry vector, or what data was
accessed. Given HSIN's role distributing threat intelligence and
coordination material, the scope of exposure could extend beyond DHS
itself. No remediation timeline has been published.
