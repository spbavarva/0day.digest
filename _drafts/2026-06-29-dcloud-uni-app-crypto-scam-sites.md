Infoblox found that more than 236,000 websites are running investment-scam
templates built on DCloud Uni-App, a legitimate Chinese open-source,
cross-platform application framework. The templates power fake
cryptocurrency exchanges, multi-language pig-butchering operations, WhatsApp
phishing networks, fake gambling platforms, and brand-impersonation scams.

The scale shows how legitimate open-source tooling can be repurposed
wholesale for scam infrastructure, complicating takedown and attribution
efforts.
