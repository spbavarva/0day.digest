The UK's regulator announced it will require technology firms to proactively detect and remove deepfakes and non-consensual intimate imagery (NCII), citing the urgent need to better protect women and girls online.
The announcement represents a formal regulatory obligation rather than guidance, with compliance obligations for platforms operating in the UK.
Technology platforms will need to implement detection, reporting, and takedown mechanisms for AI-generated NCII under the new rules.
This follows a broader pattern of regulators moving from voluntary commitments to enforceable requirements for AI-generated harmful content.
