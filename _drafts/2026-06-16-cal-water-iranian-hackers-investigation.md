California Water Service (Cal Water), which serves approximately 2 million people across California, confirmed it is investigating claims by an Iranian hacking group alleging compromise of its systems. The company stated there is currently no indication of operational disruptions to water or wastewater systems. Iran-linked threat actors targeting critical infrastructure align with a documented pattern of ICS/OT reconnaissance activity against US water utilities. No technical details, indicators of compromise, or attribution specifics have been published at this time. The investigation is ongoing; if intrusion is confirmed, this would represent another in a series of nation-state incursions against US water sector targets.
