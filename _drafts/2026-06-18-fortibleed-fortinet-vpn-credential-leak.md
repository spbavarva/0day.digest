A newly discovered leak dubbed "FortiBleed" has exposed what appears to
be a large collection of Fortinet and FortiGate VPN credentials covering
73,932 firewall URLs at organizations worldwide. The scale of the
exposure means a significant number of VPN endpoints could be at risk of
unauthorized access if the leaked credentials are valid and unrotated.
Organizations using FortiGate VPNs should rotate credentials and review
authentication logs for anomalous access as a precaution, even without
confirmation their own devices are on the list.
