OpenAI announced a preview feature allowing ChatGPT users to connect bank and brokerage accounts via Plaid, giving the AI access to portfolio performance, spending data, subscriptions, and upcoming payments across 12,000 financial institutions including Schwab and Fidelity. The integration introduces new attack surface: OAuth token handling and storage by OpenAI, Plaid session security, and the risk of sensitive financial data appearing in AI system logs or training pipelines. Users should review OpenAI's data retention and privacy policies before connecting financial accounts; organizations with employees using ChatGPT should communicate guidance on acceptable use with financial credentials.
