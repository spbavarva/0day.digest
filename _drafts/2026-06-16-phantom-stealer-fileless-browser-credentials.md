A newly identified malware strain dubbed "Phantom Stealer" targets
browser-stored credentials while executing entirely in memory. Its infection
chain incorporates additional anti-analysis techniques designed to frustrate
detection and complicate incident response. No specific scale of infections
or distribution vector was detailed in available reporting. Defenders should
ensure endpoint tooling covers fileless/in-memory execution paths rather than
relying solely on file-based signatures.
