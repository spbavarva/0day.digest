A 10-year renewal of the Cybersecurity Information Sharing Act of 2015
(CISA 2015) passed as part of the House's fiscal 2027 defense
authorization bill. The law provides legal protections for companies
sharing cyber threat information with the government and each other, and
had been set to expire without this extension. The provision still needs
Senate reconciliation and signature before taking effect.
