Minnesota Governor Tim Walz issued an executive order deploying the National
Guard to Winona County after a cyberattack disrupted critical county systems
beginning Monday. The severity of the response—a governor-level executive order
and military deployment—indicates significant operational impact.

The specific nature of the attack has not been publicly confirmed. Local
governments remain frequent targets due to underfunded IT and security teams
managing legacy infrastructure.
