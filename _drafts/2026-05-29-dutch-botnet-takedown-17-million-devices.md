Dutch law enforcement took offline a botnet comprising 17 million infected devices and seized more than 200 servers from a local hosting provider supporting the operation. The scale places this among the largest single botnet takedowns on record.

No additional technical details on the malware family or the hosting provider's identity were available in initial reporting. Further attribution and technical analysis are expected as the investigation continues.

Organizations should check endpoint inventories against any forthcoming IOCs. The size of the botnet suggests a long-dwell infection campaign — devices in unmanaged networks (IoT, legacy endpoints) are the most likely candidates for compromise.
