A vulnerability linked to the previously reported "ClaudeBleed" issue in Claude for Chrome reportedly persists across eight patches, allowing other browser extensions to read Gmail and Calendar data accessed by the AI extension. The flaw remains unpatched despite multiple update cycles addressing related issues. Extension-to-extension data exposure is a serious concern for AI browser agents that hold broad access to sensitive webmail and calendar data. Users relying on Claude for Chrome for email or calendar tasks should limit other installed extensions until a full fix is confirmed.
