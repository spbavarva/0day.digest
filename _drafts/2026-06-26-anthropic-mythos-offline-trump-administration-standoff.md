It has been two weeks since Anthropic took its Mythos-class models offline
following a Friday-evening ultimatum from the Trump administration.
Anthropic responded by sending executives to Washington, DC, but has
declined to comment publicly on the matter multiple times since, and there
is no resolution in sight. The standoff leaves Mythos unavailable to users
while the underlying dispute remains undisclosed.
