OpenAI CEO Sam Altman announced GPT-5.5-Cyber, a new frontier model purpose-built for cybersecurity. The model will not be publicly available at launch; instead, it will be rolled out first to a vetted group of "critical cyber defenders" — institutions that can use it to shore up defenses. Altman did not disclose a timeline for broader access.

The restricted distribution reflects deliberate dual-use risk management — OpenAI is screening early recipients rather than offering open API access. This positions GPT-5.5-Cyber as a defensive tool rather than a general-purpose model, though its capabilities will inevitably inform offensive AI tooling as well. Security organizations interested in early access should monitor OpenAI's communications.
