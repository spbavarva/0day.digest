OpenAI has begun rolling out a ChatGPT feature that allows users to connect all of their financial accounts to the chatbot for AI-driven personal finance advice. Security and privacy researchers are raising concerns about centralizing sensitive banking and investment data within a single AI platform. A compromised ChatGPT account or an OpenAI-side breach could expose a user's complete financial transaction history. Open questions remain around data retention policies, whether financial data feeds model training, and regulatory compliance with financial privacy legislation.
