Google's Threat Intelligence Group has attributed a previously undocumented
.NET backdoor, STOCKSTAY, to the Russian state-sponsored group Turla. The
backdoor has been deployed against government and military organizations in
Ukraine, as well as entities with an interest in Italian foreign policy.
Google describes the Windows backdoor as continually developed by the group,
indicating an active, evolving espionage operation.
