Mandiant's M-Trends 2026 report surfaces a structural gap in enterprise
security: the mean time to exploit is now estimated at negative seven days —
attackers exploit before patches reach most environments — while Verizon's 2025
DBIR puts median time to remediate edge-device vulnerabilities at 32 days.
More critically, most organizations apply patches without validating that
remediation actually worked as intended, leaving a false-confidence window
adversaries can exploit. The report identifies autonomous validation workflows
— automated re-testing after patching — as a core remediation component rather
than an optional add-on. Security teams deploying vulnerability management
programs should integrate confirmation testing into the patch lifecycle, not
treat it as a separate exercise.
