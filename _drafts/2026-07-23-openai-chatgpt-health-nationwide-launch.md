OpenAI is making ChatGPT Health available to all US users, letting people
connect medical records and health-tracking data from services like Apple
Health, Function, and MyFitnessPal. During a briefing, OpenAI's VP of health
product Ashley Alexander said the company's models "are now capable of
reasoning at levels that are better than clinician level." The rollout
expands access beyond the previously limited preview. No independent
clinical validation of the capability claim was cited in the announcement.
