US insurance company AssuranceAmerica disclosed a data breach affecting
nearly 6.9 million drivers after attackers gained access to its systems
earlier this year. The disclosure did not specify the initial access
vector or which categories of driver data were exposed. AssuranceAmerica
is notifying affected individuals per breach disclosure requirements.
