Insurance company AssuranceAmerica has disclosed a data breach affecting
nearly 7 million drivers, after attackers gained access to its systems
earlier this year. The company has not detailed the exact data types
exposed in initial disclosures. Affected individuals should watch for
notification letters and monitor for identity theft or fraud attempts
tied to insurance or driver records.
