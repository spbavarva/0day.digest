West Pharmaceutical Services, a supplier of packaging components for injectable drugs
and drug delivery systems, disclosed a ransomware attack that forced the company to take
systems offline globally. Attackers exfiltrated data before deploying file-encrypting
ransomware. West Pharmaceutical's products are used across vaccine and biologic supply
chains, making operational disruption potentially significant beyond the company itself.
No ransomware group had claimed responsibility in the feed summary. The company has not
disclosed the scope of the data exfiltration or estimated recovery timeline.
