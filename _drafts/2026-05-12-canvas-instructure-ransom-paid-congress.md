Instructure, the company behind the Canvas learning management system, confirmed it struck
a deal with hackers involving ransom payment in exchange for the return and purported
deletion of stolen data. The cyberattack disrupted Canvas access for students during final
exams. Congress has announced an investigation into the incident. Canvas is used by
millions of students and educators at institutions globally, giving the breach significant
scale. The verifiability of data deletion guarantees from ransomware actors is effectively
zero; institutions and users should treat the exposed data as permanently compromised.
