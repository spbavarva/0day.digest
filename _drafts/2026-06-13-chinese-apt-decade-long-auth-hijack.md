A China-linked threat actor took control of a target organization's
authentication stack and maintained persistent access for roughly ten years.

During that time, the attackers had full visibility into the organization's
administrative activity.

The compromised network was described as isolated, meaning the intrusion
bypassed the segmentation intended to limit exposure of that environment.

Further detail on the initial access vector and tooling used was not
available at time of writing.
