INTERPOL's Operation Ramz targeted cybercrime infrastructure across the Middle
East and North Africa, resulting in the seizure of 53 malware and phishing
servers and the arrest of more than 200 individuals. Investigators also
identified hundreds of compromised devices used in the operation and notified
affected device owners.

The operation reflects continued international law enforcement coordination
against regional cybercrime networks, though specific TTPs and IOCs have not
yet been published. Watch for follow-on technical reporting from participating
national agencies for actionable indicators.
