Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab released Inkling, its first
open-weights model — an Apache-2.0 licensed Mixture-of-Experts transformer
with 975B total parameters (41B active), trained on 45 trillion tokens of
text, images, audio, and video. A smaller companion, Inkling-Small (276B
total, 12B active), is planned but not yet released while testing
continues. The model card includes shorter training-data documentation than
is typical for US lab releases.
