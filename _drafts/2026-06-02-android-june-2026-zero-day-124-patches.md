Google released its June 2026 Android security bulletin addressing 124 vulnerabilities,
including one zero-day confirmed as actively exploited in targeted attacks prior to the
patch. The advisory covers multiple severity levels across the Android framework, system,
and kernel components.

The actively exploited zero-day is the immediate patching priority. All Android device
owners and enterprise administrators managing Android fleets should verify OTA updates
are applied. Vendor-specific patches from Samsung, Pixel, and others will follow Google's
base timeline.
