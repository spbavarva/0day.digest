Unit 42 published a research report analyzing current trends in data theft and extortion, examining how advancing frontier AI models are reshaping attacker strategies for reconnaissance, initial access, and automated data staging. The report outlines how AI is lowering the barrier for targeted extortion operations and accelerating the pace of double-extortion campaigns. Key defensive recommendations include improved data classification, faster detection of exfiltration patterns, and building negotiation preparedness for organizations facing extortion scenarios. The intersection of frontier AI capabilities with ransomware operations represents a meaningful shift in attacker economics.
