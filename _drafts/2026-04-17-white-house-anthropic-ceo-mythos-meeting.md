The White House Chief of Staff is scheduled to meet with Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei to discuss
Claude Mythos Preview, the company's restricted cybersecurity-focused AI model. A White House
official confirmed the administration is actively engaging with advanced AI labs on their models
and the security of software.

The meeting signals a potential thaw in the previously adversarial relationship between the Trump
administration and Anthropic. Earlier reporting documented DoD and senior administration officials
being briefed on Mythos' capabilities, which Anthropic has described as too powerful for broad
public release. The engagement reflects the growing role frontier AI models are playing in federal
cybersecurity strategy and defense posture discussions.
