The FBI warned that the Silent Ransom Group (SRG) has escalated tactics to
include in-person visits to US law firms as part of social engineering schemes
designed to install remote access tools and exfiltrate sensitive client data.

SRG combines vishing, callback phishing, and now physical presence at target
organizations to establish trust. Law firms are the primary targets due to the
high value of privileged client communications. Employees should verify any
walk-in "IT support" through independent channels and report unsolicited access
requests to security teams immediately.
