Threat actors breached Hims, a telehealth brand offering treatments for conditions
including hair loss, weight management, and sexual health. The breach exposed protected
health information (PHI) tied to highly stigmatized medical conditions. The sensitive
nature of Hims' patient data creates elevated risk for affected users, who may face
targeted phishing, extortion, or social harm if their treatment history is weaponized.
The breach size has not been disclosed. Affected individuals should monitor for
suspicious contact referencing their health conditions and be alert to phishing
campaigns impersonating Hims or related providers.
