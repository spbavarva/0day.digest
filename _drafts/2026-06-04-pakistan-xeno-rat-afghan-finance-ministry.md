A Pakistan-linked threat actor has been conducting cyber-espionage operations
against the Afghan Finance Ministry using Xeno RAT, a publicly available
remote access tool. Despite relying on standard TTPs and off-the-shelf tooling,
the campaign has been effective against Afghanistan's resource-constrained
cybersecurity posture. The use of commodity malware by nation-state actors is
a growing pattern — Xeno RAT has a public GitHub repository and is increasingly
repurposed for targeted intrusions. Detection signatures for Xeno RAT are
broadly available in major EDR and SIEM platforms, and organizations should
verify coverage is active.
