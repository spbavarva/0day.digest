Meta says it detected and blocked new spear-phishing attempts against WhatsApp users linked to Israeli spyware vendor NSO Group. The campaign tried to trick targets into clicking malicious links that redirected to external sites, likely as a delivery stage for spyware. Meta is filing a federal contempt order against NSO, arguing the activity violates a permanent injunction barring the company from targeting WhatsApp and its users. The case underscores that court orders alone haven't stopped commercial spyware vendors from probing the platform.
