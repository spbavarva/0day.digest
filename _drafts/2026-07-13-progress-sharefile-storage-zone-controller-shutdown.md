Progress Software has notified ShareFile customers to manually shut down
their Storage Zone Controller servers while it investigates a credible
security threat. The company has not yet disclosed technical details.
Organizations running Storage Zone Controller should follow Progress's
shutdown guidance and monitor for an official advisory with remediation
steps.
