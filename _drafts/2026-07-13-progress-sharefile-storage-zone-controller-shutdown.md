Progress Software is notifying ShareFile Storage Zone Controller customers to manually shut down their servers while it investigates a "credible threat." No breach has been confirmed. Given Progress's history with the MOVEit mass-exploitation incident, customers running Storage Zone Controller should shut down as advised and watch for further guidance.
