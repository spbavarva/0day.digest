Hackers accessed third-party partner repositories linked to DocketWise, a
legal immigration software platform, exfiltrating records for 143,000
individuals. Stolen data includes names, addresses, Social Security numbers,
financial information, and medical data.

The presence of SSNs alongside medical records creates elevated identity theft
and potential HIPAA exposure for affected users. DocketWise clients should
notify impacted individuals and assess whether third-party repository access
was properly scoped and monitored.
