Agent-testing startup Patronus AI, founded by former Meta AI researchers,
has raised $50 million to build simulated environments for stress-testing
AI agents before deployment. The company's investors point to strong
demand as more organizations ship autonomous agents and need ways to
evaluate their behavior under adversarial or edge-case conditions.
