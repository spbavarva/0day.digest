The Trump administration lifted restrictions on Anthropic's Claude models
that had been imposed following a cybersecurity alarm, SecurityWeek
reports. Anthropic separately confirmed Tuesday night that Claude Fable 5
is now widely available. Few additional details were given about the
nature of the original restrictions or why they were lifted.
