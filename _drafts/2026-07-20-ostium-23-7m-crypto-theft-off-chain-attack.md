Ostium, a decentralized trading platform, disclosed that an attacker stole
$23.75 million from its liquidity provider vault by compromising off-chain
infrastructure used to feed price data into the protocol. Rather than
exploiting smart contract code directly, the attacker targeted the
supporting off-chain price oracle systems. The incident highlights that
off-chain infrastructure feeding on-chain protocols remains a significant
attack surface for DeFi platforms.
