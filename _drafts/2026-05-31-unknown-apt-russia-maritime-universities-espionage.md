An unattributed threat actor has been running a sustained espionage campaign against Russian maritime universities, schools training personnel for Russia's shipping and fishing industries, and Russian diplomats — spanning nearly two years. More than half of observed intrusions targeted maritime educational institutions. The campaign's focus on shipping and naval training infrastructure suggests intelligence collection objectives, likely related to Russia's commercial or military maritime operations. Specific TTPs and IOCs were not detailed in available reporting. While targets are primarily Russian, the campaign is a reminder of ongoing nation-state-level espionage activity against critical sector training pipelines.
