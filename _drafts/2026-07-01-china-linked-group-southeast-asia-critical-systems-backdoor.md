A China-linked threat group has compromised at least 10 organizations
across Southeast Asia, including two state-owned entities, as part of a
campaign targeting critical systems. Dark Reading reports the group
deployed a new backdoor as part of the intrusions. No further technical
detail on the backdoor or initial access vector was disclosed.
