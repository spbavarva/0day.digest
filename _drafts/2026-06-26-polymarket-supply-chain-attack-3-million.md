Polymarket says hackers stole an estimated $3 million from customers after
injecting a malicious script into the platform's frontend. The attackers
reached the frontend through a breach at a third-party vendor rather than
Polymarket's own infrastructure directly — a supply-chain compromise rather
than a direct attack on the platform. Polymarket has committed to fully
reimbursing affected customers.
