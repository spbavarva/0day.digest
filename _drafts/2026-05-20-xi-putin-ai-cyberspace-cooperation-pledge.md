In a joint statement, Xi Jinping and Vladimir Putin pledged deeper cooperation across AI development, cyberspace governance, satellite internet, and open-source software. The stated goal is reducing dependence on Western technology and building an independent technological ecosystem. The agreement covers joint software development and shared satellite infrastructure. For security practitioners, the alignment signals continued coordination between two advanced threat actor ecosystems and potential standardization of offensive cyber tools and frameworks.
