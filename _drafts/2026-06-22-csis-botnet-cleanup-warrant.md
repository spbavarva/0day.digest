A newly public Federal Court ruling reveals that the Canadian Security
Intelligence Service used its threat reduction warrant powers for the
first time to remotely clean botnet infections from servers, home
routers, and IoT devices located in Canada. The warrant authorized CSIS
to alter the infected devices to neutralize two foreign-run botnets. The
ruling, released June 15, sets a legal precedent for state intervention
against botnet infrastructure operating domestically.
