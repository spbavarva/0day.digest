The White House issued an executive order establishing a voluntary framework under which AI labs would
provide early government access to frontier models before public release, alongside investments in
federal AI security capabilities. The voluntary nature has drawn mixed reactions — some see it as
pragmatic collaboration, others as insufficient oversight. Labs including Anthropic and OpenAI would
be expected to share pre-release model safety evaluations with federal agencies. Organizations deploying
frontier models in regulated sectors should monitor for follow-on mandatory requirements as the
voluntary framework matures.
