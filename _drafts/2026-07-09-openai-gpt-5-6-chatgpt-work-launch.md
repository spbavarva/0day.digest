OpenAI has rolled out GPT-5.6 to the public after receiving approval from
the Trump administration, roughly two weeks after the model's limited
preview to government-approved organizations drew regulatory scrutiny.
Alongside the GPT-5.6 rollout, OpenAI announced "ChatGPT Work," an agent
product described as able to take action across a user's apps and files,
stay on a project for hours, and turn a goal into finished work. OpenAI
CEO Sam Altman called GPT-5.6 the company's best model to date.
