French authorities took a 15-year-old into police custody on April 25 in connection with a breach of ANTS (Agence Nationale des Titres Sécurisés), the national agency that processes applications for passports, national identity cards, residence permits, and driver's licenses. The scope of exposed records has not been disclosed, and the investigation is ongoing.

ANTS handles sensitive identity document applications for the entire French population. If the breach is confirmed at scale, it could expose document application data for a significant number of residents. No public mitigation guidance has been issued yet; watch for official statements from ANTS on the scope and any protective measures for affected individuals.
