CISA published an advisory for ABB T-MAC Plus version 4.0-24 covering four CVEs (CVE-2025-14771 through CVE-2025-14774) with a top CVSS score of 9.9. The flaws include files/directories accessible to external parties and authorization bypass issues that could let an attacker compromise the system in different ways. ABB has released an update that resolves the reported vulnerabilities. Operators running T-MAC Plus in industrial environments should apply the update and restrict network exposure of the device in the meantime.
