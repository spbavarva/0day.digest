A campaign dubbed EvilTokens is targeting businesses across the US and
Europe with a "ghost phishing" technique that keeps the malicious page
hidden until it decrypts and renders inside the victim's browser. Because
the page doesn't resolve until execution, traditional URL-reputation
checks in email security tools miss the attack. Security teams should
supplement URL filtering with browser-side behavioral detection for
Microsoft 365 and other high-value login flows.
