Decentralized prediction market Polymarket says attackers stole roughly $3
million by compromising a third-party vendor and using that access to target
users. Polymarket has not named the vendor or detailed how the compromise
occurred. The incident is a reminder that platforms handling user funds
inherit the security posture of their vendors, not just their own stack.
