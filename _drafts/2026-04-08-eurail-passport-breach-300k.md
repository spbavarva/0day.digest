Eurail disclosed that a December 2025 breach exposed passport numbers for more than 300,000 customers. A threat actor claimed the attack in February, alleging theft of 1.3 TB including source code, database backups, and Zendesk support tickets.

Passport data exposure carries long-tail identity fraud risk that outlasts typical breach notification windows. Affected Eurail customers should monitor for identity fraud and consider requesting new travel documents. The source code and database exfiltration suggests the attacker had deep access to backend systems.
