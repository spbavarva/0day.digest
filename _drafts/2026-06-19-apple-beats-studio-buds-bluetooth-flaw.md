Apple patched CVE-2025-20701 (CVSS 8.8) in its Beats Studio Buds wireless
earbuds. The flaw is an incorrect-authorization issue in the Airoha
Bluetooth audio SDK that allowed a nearby attacker to pair with the
earbuds without user consent and eavesdrop via the microphone. Users
should update their Beats Studio Buds firmware as soon as it's available
through the Apple/Beats companion app.
