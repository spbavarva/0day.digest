Slovakian national Alan Bill, 33, received a 16-year federal prison sentence after pleading guilty to conspiracy to distribute controlled substances for his role administering Kingdom Market. The platform operated from March 2021 to December 2023 and was used by drug dealers and cybercriminals.

The sentence is among the harsher outcomes for dark web marketplace operators, signaling sustained DOJ focus on criminal infrastructure administrators rather than just end-users. Kingdom Market was taken down in a multinational law enforcement operation in December 2023.
