Western officials have warned that Russian intelligence services are aggressively expanding efforts to acquire sanctioned technology, deploying fake front companies, recruited middlemen, and embedded cyber operators who gather intelligence for potential attacks on critical infrastructure.

The campaign blends traditional espionage tradecraft with cyber collection — organizations in defense, semiconductor, and critical infrastructure sectors should review supply chain partner vetting and watch for anomalous requests from unfamiliar foreign entities. Elevated insider-threat awareness is also warranted for teams that handle dual-use technology.
