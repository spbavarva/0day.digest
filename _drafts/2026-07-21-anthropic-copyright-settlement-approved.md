A federal court has given final approval to Anthropic's $1.5 billion
settlement resolving claims that it used copyrighted books to train its
Claude models without authorization. The settlement is one of the largest
AI copyright resolutions to date, but it only resolves this specific case —
the broader legal question of whether training on copyrighted works
constitutes fair use remains unsettled across the industry.
