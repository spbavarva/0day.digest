CISA added four vulnerabilities to its Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog with confirmed active
exploitation. CVE-2024-57726 (CVSS 9.9) is a missing authorization flaw in SimpleHelp remote
support software. The catalog additions also cover Samsung MagicINFO 9 Server and D-Link DIR-823X
series routers, both under active exploitation.

Federal agencies face a May 2026 remediation deadline. Organizations running SimpleHelp, Samsung
MagicINFO 9, or D-Link DIR-823X gear should treat patching as emergency priority. The CVSS 9.9 score
on the SimpleHelp flaw indicates near-total access without authentication — a common initial access
vector for ransomware affiliates targeting MSP environments.
