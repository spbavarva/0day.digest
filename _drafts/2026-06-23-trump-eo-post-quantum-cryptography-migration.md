A new executive order requires U.S. federal agencies to transition
high-value assets and high-impact systems to post-quantum cryptography (PQC)
by the end of 2030, with a 2031 deadline for remaining systems. The order
accelerates existing federal PQC migration timelines. Vendors and contractors
selling software or cloud services to federal agencies should expect
PQC-readiness requirements to start appearing in procurement and compliance
contracts well ahead of those deadlines.
