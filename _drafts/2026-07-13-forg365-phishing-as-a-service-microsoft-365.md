A new phishing-as-a-service operation called Forg365 combines device code
phishing, adversary-in-the-middle session theft, antibot evasion, and
AI-assisted lure creation to target Microsoft 365 accounts, followed by
post-compromise mailbox operations. The service is distributed via
Telegram and priced at $400 a month or $3,800 a year, lowering the barrier
to entry for attackers. The AitM and device-code combination lets buyers
bypass MFA protections that would stop simpler credential-phishing kits.
