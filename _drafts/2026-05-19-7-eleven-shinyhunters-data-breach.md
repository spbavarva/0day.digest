7-Eleven confirmed its systems were breached in a cyberattack first claimed by the ShinyHunters extortion group last month.
7-Eleven operates one of the world's largest convenience store chains with tens of thousands of locations globally.
The company has acknowledged the breach but has not publicly disclosed the scope of exposed data or the number of individuals affected.
ShinyHunters has a history of large-scale data exfiltration targeting consumer-facing platforms; affected customers may face follow-on phishing and credential stuffing risks once the dataset surfaces.
