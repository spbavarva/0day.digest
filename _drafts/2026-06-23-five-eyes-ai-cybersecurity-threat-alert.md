Intelligence agencies from the Five Eyes alliance issued a joint alert
warning that artificial intelligence is accelerating cybersecurity threats on
a much faster timeline than previously expected, stating that the relevant
timeline "is not years, it is months." The alert did not detail specific new
indicators of compromise but signals a shift in how allied intelligence
services are prioritizing AI as a near-term threat vector.
