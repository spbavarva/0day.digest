The Iranian cyber group Handala sent WhatsApp messages to US service members stationed in Bahrain claiming they would be targeted with drone and missile strikes. The campaign appears to be a social engineering and psychological operations effort, consistent with Handala's history of targeting Israeli and Western personnel with blended technical and influence operations.

US military personnel and contractors in the region should report suspicious WhatsApp contacts through proper security channels and avoid engaging with unsolicited messages. While this incident appears primarily psychological in nature, Handala has previously deployed data-wiping malware against targets in the Middle East. Social engineering campaigns from this group often precede or accompany technical intrusion attempts.
