Kaspersky GReAT's 2026 threat report on small and medium businesses
highlights a rise in attacks impersonating fake AI tools, alongside
established phishing schemes and stolen data sold on the dark web. The
report frames fake-AI lures as a growing addition to SMB-targeted social
engineering, on top of already-prevalent phishing and credential-theft
activity. No specific campaigns or IOCs were named in the summary.
