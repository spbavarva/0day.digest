CISA is urging Fortinet customers to secure FortiGate appliances against
ongoing malicious activity tied to the "FortiBleed" credential-leak
campaign. The number of affected devices has grown to 86,644, up from the
~74,000 reported a day earlier, and CISA attributes the activity to
Russian-speaking threat actors. Organizations running FortiGate should
rotate credentials, review authentication logs for anomalous access, and
confirm patch status even if their devices weren't on the earlier count.
