The House Committee on Homeland Security has requested a briefing from
Instructure on a data breach and service disruption affecting Canvas, the
widely deployed learning management system used across K-12 and higher
education. Canvas serves tens of millions of students and educators in the
United States; a breach of this scale implicates student PII, academic records,
and institutional credentials. Instructure has not publicly disclosed the scope
of the breach or the data categories compromised. Congressional scrutiny
typically precedes mandatory disclosure requirements and can accelerate vendor
transparency. Educational institutions using Canvas should monitor for official
guidance and review their incident response posture ahead of potential
follow-on disclosures.
