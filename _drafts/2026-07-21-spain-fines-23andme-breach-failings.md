Spain's data protection authority, the AEPD, fined 23andMe nearly $3 million
over cybersecurity failings that enabled the company's 2023 data breach.
The AEPD's decision states more than 2,600 Spaniards were among the roughly
6.9 million people affected worldwide by the original breach. The fine adds
to the regulatory fallout 23andMe has faced globally since the incident.
