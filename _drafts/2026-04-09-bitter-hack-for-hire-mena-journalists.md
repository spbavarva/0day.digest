A hack-for-hire campaign attributed to a threat actor with suspected Indian government ties
targeted journalists, activists, and government officials across the Middle East and North
Africa (MENA). Research from Access Now, Lookout, and SMEX documented attacks on prominent
Egyptian journalists and government critics.

The campaign is linked to the Bitter threat cluster, which has a history of targeted
espionage operations in South and Southeast Asia. Targets across the MENA region represent
a notable geographic expansion. Journalists and civil society organizations in the region
should audit device security and review guidance from digital rights organizations such as
Access Now.
