The Klue-Salesforce breach continues to widen, with a threat actor
calling itself Icarus now leaking data taken from victim organizations,
according to Dark Reading. The incident traces back to attackers who
breached Klue, a competitive-intelligence vendor, and abused its OAuth
tokens to access customers' Salesforce data. Additional victims have
emerged since the breach was first disclosed. Organizations that
integrated Klue with Salesforce should treat previously connected data
as potentially exposed and monitor for leaked data tied to their org.
