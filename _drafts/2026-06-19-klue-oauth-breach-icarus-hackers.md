Market intelligence platform Klue has confirmed a security incident in
which attackers stole OAuth tokens used to connect to customers'
Salesforce environments. A new extortion group calling itself "Icarus"
has publicly claimed the attack, and the list of affected victims is
reportedly growing. The incident follows the pattern of recent OAuth
token-theft campaigns targeting Salesforce integrations. Organizations
using Klue should rotate connected-app OAuth tokens and audit
third-party Salesforce integrations.
