Unit 42 has tracked an espionage cluster, CL-STA-1062, targeting government
entities and critical infrastructure in Southeast Asia. The attackers use a
hybrid toolkit that includes a custom backdoor dubbed TinyRCT. The campaign
focuses on espionage rather than disruption, consistent with state-aligned
intelligence-gathering operations in the region.
