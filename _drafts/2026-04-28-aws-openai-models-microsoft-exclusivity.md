Amazon Web Services announced a slate of OpenAI model offerings on AWS — including access to a new OpenAI agent service — one day after OpenAI and Microsoft restructured their partnership to remove Microsoft's exclusive cloud distribution rights. OpenAI models are now available on both AWS and Google Cloud's Vertex AI.

The move significantly broadens OpenAI's distribution reach and gives AWS Bedrock customers native access to GPT-class models alongside existing Bedrock offerings. Organizations with multi-cloud AI workloads should review updated pricing and compliance implications of routing production AI traffic through AWS-hosted OpenAI endpoints.
