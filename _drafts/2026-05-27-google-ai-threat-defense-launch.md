Google Cloud announced AI Threat Defense, a new offering that integrates
AI-powered capabilities into threat detection and response workflows on the
Google Cloud Security platform. The announcement positions it to help teams
respond to AI-accelerated threats faster than manual processes allow.

The launch is part of a broader industry push toward AI-native security
operations as threat actors increasingly use AI to compress attack timelines.
Specific detection capabilities and pricing were not detailed in the
announcement.
