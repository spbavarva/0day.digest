Anthropic reversed course on pulling Claude Fable 5 from subscription plans,
per an update from the @claudeai account. Starting July 20, Fable 5 will be
included in all Max and Team Premium plans at 50% of standard limits. Pro
and Team Standard users keep access via usage credits and get a one-time
$100 credit. The reversal follows competitive pressure from OpenAI's
GPT-5.6 Sol and, to a lesser extent, Kimi 3 — Anthropic had planned to move
Fable 5 to API-only pricing.
