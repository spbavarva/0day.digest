Unit 42 published research on supply chain risks in third-party "skills" used to extend AI agents.
The research outlines how unaudited skills can introduce hidden vulnerabilities or be chained together into multi-stage attacks against enterprise AI agent deployments.
Recommendations focus on integrity verification of third-party skills before they're loaded into production agents.
As AI agent ecosystems grow, treating agent extensions with the same supply chain scrutiny as software dependencies will become increasingly important.
