Meta disabled an Instagram AI feature, launched earlier this week, that let
users generate AI images of any public account by tagging it — without the
account owner's consent. The feature drew immediate backlash for enabling
non-consensual AI deepfakes of public profiles. In a blog post, Meta said
the tool "missed the mark" and confirmed it is "no longer available." Details
on whether previously generated images will be removed were not disclosed.
