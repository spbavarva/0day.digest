Eurail confirmed that passport numbers for more than 300,000 individuals were
leaked in a breach that occurred in December 2025. The attacker who claimed
responsibility says they stole 1.3 TB of data including source code, database
backups, and Zendesk support tickets.

Affected individuals should monitor for identity fraud and phishing attempts
leveraging travel booking data. The inclusion of source code and database
backups in the claimed haul suggests the attacker had deep access to Eurail's
backend systems.
