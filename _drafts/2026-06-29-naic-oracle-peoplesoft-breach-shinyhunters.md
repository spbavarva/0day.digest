The National Association of Insurance Commissioners (NAIC) was breached via
an attack on its Oracle PeopleSoft deployment. The ShinyHunters extortion
group claims to have stolen 3.1TB of data from the organization.

NAIC represents US state insurance regulators, raising questions about what
regulatory or member-state data may have been exposed. The group has been
linked to a string of recent breaches involving Oracle-hosted enterprise
applications.
