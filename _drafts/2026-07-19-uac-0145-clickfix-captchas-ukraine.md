Russian state-sponsored threat actor UAC-0145, a sub-cluster of Sandworm affiliated with Russia's GRU, is using the ClickFix social engineering technique to trick Ukrainian targets into infecting their own devices. Victims are presented with fake CAPTCHA verification pages that lead them to run malicious commands manually, sidestepping automated malware delivery defenses. According to Ukraine's CERT-UA, the campaign deploys data-stealing malware once the lure succeeds. Ukrainian organizations should train users to recognize fake CAPTCHA/verification prompts and restrict execution of copy-pasted commands.
