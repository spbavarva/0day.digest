CISA launched a nomination form allowing security researchers, vendors, and industry partners to
submit vulnerability reports for consideration in the Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog. The
KEV catalog is used by federal agencies and widely adopted by the private sector as a prioritized
patch list.

Opening nominations to the broader research community may accelerate the time between public
exploitation discovery and KEV inclusion, giving defenders earlier signal on which CVEs warrant
emergency patching priority.
