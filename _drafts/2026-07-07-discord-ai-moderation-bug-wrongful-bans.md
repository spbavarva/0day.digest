Discord confirmed a bug in its AI moderation system wrongfully banned users
over harmless images, with the issue affecting accounts since May. An
additional 200 users were banned over a single weekend before Discord's team
identified and fixed the problem. The company has not detailed the root
cause of the moderation model's false positives. The incident underscores
ongoing reliability risk in automated AI moderation at scale.
