The Trump administration issued a US export control directive Friday requiring Anthropic to suspend access to its Mythos 5 and Fable 5 models for foreign nationals. The action followed a White House report claiming the models could be "jailbroken" for cyberoffensive use — specifically that asking models to "fix this code" after an initial refusal produced exploit-usable output via a multi-step manual process. Cybersecurity expert Kate Moussouris, who reviewed the report at Anthropic's request, described the finding as "the model working as intended" — normal defensive security research. A coalition of cybersecurity executives subsequently urged the administration to reverse the restrictions, arguing they harm US cyber defense capabilities. The episode highlights deepening tension between AI export control policy and legitimate security research workflows.
