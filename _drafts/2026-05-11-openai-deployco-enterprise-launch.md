OpenAI has launched DeployCo, a new enterprise deployment subsidiary designed to help
organizations bring frontier AI models into production and measure business impact.
DeployCo positions OpenAI as a managed implementation partner in addition to a model
developer, competing directly with system integrators and cloud providers on enterprise
AI deployment.

No specific model, security architecture, or data-handling details were disclosed in the
announcement. Organizations evaluating DeployCo engagements should assess data residency,
model version pinning, and access control provisions before committing production workloads.
