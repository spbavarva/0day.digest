OpenAI confirmed it limited the rollout of its new GPT-5.6 model series
after a request from the U.S. government. The company previewed the models
and their capabilities to officials ahead of launch and, at the
government's request, is starting with a limited preview rather than a
full release. OpenAI said it does not want this kind of government access
process to become the long-term default, warning it keeps capable tools
away from developers, enterprises, cyber defenders, and global partners.
