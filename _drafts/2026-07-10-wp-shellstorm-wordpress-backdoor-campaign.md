A cybercrime crew left one of its own servers exposed on the internet for
three weeks, revealing the inner workings of a mass WordPress-hacking
operation now tracked as WP-SHELLSTORM. The exposed files included
hacking tools, activity logs, and a target list naming more than 1.4
million websites, though researchers say far fewer sites were actually
compromised. The leak gives defenders unusual visibility into how a
large-scale site-hacking operation runs from the inside, including its
targeting and backdooring methodology.
