The FBI has issued an alert warning that the Silent Ransom Group is sending
physical operatives directly to law firm offices to insert USB drives and
steal data from workstations. The technique bypasses network perimeter
defenses entirely by gaining physical access to endpoints.

Law firms are the primary stated targets, likely due to the sensitive and
valuable client data they hold. The hybrid physical-digital approach is
notable as it expands the threat model beyond traditional network-based attack
chains.

Organizations should enforce USB port lockdown via endpoint policy, add
physical access controls to workstations in sensitive areas, and train staff
to challenge and report unfamiliar individuals attempting physical device
access.
