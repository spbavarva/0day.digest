Kaspersky's Global Research and Analysis Team has published its quarterly threat landscape
report for industrial automation systems covering Q4 2025. The report covers infection vectors,
malware categories active against ICS/OT environments, regional attack statistics, and
breakdown by industry vertical.

Kaspersky GReAT quarterly ICS reports are among the most comprehensive public sources of
telemetry on operational technology threats. The Q4 2025 data covers the period immediately
preceding several notable ICS-targeting campaigns disclosed in early 2026, making the infection
vector breakdown particularly useful for calibrating defensive controls.

OT security teams should review the full report at Securelist for statistics relevant to their
region and industry. The infection vector data is actionable for patch prioritization and
network segmentation decisions in industrial environments.
