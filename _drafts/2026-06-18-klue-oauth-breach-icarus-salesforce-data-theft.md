Market intelligence platform Klue suffered an OAuth breach that let
threat actors tracked as "Icarus" steal Salesforce CRM data from multiple
connected organizations in an ongoing extortion campaign. Klue is now the
third integrated Salesforce app compromised in this wave of attacks;
confirmed victims include cybersecurity vendor Huntress. Organizations
using Klue's Battlecards integration should revoke its OAuth grants and
audit Salesforce data access logs for signs of exfiltration.
