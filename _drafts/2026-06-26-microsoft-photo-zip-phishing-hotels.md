Microsoft says an active phishing campaign has targeted hotel and other
hospitality organizations across Europe and Asia since April 2026. The lure
uses photo-themed ZIP archives to drop a Node.js-based implant onto
front-desk machines. Microsoft has not attributed the activity to a known
threat actor and the operators' end goal remains unclear. Hospitality staff
should be wary of unsolicited ZIP attachments claiming to contain guest or
booking photos.
