Kyushu Electric Power disclosed a physical security incident in which a storage drive containing private data on more than 10.9 million customers went missing.
The company described it as a physical security incident rather than a network intrusion.
Details on which specific data fields were stored on the drive, whether it was encrypted, and the circumstances of the loss were not specified in the disclosure.
Affected customers should watch for phishing or fraud attempts referencing their utility accounts pending further clarification from the company.
