OpenAI began a limited preview of its GPT-5.6 model series: Sol, the
flagship model; Terra, a balanced mid-tier model; and Luna, a fast,
low-cost model. OpenAI says Terra matches GPT-5.5's performance at half the
cost, while Luna offers strong capability at the lowest price point. Sol
brings stronger capabilities in coding, science, and cybersecurity alongside
what OpenAI calls its most advanced safety stack to date. OpenAI previewed
the models to the U.S. government ahead of launch and, at the government's
request, is starting with a limited rollout rather than general
availability.
