Google introduced two new additions to its general-purpose Gemini lineup:
Gemini 3.6 Flash and Gemini 3.5 Flash-Lite. The announcement notably did not
include a Gemini 3.5 Pro release, continuing to raise questions about
Google's roadmap for its flagship model tier. The same announcement also
introduced a dedicated security-focused model, Gemini 3.5 Flash Cyber,
covered separately.
