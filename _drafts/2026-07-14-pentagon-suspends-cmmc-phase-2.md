The Pentagon has suspended Phase 2 of its Cybersecurity Maturity Model Certification (CMMC) program while a newly formed review and reform task force conducts a comprehensive assessment of contractor cybersecurity requirements. CMMC Phase 2 was set to require defense contractors to undergo third-party assessments of their cybersecurity controls to handle sensitive government data. The suspension leaves affected contractors in a holding pattern on compliance timelines and investments tied to the program. Organizations in the defense industrial base should monitor for guidance from the task force before making further CMMC-driven compliance decisions.
