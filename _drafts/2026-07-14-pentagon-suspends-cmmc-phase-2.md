The Pentagon has suspended rollout of Cybersecurity Maturity Model
Certification (CMMC) Phase 2, the framework requiring defense contractors
to certify compliance with DoD cybersecurity controls. A newly formed CMMC
review and reform task force will conduct a comprehensive review of the
program. No new timeline for resumption has been announced. Contractors
mid-certification should watch for updated guidance as the review
proceeds.
