KDDI Corporation, a major Japanese telecommunications operator, disclosed that
threat actors gained unauthorized access to an email system it operates on
behalf of five other internet service providers in Japan. The intrusion
exposed up to 14.2 million email login credentials across the six affected
ISPs. KDDI has not disclosed the attack vector or how long the attackers had
access. Affected users should change their email passwords and watch for
phishing or account-takeover attempts using the exposed credentials.
