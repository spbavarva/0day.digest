Major AI companies have signed an open letter to Congress calling for legislation to close what they describe as an alarming biosecurity gap created by AI-assisted biological weapons research. The letter represents an unusual moment of cross-industry cooperation among typically competing firms. It signals that the AI industry is proactively seeking regulatory guardrails for dual-use biological risks before a significant incident forces reactive legislation.
