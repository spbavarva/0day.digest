The FSB-linked group Gamaredon has improved its malware loading techniques
and server-hiding methods, according to Dark Reading. The upgrades make the
group's long-running operation harder to detect with defenses built around
its older tradecraft, pushing defenders to revisit detection rules tuned to
the group's previous toolset.
