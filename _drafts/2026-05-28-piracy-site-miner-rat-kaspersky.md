Kaspersky GReAT researchers tracking a long-running cybercrime gang have found the group expanding to new piracy sites drawing tens of millions of visitors while upgrading its crypto miner payload with a newly discovered RAT module. The gang targets consumers of pirated books, movies, and TV shows who unknowingly download infected content. Adding remote access trojan capability to an established miner operation represents a significant escalation — operators can now conduct post-exploitation beyond passive coin mining, including credential theft, lateral movement, and ransomware staging. Consumers who have accessed pirated content sites should scan for miner and RAT indicators.
