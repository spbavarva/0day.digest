Apple, Google, Microsoft, Mozilla, and Oracle patched near-record security bug volumes in May
2026. Krebs on Security attributes the acceleration to AI platforms proving effective at finding
code vulnerabilities in human-made software, quickening the tempo of discovery and the resulting
patch workload across major vendors.

Microsoft's batch of 137 flaws included nine critical fixes and — notably — no zero-days, the
first such Patch Tuesday in two years according to Dark Reading. Administrators should prioritize
the critical-rated fixes across all affected vendor products in this cycle.
