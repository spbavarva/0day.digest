A threat actor is targeting organizations across multiple sectors with
voice-based social engineering calls impersonating fake security
requests, asking Microsoft 365 users to enroll a new Entra passkey. A
successful enrollment gives the attacker a persistent, phishing-resistant
credential on the victim's account. Organizations should train staff to
verify passkey enrollment requests through a separate trusted channel and
monitor for unexpected Entra passkey registrations.
