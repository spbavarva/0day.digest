OpenAI has upgraded the model powering ChatGPT's voice mode to GPT-Live.
The new model can delegate harder requests, such as web search or deeper
reasoning, to GPT-5.5 in the background while keeping the conversation
flowing, then brings the result back once ready. No security or safety
disclosures accompanied the launch; this is a model-landscape signal
rather than an incident.
