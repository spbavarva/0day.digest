A phishing campaign is targeting credentials for ManageWP, GoDaddy's platform for managing fleets of WordPress websites, by serving malicious sponsored search results through Google Ads. Victims searching for ManageWP are redirected to phishing login pages designed to harvest credentials. Compromise of ManageWP accounts could expose administrators to large-scale WordPress site takeovers. Administrators should enable multi-factor authentication on ManageWP accounts and verify they are accessing the legitimate domain before entering credentials.
