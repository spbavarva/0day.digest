Several healthcare data breaches collectively impacting hundreds of thousands to millions of US patients have been added to the HHS HIPAA breach reporting portal. The affected organizations span hospital networks and healthcare service providers; specific names and breach vectors were not detailed in reporting. Healthcare data carries elevated risk due to the permanence of medical records and their utility for insurance fraud, identity theft, and targeted social engineering. Affected patients should monitor explanation-of-benefits statements and exercise caution with unsolicited communications referencing medical history.
