Reps. Ted Lieu (D-CA) and Nathaniel Moran (R-TX) are expected to introduce
an "AI Kill Switch Act" that would require AI companies to shut down or
throttle their systems on orders from the Department of Homeland Security,
according to a Politico report cited by The Verge. The bill's specific
triggering conditions and enforcement mechanism weren't detailed in the
available summary. If introduced, it would mark one of the more direct
federal proposals for compelled AI shutdown authority to date.
