Cisco Talos has disclosed UAT-8302, a sophisticated China-nexus advanced persistent threat group that has been targeting government entities in South America since at least late 2024 and government agencies in southeastern Europe through 2025. Post-exploitation activity involves deployment of custom malware families that appear to be shared across multiple Chinese APT operations, suggesting infrastructure or tooling overlap with other state-sponsored groups. The targeting pattern — government entities across two distinct geographies — is consistent with intelligence-gathering objectives. Defenders at government agencies, particularly those in South America or southeastern Europe, should hunt for the indicators of compromise and TTPs detailed in the Talos disclosure.
