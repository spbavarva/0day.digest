A threat group tracked as "Armored Likho" is using the BusySnake
infostealer to compromise government agencies and electrical power
entities across Russia, Brazil, and Kazakhstan, according to researchers.
The campaign specifically targets critical infrastructure sectors across
multiple countries. No IOCs or initial-access vector were included in the
available summary.
