Eurail B.V. disclosed that a December 2025 network intrusion resulted in the theft of
personal data for over 300,000 individuals, including names and passport numbers. The
company provides digital rail passes covering 33 European national railways.

Affected individuals should monitor for identity theft attempts and targeted phishing
leveraging leaked passport data. Eurail has notified regulators and is contacting
impacted customers. No payment card data was mentioned in the disclosure.
