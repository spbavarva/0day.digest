French AI startup ZML, backed by Turing Award winner Yann LeCun, has
released a free product called LLMD aimed at speeding up AI inference across
a range of AI chips. The stated goal is to lower the cost of running AI
workloads by improving inference efficiency across heterogeneous chip
fleets. No architecture or benchmark details are available beyond the
initial announcement.
