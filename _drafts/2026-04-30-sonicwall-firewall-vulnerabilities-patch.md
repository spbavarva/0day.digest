SonicWall has issued an urgent advisory for newly disclosed firewall vulnerabilities that allow attackers to bypass security controls, access restricted services, and crash firewall devices. CVE identifiers and full technical details have not yet been published in the initial disclosure. SonicWall has characterized the patching as immediate priority.

SonicWall firewalls are widely deployed at enterprise and SME network edges. An exploitable bypass or DoS flaw on a perimeter firewall can serve as an entry point into protected networks or knock out visibility. Apply available vendor patches immediately; if patching is not possible, restrict management interface access to trusted IP ranges and monitor for anomalous traffic patterns.
