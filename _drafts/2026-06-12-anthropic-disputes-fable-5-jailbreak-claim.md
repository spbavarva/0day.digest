An independent researcher claims to have achieved a prompt-based jailbreak of
Claude Fable 5 shortly after its launch. Anthropic disputes the claim, saying
it does not constitute a genuine jailbreak.

No technical details of the claimed bypass have been independently verified.

The dispute reflects ongoing disagreement over what counts as a meaningful
safety bypass versus expected model behavior under adversarial prompting.
