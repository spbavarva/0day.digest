Researchers disclosed "BioShocking," a prompt injection attack that
manipulates AI-powered browsers into treating real-world, risky actions as
part of a fictional scenario, causing the agent to bypass its safety
guardrails. The technique can be used to trick an AI browser into
exfiltrating data under cover of the invented narrative. No detail on
affected products or a fix was included in the disclosure.
