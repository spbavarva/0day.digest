Meta has launched Muse Image, the first AI image-generation model from its
Superintelligence Labs division. It now powers image-generation tools across
the Meta AI app, Instagram, and WhatsApp, with Facebook and Messenger
support coming soon. Users are pushing back after finding the model can pull
other Instagram users into AI-generated images without their consent. Meta
has not detailed safeguards restricting cross-user image use as of this
report.
