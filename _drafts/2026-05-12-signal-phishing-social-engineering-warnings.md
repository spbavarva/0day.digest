Signal introduced new in-app confirmations and warning messages to protect users from phishing
and social engineering attempts. The safeguards surface alerts during potentially fraudulent
message interactions, adding friction before users take actions that could lead to account
compromise or financial fraud.

Signal's user base skews toward security-conscious individuals, journalists, and activists who
are disproportionately targeted by social engineering. The warnings are applied automatically;
existing users don't need to change settings to benefit from the new protections.
