Apple released iOS 26.5 with E2EE support for RCS messaging between iPhones and Android devices
running the latest Google Messages, rolling out as part of a cross-industry effort to replace SMS.
E2EE RCS is enabled by default with supported carriers, closing the longstanding plaintext SMS gap
for cross-platform messaging.

This matters for enterprise and security teams who have struggled to enforce encrypted messaging
across mixed-device environments. RCS E2EE is based on the MLS protocol, the same standard backing
secure group messaging in other modern applications. Carrier support rollout will determine the
practical timeline for full coverage.
