Dental benefits administrator DentaQuest has disclosed a data breach affecting roughly 2.6 million accounts. The exposed data includes sensitive personal information; technical details about the attack vector have not been publicly released. Affected individuals may be contacted by DentaQuest directly. Those with accounts should monitor credit reports for identity theft indicators and consider placing fraud alerts with credit bureaus.
