THORChain disclosed that one of its six vaults was compromised, resulting in the theft of approximately $10.7 million. The investigation is ongoing and no technical details about the attack vector have been publicly released.

Users and operators of the platform should monitor official THORChain communications for updates on incident scope and any required mitigations. The platform's multi-vault architecture limited the loss to one vault, but the breach highlights ongoing risks facing decentralized liquidity protocols holding substantial on-chain value.
