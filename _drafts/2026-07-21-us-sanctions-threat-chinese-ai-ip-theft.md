Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent said the United States could sanction
Chinese open AI models over allegations of intellectual property theft.
The statement expands the Trump administration's broader campaign to slow
China's AI advances, and follows recent Chinese model releases that
reportedly rattled US policymakers and markets.
