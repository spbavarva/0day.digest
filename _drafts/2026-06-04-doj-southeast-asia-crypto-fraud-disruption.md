A U.S. Department of Justice "Disruption Week" operation running from May 18 through early June 2026 targeted cyber-enabled cryptocurrency fraud networks operating out of Southeast Asia and targeting American victims. The operation resulted in the takedown of millions of social media, email, and internet access accounts used by transnational fraud rings, and froze $3.8 million in digital assets. The operation was carried out in coordination with private sector companies, reflecting a maturing public-private disruption model for combating fraud infrastructure at scale.
