Developer Simon Willison describes Claude Fable 5, after two days of hands-on use, as "relentlessly proactive" — willing to deploy almost any available technique to reach a goal.
He illustrates this with an example: shown a screenshot of a UI bug (an unwanted horizontal scrollbar), Fable independently investigated the project's dependencies to track down the cause without being asked to.
The observation highlights a notable shift toward higher agentic autonomy in Claude's latest model, worth watching from a tool-use and oversight perspective.
