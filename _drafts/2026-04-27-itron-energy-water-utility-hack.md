Itron, a technology provider serving utilities and municipalities globally, disclosed it discovered unauthorized access to its systems on April 13. The breach is described as affecting corporate IT systems; full scope has not been disclosed.

Itron's platform is used for smart grid management, water metering, and demand response across critical infrastructure operators. While the breach is currently described as corporate IT access, proximity of corporate and operational technology systems in utility environments warrants close monitoring. Utilities and cities running Itron products should confirm with the vendor which systems were affected and look for downstream indicators of compromise.
