OpenAI launched its new GPT-5.6 model family, which the company says
brings improvements across a range of areas including cybersecurity. The
new models are also positioned as the "preferred model" powering
Microsoft Copilot 365, continuing OpenAI's role as Microsoft's primary
model supplier. Specific capability benchmarks were not detailed in
initial coverage.
