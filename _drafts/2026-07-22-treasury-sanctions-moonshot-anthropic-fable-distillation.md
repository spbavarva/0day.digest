The US Treasury Department threatened sanctions after the White House
claimed Chinese AI company Moonshot distilled Anthropic's Fable model
without authorization. No sanctions have been imposed yet — the threat is
at the review/consideration stage. The episode intensifies an already
contentious Washington debate over Chinese labs' access to and use of US
frontier model outputs.
