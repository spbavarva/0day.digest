The JaredFromSubway Ethereum MEV (Maximal Extractable Value) bot suffered a
$15 million loss after an attacker manipulated its opportunity-detection
logic by feeding it fake cryptocurrency trading opportunities. The bot acted
on the fabricated signals, executing trades that funneled funds to the
attacker. The incident is a reminder that automated trading and arbitrage
systems are exploitable through their decision logic, not only through
smart-contract bugs.
