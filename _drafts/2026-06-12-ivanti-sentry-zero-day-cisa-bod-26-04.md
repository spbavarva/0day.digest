CISA issued Binding Operational Directive 26-04, ordering federal agencies to patch an actively exploited Ivanti Sentry vulnerability within three days.
The flaw is a critical-severity OS command injection bug that allows attackers to execute arbitrary code with root privileges.
SecurityWeek reports exploitation attempts are already hitting honeypots, indicating broad scanning against internet-facing Sentry instances.
Any organization running Ivanti Sentry should apply available patches immediately and check for signs of compromise, not just the federal agencies covered by the directive.
