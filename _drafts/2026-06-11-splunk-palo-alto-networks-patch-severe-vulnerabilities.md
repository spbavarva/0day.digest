Splunk and Palo Alto Networks have each released patches for severe vulnerabilities in their products.
The flaws could let attackers create or modify arbitrary files and access or modify protected resources.
No public proof-of-concept or active exploitation has been reported.
Admins running affected Splunk or Palo Alto Networks deployments should review the vendor advisories and apply updates promptly.
