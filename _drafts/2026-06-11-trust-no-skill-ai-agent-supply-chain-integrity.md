Unit 42 published research on supply chain risks facing enterprise AI agents that rely on third-party "skills" or plugins.
The research highlights how unaudited skills can introduce hidden vulnerabilities and enable multi-stage attack chains against agent deployments.
The recommended mitigation is integrity verification: auditing third-party skills before they're loaded into production agent environments.
As agentic AI adoption grows, this kind of supply chain hygiene becomes as important as dependency auditing in traditional software.
