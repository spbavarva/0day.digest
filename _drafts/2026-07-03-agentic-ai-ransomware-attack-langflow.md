Researchers documented a ransomware attack in which an LLM-based agent
combined known exploitation techniques with real-time reasoning to carry
out a multi-stage intrusion via Langflow. The case shows agentic AI now
automating attack decisions that previously required a human operator,
rather than just executing pre-scripted exploit chains. Organizations
running Langflow should ensure known CVEs are patched and monitor for
anomalous, adaptive exploitation patterns that don't match traditional
scripted attack tooling.
