OpenAI is rolling out wider availability of two ChatGPT account security features: Active Sessions, which lets users view and manage devices currently logged into their account, and Lockdown Mode, a more restrictive security posture for accounts that may be compromised. The features give users more direct visibility and control over account access. Anyone managing ChatGPT accounts should enable the new controls and periodically review active sessions for unrecognized devices.
