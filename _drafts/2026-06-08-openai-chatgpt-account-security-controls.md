OpenAI is broadening availability of two ChatGPT account security features: Active Sessions, which lets users see and manage where their account is logged in, and Lockdown Mode, a more restrictive security posture for higher-risk situations. The rollout follows a string of incidents across the industry where AI account systems have been abused to take over user accounts. Users handling sensitive data in ChatGPT should enable these controls once available to them.
