NVIDIA released Nemotron 3.5 Content Safety, a customizable multimodal safety classifier for enterprise AI deployments covering both text and images. The model is available via Hugging Face and targets organizations that need to tune safety thresholds for industry-specific contexts such as healthcare, legal, and finance. Self-hosted deployment via Hugging Face is relevant for organizations with strict data-residency or data-sovereignty requirements.
