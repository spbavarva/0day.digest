A hacker used an employee's credentials to access AI music generator Suno's
source code, exposing how the company built its training data. The code
revealed that Suno scraped decades of audio and lyrics from YouTube Music,
Deezer, and Genius. Suno has not previously disclosed the contents or
provenance of its training datasets. No user data breach has been reported —
the exposure is limited to internal source code and engineering details.
