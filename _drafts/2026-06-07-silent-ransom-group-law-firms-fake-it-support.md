The Silent Ransom Group extortion gang is actively targeting U.S. law firms
and professional services organizations using social engineering attacks that
impersonate IT support staff. According to a new Mandiant report, these calls
often lead to data theft within hours of initial contact.

Legal and professional services organizations should train staff to verify the
identity of anyone claiming to be internal IT support before granting remote
access or sharing credentials, and review remote-access tooling for signs of
unauthorized sessions.
