Google has rolled out end-to-end encryption for Gmail on Android and iOS, making the
feature available to enterprise Google Workspace users on mobile without additional tools
or plugins. Previously, Gmail E2EE on mobile required S/MIME configuration or third-party
email clients.

This is a meaningful usability improvement for regulated industries where encrypted email
is a compliance requirement. Enterprise Workspace admins should verify the feature is
enabled and confirm their key management approach aligns with organizational policy.
