7-Eleven has confirmed a data breach following a ransom demand from ShinyHunters, a prolific threat group responsible for numerous large-scale data theft operations. The attackers claim to have exfiltrated more than 600,000 Salesforce records containing personal information and corporate data. 7-Eleven confirmed the incident but full scope, affected jurisdictions, and notification timelines have not yet been disclosed. ShinyHunters continues to operate as one of the most active data extortion groups, with targets spanning retail, healthcare, and technology sectors.
