CISA, the FBI, and international partners issued a joint advisory warning
that Russian FSB Center 16 cyber actors continue to exploit poorly
configured and vulnerable networking devices to opportunistically
compromise critical infrastructure networks worldwide. The advisory
builds on a prior FBI public service announcement covering the same
actor's long-running campaign against networking devices. Organizations
operating exposed or legacy network hardware should review router
hardening guidance and check for indicators tied to FSB Center 16 activity.
