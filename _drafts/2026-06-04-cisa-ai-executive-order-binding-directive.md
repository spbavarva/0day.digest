CISA Director Andersen announced at the TechNet Cyber conference that a binding operational directive implementing the AI executive order will be released this week. The directive will focus on vulnerability alleviation and vulnerability management for AI systems across federal agencies. Federal agencies and their contractors should prepare for mandatory compliance timelines; private-sector organizations can use the directive as a benchmark for their own AI security programs.
