Seqrite Labs documented Operation Dragon Weave, a China-aligned cyber espionage campaign delivering AdaptixC2 agents to targets in the Czech Republic and Taiwan. Targeted sectors include government, research, academic, technology, and financial services. Initial access is via spear-phishing emails containing malicious ZIP attachments.

AdaptixC2 is a commercial-grade C2 framework used across multiple threat actor groups. Organizations in the targeted sectors should review email gateway logs for suspicious ZIP attachments from unfamiliar senders and verify that endpoint detection tools have coverage for known AdaptixC2 indicators of compromise.
