Progress Software has confirmed that a high-severity zero-day vulnerability was behind the emergency shutdown of ShareFile Storage Zone Controllers last week. The company has released security updates that patch the flaw. The emergency shutdown suggests the issue was serious enough to take the affected service offline rather than risk continued exposure, though active exploitation has not been explicitly confirmed. Organizations running ShareFile Storage Zone Controllers should apply the patch immediately and review logs for signs of compromise during the exposure window.
