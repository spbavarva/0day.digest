MITRE has released the Fight Fraud Framework, a behavior-based model cataloging the
tactics and techniques used by fraudsters. The structure mirrors MITRE ATT&CK, providing
a common vocabulary for fraud detection teams, threat analysts, and platform defenders.

Organizations operating in fintech, banking, or e-commerce can use the framework to
structure detection logic, threat models, and tabletop exercises focused on fraud-specific
adversary behavior. No vendor integrations or tooling were announced alongside the release.
