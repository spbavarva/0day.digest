Securonix identified a new multi-stage malware delivery chain, dubbed
VEIL#DROP, that uses Blogger pages to deliver the PureLogs information
stealer. Initial payloads are suspected to arrive via spear-phishing or
drive-by compromise. Using a legitimate blogging platform as a delivery
mechanism is intended to evade domain-reputation-based defenses. Defenders
should treat unexpected Blogger-hosted content flowing into endpoint
download activity as suspicious.
