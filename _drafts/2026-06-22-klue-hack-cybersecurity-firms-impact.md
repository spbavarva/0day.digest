Additional security vendors have confirmed exposure from a breach at Klue, a competitive-intelligence platform used internally by many security firms. HackerOne, Huntress, Jamf, OneTrust, Recorded Future, Snyk, and Tanium are among the customers now disclosing impact. The incident shows how a breach at a single third-party SaaS vendor can cascade across a large slice of the security industry at once. Affected organizations should review what data they shared with Klue and whether it included sensitive internal intelligence.
