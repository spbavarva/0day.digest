More than a dozen customers of competitive-intelligence vendor Klue have
confirmed that attackers stole data from their connected Salesforce
instances, according to SecurityWeek. Security vendors BeyondTrust and
LastPass are among the newly confirmed victims, with the breach traced
to abuse of Klue's OAuth integration with Salesforce. The incident,
first disclosed earlier this month, continues to widen as more affected
organizations come forward. Organizations that connected the Klue
Battlecards app to Salesforce should review access logs for anomalous
activity tied to its OAuth tokens.
