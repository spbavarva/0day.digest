Healthcare utilization management vendor Xsolis disclosed that threat actors
gained access to personal and protected health information it received from
client healthcare organizations, affecting roughly 1.4 million individuals.
The breach underscores the downstream exposure healthcare providers carry
through third-party data-processing vendors. No technical details on the
intrusion vector have been disclosed.
