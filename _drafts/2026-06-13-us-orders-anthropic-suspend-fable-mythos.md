On Friday evening, the US government ordered Anthropic to block all foreign
nationals, both inside and outside the US, from accessing its most capable
models, Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5, citing national security concerns. The
order also covers Anthropic's own employees.

Anthropic said it received the directive at 5:21 p.m. ET and, to comply,
disabled both models for all users worldwide rather than attempting to
restrict access by nationality. The company described the move as compliance
with new export controls.

Anthropic is complying with the order but disputes its basis, characterizing
the jailbreak capability the government cited as narrow and widely available
elsewhere.
