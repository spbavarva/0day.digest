Palo Alto Networks' Unit 42 published an updated analysis of the npm supply
chain threat landscape following the Shai-Hulud campaign. The report covers
the evolution of wormable malware, CI/CD persistence techniques, and
multi-stage attack patterns observed in the npm ecosystem. It's intended as
a reference for teams assessing their exposure to npm-based supply chain
attacks and hardening CI/CD pipelines against similar techniques. No new
CVEs or specific package compromises were announced alongside this update.
