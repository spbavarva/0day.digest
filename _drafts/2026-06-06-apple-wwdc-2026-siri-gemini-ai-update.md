At WWDC 2026, Apple is reintroducing an updated Siri with apparent Gemini
integration, continuing its strategy of partnering with frontier AI labs to
close the gap with competitors. Apple has been rebuilding Siri's underlying
architecture after slow progress on Apple Intelligence features.

The Gemini partnership mirrors Apple's earlier arrangement with OpenAI, signaling
that device manufacturers and OS vendors are increasingly relying on external
frontier models rather than developing fully in-house AI capabilities. Privacy
handling details — particularly on-device vs. cloud processing boundaries for
Gemini queries — are expected at the full WWDC keynote.
