The Texas Parks and Wildlife Department disclosed a breach at its
license-system vendor that exposed personal information, including
driver's license data, for more than 3 million individuals. No further
technical detail on the cause or vendor was provided in the disclosure.
Affected individuals should watch for phishing or identity-fraud attempts
referencing their driver's license information.
