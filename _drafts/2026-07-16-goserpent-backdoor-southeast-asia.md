Kaspersky researchers detailed a two-phase campaign using the GoSerpent
backdoor alongside the Stowaway RAT, ThumbcacheService, and other tools to
steal data from government entities in Southeast Asia. The toolset shows
continued evolution in data collection and exfiltration sophistication,
indicating an actively maintained, targeted espionage operation against
regional government targets.
