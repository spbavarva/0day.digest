Trail of Bits paired its engineers with open-source maintainers and frontier models, including GPT-5.5-Cyber, as part of OpenAI's Daybreak initiative. In the program's first week, the effort produced hundreds of discovered bugs, 64 pull requests, and 51 filed issues across 19 open-source projects, with more still under coordinated disclosure. The results suggest frontier coding models can meaningfully accelerate open-source vulnerability triage when paired with experienced engineers rather than run unsupervised.
