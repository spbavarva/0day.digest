US prosecutors say 19-year-old Peter Stokes, now extradited to the US,
was a member of Scattered Spider, a hacking group linked to more than 100
network intrusions and over $100 million in ransom payments. The
extradition marks a legal action against an individual tied to one of
the most active English-speaking cybercrime groups of recent years.
