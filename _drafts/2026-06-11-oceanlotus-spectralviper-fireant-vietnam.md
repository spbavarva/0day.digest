The Vietnam-aligned threat actor OceanLotus has been linked to two campaigns, tracked together as "FireAnt," deploying a backdoor called SPECTRALVIPER.
One campaign ran a prolonged espionage operation against a Vietnamese infrastructure and transport construction corporation between mid-2024 and February 2026.
A second campaign used a supply chain attack to target domestic stock investors.
The activity underscores OceanLotus's continued focus on Vietnamese critical infrastructure and financial-sector targets.
