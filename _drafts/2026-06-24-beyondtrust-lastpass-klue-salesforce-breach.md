Over a dozen customers of competitive-intelligence vendor Klue, including
BeyondTrust and LastPass, have confirmed that attackers stole data from
their Salesforce instances through the Klue integration. The incident is
another example of a third-party SaaS integration becoming the entry point
for data theft against otherwise well-defended organizations.
