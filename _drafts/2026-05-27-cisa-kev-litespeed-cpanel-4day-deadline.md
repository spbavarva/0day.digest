CISA added a critical vulnerability in the LiteSpeed cPanel user-end plugin to
its Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog, giving federal agencies just four
days to remediate — a compressed deadline that signals active, widespread
exploitation.

Any organization running cPanel with the LiteSpeed plugin should apply the
available patch immediately regardless of FISMA obligations. The four-day window
reflects CISA's assessment of active in-the-wild exploitation. Check cPanel
panel logs for unusual LiteSpeed plugin activity as an indicator of prior
compromise.
