Law enforcement from the Netherlands, Canada, Germany, and the U.S.
disrupted malicious infrastructure tied to SocGholish — a malware loader
linked to Russia's Evil Corp — and cleaned up nearly 15,000 infected
WordPress sites as part of the ongoing Operation Endgame. Dutch
investigators said the action cuts off cybercriminal access to the
infected systems. Site operators previously compromised should still
verify their WordPress installs and plugins are fully patched, since the
takedown addresses the infrastructure, not the original infection vector.
