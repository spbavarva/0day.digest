Google Cloud is incorporating Wiz capabilities into a new "agentic defense" platform intended to automate threat detection and remediation against AI-driven attacks. The move signals cloud providers increasingly betting on agentic automation for security operations rather than purely manual or rules-based workflows. Practitioners evaluating CNAPP/CSPM tooling should watch how these agentic capabilities are scoped and audited before granting them remediation permissions in production.
