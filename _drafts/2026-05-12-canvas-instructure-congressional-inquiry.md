The U.S. House Committee on Homeland Security is calling on Instructure executives to testify
about two ShinyHunters attacks on the Canvas learning management system. The attacks allowed
the extortion group to steal student data and disrupt schools during final exams. Canvas is
used by millions of students across thousands of institutions in the US and internationally.

Congressional scrutiny follows Instructure's reported ransom "agreement" with ShinyHunters to
suppress the leak. Institutions using Canvas should ensure their incident response documentation
and breach notification timelines are in order ahead of potential regulatory follow-up stemming
from the committee inquiry.
