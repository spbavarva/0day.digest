Google Cloud announced expansions to its Confidential Computing capabilities
aimed at protecting AI workloads, scaling hardware-based Trusted Execution
Environments (TEEs) with verifiable data integrity across its global
infrastructure. The goal is to keep data used in AI inference and
fine-tuning cryptographically protected while in use. Organizations running
sensitive AI workloads on Google Cloud should evaluate the new Confidential
AI capabilities for workloads handling regulated or sensitive data.
