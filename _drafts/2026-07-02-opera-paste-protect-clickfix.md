Opera introduced a browser feature called Paste Protect designed to block
ClickFix-style attacks, which trick users into pasting and executing
malicious commands via social engineering. BleepingComputer reports the
feature targets the clipboard-paste step that ClickFix campaigns rely on
to get victims to run attacker-supplied commands. The launch follows a
string of ClickFix and ConsentFix campaigns targeting browsers and
Microsoft 365 accounts this week.
