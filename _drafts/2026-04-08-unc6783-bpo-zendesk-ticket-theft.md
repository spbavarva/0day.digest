Google researchers documented UNC6783, a newly tracked threat group that
compromises business process outsourcing (BPO) providers to gain access to
high-value companies across multiple sectors. The group's focus on stealing
Zendesk support tickets suggests an intent to harvest credentials, PII, and
sensitive internal communications from victim organizations.

BPO and managed service providers should treat their access to client systems
as a high-value attack surface. Companies using third-party support providers
should audit what data those providers can access and whether support ticket
contents could expose sensitive material.
