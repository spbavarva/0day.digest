CISA added four actively exploited vulnerabilities to its Known Exploited
Vulnerabilities catalog, citing evidence of in-the-wild exploitation. The
list includes CVE-2026-48282, a CVSS 10.0 path traversal flaw in Adobe
ColdFusion that enables arbitrary code execution, alongside flaws in Joomla
and the Langflow AI workflow platform. Federal agencies have been ordered to
patch the ColdFusion flaw by Friday. Langflow's inclusion extends the
exploited-vulnerability list into AI application tooling. Organizations
running any of the four affected products should prioritize patching
immediately.
