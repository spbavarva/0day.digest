An open source, AI-driven system called ScamBuster adopts victim personas
to actively engage with phishing and scam attackers. The tool is designed
to let organizations and law enforcement gather intelligence on
cybercriminal operations by drawing scammers into extended, AI-managed
conversations. It's positioned as a defensive counter-engagement tool
rather than a passive detection system.
