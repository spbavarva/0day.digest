Adobe has patched critical vulnerabilities in ColdFusion that could allow attackers to execute arbitrary code or elevate privileges on affected servers. ColdFusion has a history of being targeted by attackers shortly after patches are disclosed, as reverse-engineering the fix often reveals the underlying flaw. No public proof-of-concept or active exploitation has been confirmed at this time. Administrators running ColdFusion should apply Adobe's updates as soon as possible given the platform's track record.
