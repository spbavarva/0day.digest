The UN World Food Programme disclosed that unauthorized parties accessed its self-registration application for Palestine, exposing data for approximately 600,000 households enrolled in Gaza aid distribution. WFP notified affected recipients via Telegram over the weekend. The breach affects one of the most vulnerable populations receiving humanitarian assistance; the exposed registration data could be used to interfere with aid delivery or to identify and target individuals. WFP has opened an investigation.
