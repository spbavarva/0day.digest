Cisco Talos reports that China-linked threat actor UAT-7810 is refining
its bespoke malware to expand LapDogs, an Operational Relay Box network
first identified in June 2025, by compromising internet-facing networking
devices. The latest addition, LONGLEASH, is used to maintain and grow the
ORB infrastructure. Organizations running internet-facing SOHO or
enterprise networking gear should verify patch levels and monitor for
unusual outbound traffic consistent with ORB relay activity.
