A viral image appearing to show Kentucky Senator Mitch McConnell in
apparent medical distress was confirmed as an AI-generated fake using
Google's deepfake detection system. The case is a practical example of
detection tooling being applied to debunk a specific piece of
AI-generated misinformation as it spread.
