Kaspersky's GReAT team detailed incidents tied to "The Gentlemen," a
ransomware-as-a-service group, including a newly identified ransomware
variant and custom backdoors. Researchers disclosed the group's tools and
tactics, techniques, and procedures based on observed incidents. The group
continues to evolve its toolset, indicating active development rather than
a static campaign. Consult Kaspersky's full report for IOCs and detection
guidance.
