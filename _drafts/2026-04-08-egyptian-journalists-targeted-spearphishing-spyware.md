Access Now and Lookout jointly documented an elaborate spearphishing campaign
targeting two prominent Egyptian journalists. Researchers found evidence the
campaign infrastructure may have been used to deliver spyware and exfiltrate
data from victim devices.

The operation fits a recurring pattern of commercial or state-linked spyware use
against civil society targets. Journalists and activists in high-risk environments
should review Access Now's Digital Security Helpline resources and ensure device
security hygiene is current.
