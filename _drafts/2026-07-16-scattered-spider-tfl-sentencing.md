Two leading members of the Scattered Spider cybercrime collective have been
sentenced to more than five years in prison for the 2024 cyberattack
against Transport for London, which caused an estimated £29 million in
damages. The sentencing reflects continued law enforcement pressure on the
loosely affiliated hacking collective, which has been linked to intrusions
against a string of major organizations.
