Google's Chrome 150 update resolves six critical and high-severity
use-after-free vulnerabilities described as severe memory safety bugs.
The fix is available now, and users should update their browser through
Chrome's standard update mechanism. No further technical detail on
individual CVEs or active exploitation was included in the initial
report.
