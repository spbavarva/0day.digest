Chinese AI lab Moonshot AI announced Kimi K3, described as their most
capable model to date with 2.8 trillion parameters. It's available now via
Moonshot's website and API, with an open-weight release promised by
July 27, 2026. Moonshot is calling it the first "open 3T-class model,"
surpassing DeepSeek's 1.6T V4 Pro. Moonshot's self-reported benchmarks show
K3 outperforming Claude Opus 4.8 Max and GPT-5.5 High, while trailing
Claude Fable 5 and GPT-5.6 Sol.
