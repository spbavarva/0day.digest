Dark Reading reports on a cryptomining incident that illustrates how AI
gateways — the middleware that routes requests to AI models — can become a
pivot point for attackers. Once compromised, an AI gateway can expose
access to AI models, cloud infrastructure, and identity and access
management (IAM) data, giving attackers a path well beyond the AI service
itself. Organizations should treat AI gateway credentials and IAM scope
with the same rigor as other high-privilege cloud infrastructure.
