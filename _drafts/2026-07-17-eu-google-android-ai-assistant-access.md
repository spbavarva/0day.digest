The European Commission has ordered Google to give rival AI assistants the same Android system access that Gemini already has: camera, microphone, on-screen content, an always-listening wake word, and the ability to drive other apps in the background by simulating taps and typing. Google must ship this in Android 18, with a hard deadline of August 1, 2027. Opening this level of OS access to third-party assistants meaningfully expands Android's attack surface, and practitioners building or securing Android fleets should factor rival assistants with deep device permissions into their threat models.
