Citizen Lab found that former MEP Stelios Kouloglou was repeatedly hacked
with NSO Group's Pegasus spyware while he served on the European
Parliament committee investigating abuse of commercial surveillance
tools. Forensic analysis of his device showed the compromise occurred
during his tenure on that committee. The case adds to a pattern of
spyware being used against officials and researchers scrutinizing the
surveillance industry itself. Lawmakers and researchers working on
spyware oversight should treat their devices as potential targets.
