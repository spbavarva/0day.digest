Researchers found Pegasus spyware on the phone of Stelios Kouloglou, a former
member of the European Parliament's PEGA committee, which investigated
abuses of commercial spyware. Kouloglou was reportedly infected twice with
Pegasus while serving on the committee. The case underscores that even
lawmakers actively investigating spyware abuse are not immune to being
targeted by it.
