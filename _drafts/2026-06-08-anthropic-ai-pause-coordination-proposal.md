Anthropic is urging coordination across the AI industry to enable a potential "pause" in frontier AI development if risks from advanced systems grow too high. The proposal centers on verification mechanisms that would let major AI labs confirm that competitors have genuinely slowed or halted development, rather than relying on unverifiable pledges. The pitch reflects an ongoing industry debate over whether voluntary safety commitments can hold without cross-lab trust and monitoring infrastructure.
