The UK's signals intelligence chief described AI as "an unstoppable force" and
warned that Russia is stepping up hostile activity in a gray zone that falls
below the threshold of conventional warfare. The speech adds to a string of
Western intelligence warnings about AI-enabled Russian operations.

The UK intelligence community frames AI as both a capability multiplier for
defenders and a force amplifier for adversaries, particularly in disinformation,
automated reconnaissance, and accelerated attack development.
