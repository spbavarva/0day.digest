YouTube is rolling out its AI-powered likeness detection tool to all users aged 18 and over. The feature accepts a selfie-style face scan and monitors the platform for videos matching a person's likeness, alerting users to potential deepfake matches.

The expansion makes a practical AI-driven defense against non-consensual synthetic media available to the platform's entire adult user base for the first time. Previously limited to a smaller group, the broader rollout reflects growing platform-level investment in identity protection as AI-generated video becomes increasingly accessible to bad actors.
