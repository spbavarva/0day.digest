Kaspersky GReAT documents two recent extortion cases that combine RDP,
MSSQL, RMM tools, web shells, and — unusually — office printers, which are
used to print ransom notes directly. Attackers then encrypt victim systems
using native BitLocker rather than deploying custom ransomware, demanding
comparatively small ransoms. The report includes TTPs and recommendations
for defenders.
