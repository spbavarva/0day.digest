The FBI has disrupted First VPN, a cybercrime anonymization service that dozens of ransomware groups
used for network reconnaissance and initial access operations. The service's administrator has been
arrested. First VPN provided operators with clean egress infrastructure to blend into legitimate
traffic during intrusion campaigns.

The takedown removes a shared resource from the ransomware-as-a-service ecosystem. Defenders who
may have logged connections from First VPN exit nodes should treat those as potential pre-intrusion
indicators and review associated activity.
