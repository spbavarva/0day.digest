Apple's WWDC 2026 keynote centered on a rebuilt "Siri AI" with expanded conversational capabilities, iOS 27, and a new suite of AI-powered photo editing tools enabling generative image manipulation.
Apple is continuing its Private Cloud Compute architecture to process AI workloads off-device while preserving data privacy — its primary differentiator against competitors.
The announcements represent Apple catching up to capabilities already available in competing AI products rather than introducing new paradigms.
The generative photo editing tools raise questions about deepfake potential at consumer scale; Apple's track record on-device privacy controls will be critical to how this capability is received.
