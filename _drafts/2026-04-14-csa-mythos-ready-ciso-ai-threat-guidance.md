The Cloud Security Alliance has issued guidance urging CISOs to adopt a "Mythos-Ready" security
posture in response to AI models like Claude Mythos compressing the gap between vulnerability
discovery and functional exploit development. The CSA warns that high-capability AI models
enable attackers to move from initial vulnerability identification to weaponized exploit in
hours rather than days, shrinking the effective remediation window defenders have historically
relied on.

Recommended defensive actions include continuous vulnerability scanning, aggressive patch SLAs
that assume same-day or next-day remediation for critical findings, and investment in
AI-assisted defensive tooling to match offensive AI capabilities.

The guidance reflects an emerging consensus that AI has fundamentally changed the economics of
offensive security. The AISI's Mythos evaluation (see companion post) provides empirical backing
for this assessment.
