Ukrainian cyberpolice, working with U.S. law enforcement, identified an 18-year-old from Odesa suspected of operating an infostealer malware campaign that compromised accounts tied to a California-based online store. The operation resulted in roughly 28,000 stolen accounts. The suspect's age underscores the low barrier to entry for running commodity infostealer campaigns. No malware family name was disclosed at time of reporting.
