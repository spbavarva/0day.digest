Nissan has confirmed that employee data was exposed as part of a broader
hacking campaign targeting Oracle PeopleSoft deployments. The campaign
reportedly targeted around 100 organizations, though only a handful,
including Nissan, have been confirmed as affected so far. Details on the
exact data types exposed or the vulnerability exploited were not specified
in available reporting.
