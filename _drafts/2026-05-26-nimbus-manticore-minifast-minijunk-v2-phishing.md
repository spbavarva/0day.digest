Nimbus Manticore (also tracked as Screening Serpens and UNC1549), an Iranian state-sponsored threat actor, has launched a new campaign targeting aviation and software organizations across the U.S., Europe, and the Middle East. The activity began following the joint U.S.-Israeli military campaign against Iran in late February 2026.

The group is delivering two updated malware variants—MiniFast and MiniJunk V2—via phishing lures impersonating aviation and software companies, combined with SEO poisoning to intercept search traffic. Organizations in targeted sectors should review perimeter defenses, inspect SEO-facing content for indicators of compromise, and hunt for MiniFast and MiniJunk artifacts in endpoint telemetry.
