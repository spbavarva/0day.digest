China-linked threat actor TA4922 has expanded its targeting to organizations in the UK, Germany, Italy, and South Africa, researchers report. The group operates at a notably rapid campaign tempo and deploys a multi-family malware arsenal including ValleyRAT (aka Winos 4.0) and Atlas RAT (aka AtlasCross RAT) for credential phishing, malware distribution, and fraud. European organizations in financial services and government sectors should prioritize hunting for these malware families and review inbound email security controls for TA4922 TTPs.
