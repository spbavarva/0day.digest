A new phishing-as-a-service platform called Forg365 is being used to target
Microsoft 365 accounts. It combines adversary-in-the-middle (AiTM) proxying
and device code phishing — both of which can bypass standard MFA — with
AI-assisted generation of phishing lures. The AI component is used to
produce more convincing, tailored bait rather than to automate the technical
attack itself.
