Kaspersky researchers identified overlapping infrastructure between pro-Ukraine hacktivist groups
BO Team and Head Mare, including command-and-control systems operating on the same compromised
host. The overlap in tooling and C2 suggests active coordination between the two groups, which have
both targeted Russian industrial and government organizations since the start of the conflict.
Shared infrastructure between previously independent hacktivist groups is a notable escalation in
the ongoing cyber dimension of the Russia-Ukraine war.
