A U.S. government official said Anthropic's Mythos model identified
vulnerabilities in classified government systems during testing,
according to SecurityWeek. The official noted that flaws were found
within hours, but cautioned that rapid discovery does not mean the
model could have exploited them in that timeframe. Few additional
technical details were disclosed about which systems were involved or
the nature of the vulnerabilities. The disclosure reflects growing
interest in using frontier models for offensive security testing inside
sensitive government environments.
