Security researchers documented the Masjesu botnet, a DDoS-for-hire service
advertised on Telegram since 2023 that targets routers and gateways across
multiple CPU architectures. The botnet follows the established commercial
DDoS-as-a-service model, renting out IoT device capacity for volumetric attacks.

Organizations with internet-exposed routers or gateways should ensure firmware
is current and default credentials have been changed. The Telegram-based
commercial model lowers the barrier for non-technical actors to launch DDoS
campaigns.
