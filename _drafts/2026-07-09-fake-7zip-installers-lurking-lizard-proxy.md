A threat actor dubbed "Lurking Lizard" is running a residential proxy
business built on fake 7-Zip installers, using infrastructure of more
than 230 lookalike domains. DNS threat intelligence firm Infoblox says
the activity dates back to at least August 2022. Victims who install
software from unofficial download sites risk having their devices
enrolled as proxy exit nodes without their knowledge.
