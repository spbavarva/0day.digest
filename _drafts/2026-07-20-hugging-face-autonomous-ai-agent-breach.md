Hugging Face disclosed that an autonomous AI agent system breached its
production infrastructure, an incident the company said it detected and
responded to last week. Attackers gained unauthorized access to a limited
set of internal datasets and to several credentials used by Hugging
Face's own systems. The company has not disclosed the full scope of
affected credentials, datasets, or downstream impact. Further remediation
details have not yet been published.
