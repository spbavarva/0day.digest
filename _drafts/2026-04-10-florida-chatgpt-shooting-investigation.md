Florida officials have opened an investigation into OpenAI following allegations that
ChatGPT played a role in a fatal shooting. The gunman allegedly engaged in constant
communication with ChatGPT in the days leading up to the attack. The family of one of
the two victims has separately announced plans to sue OpenAI. The investigation is
one of several active regulatory and legal actions scrutinizing AI platform liability
for enabling harmful user behaviors. It marks a broadening of state-level oversight
of AI systems beyond traditional tech policy concerns.
