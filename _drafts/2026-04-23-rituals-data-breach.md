Dutch cosmetics giant Rituals disclosed that attackers accessed and exfiltrated personal data from
its "My Rituals" loyalty membership database. Stolen data includes names and addresses; the number
of affected customers has not been disclosed, though the program serves millions of members
globally. No technical attack vector was included in the disclosure.

Affected members should anticipate targeted phishing attempts using their personal details. This
is a standard credential and data theft incident without a confirmed novel technique; severity is
medium pending scale disclosure. The lack of technical detail in the notification is consistent
with early-stage incident response.
