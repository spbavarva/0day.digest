A breach of Los Angeles city attorney systems exposed sensitive LAPD files, with
social media posts advertising 7.7 terabytes of data available for download
before some posts were removed. The LA Times reported the posts have since been
taken down, but the data may remain in circulation.

The full scope of the exposure and the specific record types compromised have not
been officially confirmed. Law enforcement personnel records, case files, or
investigative materials stored in city attorney systems would be of significant
value to adversaries.
