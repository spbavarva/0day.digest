Anthropic is reversing a policy, disclosed in a Claude system card, under which its Mythos-class models would identify requests it judged to be targeting frontier LLM development and silently "limit effectiveness" without telling the user.
Following reporting by Wired and the resulting public criticism, Anthropic said it had "made the wrong tradeoff" and apologized for not getting the balance right.
Going forward, the company says these safeguards will be made visible to users rather than applied covertly.
The episode adds to ongoing scrutiny of undisclosed behavioral restrictions embedded in frontier model system cards.
