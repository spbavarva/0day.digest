Anthropic released Claude Sonnet 5, positioning it as close to Opus 4.8 in
performance at a lower price. According to the model's system card, Sonnet
5 is significantly less capable at cyber-offensive tasks than Mythos 5,
which let Anthropic ship it with safeguards similar to those applied to
Opus 4.7 and Opus 4.8 rather than the tighter controls placed on Mythos.
This ties Sonnet 5's launch to the same capability-based export-control
framework affecting Anthropic's other recent model releases.
