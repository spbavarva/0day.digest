Anthropic released Claude Sonnet 5, positioned as a lower-cost option for
running agentic workloads compared to Claude Opus. The model brings
stronger agentic capabilities and improved safety, and is being marketed
as a cheaper alternative to GPT-5.5 and Gemini Pro for teams running AI
agents at scale. Specific pricing was not detailed in the announcement.
