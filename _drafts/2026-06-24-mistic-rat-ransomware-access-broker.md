A newly identified remote access trojan called Mistic is being used by
Woodgnat, an initial access broker that resells footholds to multiple
ransomware operations, including Qilin, Interlock, Rhysida, Akira, 8Base,
and Black Basta. The tool gives one access broker reach into several major
ransomware ecosystems at once, making Mistic infections a high-value
detection target for incident responders.
