A malicious application impersonating Ledger Live — the official management interface for Ledger
hardware wallets — was available on Apple's official macOS App Store and drained approximately
$9.5 million in cryptocurrency from 50 victims over just a few days this month. The fraudulent
app passed Apple's review process.

Cryptocurrency wallet applications in official app stores have become a high-value fraud surface
precisely because users extend implicit trust to App Store listings. Any user who entered their
24-word recovery phrase into this app will have had their wallet fully drained.

Ledger hardware wallet users should only download Ledger Live from ledger.com directly. Never
enter a seed phrase in any app, website, or interface other than the physical Ledger device
itself — no legitimate software wallet will ever ask for a recovery phrase. Review all installed
crypto applications for authenticity.
