A banking trojan associated with Brazilian threat actors is actively
spreading among businesses in Portugal, researchers report. The shared
Portuguese language between the two countries makes Portuguese organizations
easier targets for social-engineering lures originally developed for
Brazilian audiences. No specific malware family name, infection vector, or
victim count was included in the available summary.
