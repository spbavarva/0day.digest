South Korea's Personal Information Protection Commission fined e-commerce giant Coupang 624.6 billion won (roughly $409 million) over a data breach affecting more than 37 million customers.
The fine is a record for the regulator and signals an increasingly aggressive stance on data protection enforcement in South Korea.
The breach affected personal information for the majority of Coupang's customer base.
Companies operating in South Korea should expect similarly large penalties for breaches of comparable scale going forward.
