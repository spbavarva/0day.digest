Unit 42 published a technical analysis of three chained zero-day vulnerabilities in Siemens ROX II OT switches that together allow privilege escalation and persistent root access. ROX II devices are used in industrial control system and operational technology networks, where compromise can translate into physical-process impact rather than just data loss. Operators running ROX II hardware should track Siemens advisories for patches and review network segmentation around these switches in the meantime.
