Progress Software is directly emailing ShareFile customers who run
on-premises Storage Zone Controllers, urging them to immediately shut down
their servers. The company says it identified a "credible external
security threat" targeting the on-premises secure file-sharing software,
though it has not yet published technical details or a patch.
Organizations running ShareFile Storage Zone Controllers should treat this
as an active precaution: take affected servers offline until Progress
issues further guidance, and watch for an official advisory with
remediation steps.
