A financially motivated campaign is using malvertising and lures of
cracked or pirated software to deliver Vidar infostealer bundled with a
cryptominer, primarily targeting small and midsize businesses. The
combination gives attackers both data theft and monetization via
compute hijacking from a single infection. SMBs should restrict
installation of unlicensed software and monitor for unusual outbound
credential-store access.
