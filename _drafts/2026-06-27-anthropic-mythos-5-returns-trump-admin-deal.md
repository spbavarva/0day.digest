After a two-week negotiation with the Trump administration, Anthropic's
Mythos 5 model is back in limited use. More than 100 US companies and
government agencies are reportedly authorized to use it, including their
non-American employees. The public-facing Fable 5 release is described as
still separately restricted, with terms not fully resolved. Details of the
underlying dispute and the conditions of the new authorization have not been
fully disclosed.
