Anthropic has announced annualized revenue crossed $47 billion in May 2026, up sharply
from approximately $9 billion at end of 2025, as the company advances toward a public
offering. Daniela Amodei acknowledged investor skepticism around AI's commercial returns
but pointed to enterprise adoption and API demand as the growth driver. The figure is
self-reported and pre-audit; nonetheless it signals Anthropic's continued momentum in
the enterprise AI market alongside OpenAI.
