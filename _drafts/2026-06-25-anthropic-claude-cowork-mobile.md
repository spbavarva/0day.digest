Anthropic appears to be testing mobile support for Claude Cowork, its
desktop-oriented feature for managing long-running Claude tasks. The mobile
version would let users monitor and manage in-progress Claude tasks from
their phone. Details on rollout timing or availability have not been
confirmed by Anthropic.
