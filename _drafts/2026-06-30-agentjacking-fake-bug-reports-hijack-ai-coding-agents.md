Researchers described "Agentjacking," a technique that uses fake bug
reports to hijack AI coding agents at scale by exploiting the agents'
inability to distinguish untrusted content from legitimate instructions.
Dark Reading frames it as another demonstration of prompt injection risk in
agentic coding tools. No specific affected products or mitigations were
detailed in the disclosure.
