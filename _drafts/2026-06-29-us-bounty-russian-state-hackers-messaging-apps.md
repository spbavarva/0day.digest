The US government is offering a $10 million reward for information on
Russian state-sponsored hackers tracked as UNC5792 and UNC4221, who have
been targeting US government officials, military leaders, and allied
personnel. The campaigns reportedly focus on compromising messaging app
accounts used by these officials. The bounty signals continued escalation
in US efforts to disrupt Russian state-backed intrusion operations. No new
technical IOCs were included in the source summary.
