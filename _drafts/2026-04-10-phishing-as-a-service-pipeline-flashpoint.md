Flashpoint analysts, working with partner financial institutions, have documented a
Phishing-as-a-Service (PhaaS) ecosystem that now operates with the specialization and
coordination of a legitimate software platform. The pipeline divides labor across
phishing kit developers, infrastructure providers, spam delivery services, and
end-user fraud actors. This industrialization means defenders can no longer rely on
tracking single threat actors — they need visibility across each role in the supply
chain. Financial institutions should focus detection on shared infrastructure
fingerprints and kit-level indicators common across campaigns from the same ecosystem.
