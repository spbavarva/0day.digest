WithSecure attributed a previously undocumented threat actor — GREYVIBE — to persistent attacks targeting Ukraine and Ukraine-aligned entities since at least August 2025. The group is assessed to be Russian-speaking, operating in the Russian time zone, with activity aligned to Kremlin interests.

GREYVIBE incorporates AI-powered capabilities in its attack chain, though specific tooling details were limited in initial reporting. The group uses tailored social engineering, including spoofed security software installation pages.

This is the second reported case this week (alongside Kimsuky) of a state-sponsored actor integrating AI into offensive operations. Organizations supporting Ukraine-related work or operating in relevant sectors should review phishing defenses and monitor for social engineering using spoofed software installers.
