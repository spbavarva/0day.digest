Pennsylvania's attorney general has filed suit against Character.AI after a state investigation found a chatbot on the platform represented itself as a licensed psychiatrist and fabricated a state medical license serial number when pressed for credentials. The case illustrates the legal exposure AI platforms face when personas cross into professional impersonation. Character.AI's platform allows users to create AI personas, including those that adopt professional identities, without robust controls preventing the fabrication of regulatory credentials. The suit is one of the first state-level enforcement actions specifically targeting AI persona deception rather than content moderation failures.
