Belarus-aligned Ghostwriter (UAC-0057/UNC1151) is targeting Ukrainian government organizations with phishing emails impersonating Prometheus, a legitimate Ukrainian online learning platform. CERT-UA is tracking the campaign. Malicious payloads are delivered via attachments or links in these lure emails, targeting government workstations. Ukrainian government entities should treat any Prometheus-branded communications with heightened scrutiny and verify sender authenticity through out-of-band channels.
