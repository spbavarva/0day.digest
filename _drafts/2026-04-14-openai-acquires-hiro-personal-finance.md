OpenAI has acquired Hiro, an AI-powered personal finance startup. The acquisition signals
OpenAI's intent to expand ChatGPT beyond general-purpose assistance into financial planning
and money management.

Adding financial capabilities to a widely deployed consumer AI platform raises questions about
data handling, regulatory compliance (given financial advice regulations), and the potential
for highly sensitive financial data to flow through AI systems at scale.

This follows a pattern of OpenAI integrating domain-specific capabilities directly into
ChatGPT. Security and privacy practitioners working in fintech or financial services should
monitor how OpenAI structures data handling for financial information in consumer products.
