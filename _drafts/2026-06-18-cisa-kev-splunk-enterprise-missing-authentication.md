CISA added CVE-2026-20253, a missing-authentication-for-critical-function
vulnerability in Splunk Enterprise, to its Known Exploited Vulnerabilities
catalog based on evidence of active exploitation. Under Binding
Operational Directive 26-04, federal civilian agencies must prioritize
patching KEV-listed vulnerabilities. Missing-authentication flaws in
widely deployed enterprise software like Splunk are a frequent vector for
unauthorized access; organizations running Splunk Enterprise should patch
immediately regardless of federal mandate status.
