Meta has disclosed that 20,225 Instagram accounts were hijacked after attackers abused the company's AI-powered account-recovery support system to reset victims' passwords. Meta has informed authorities about the scope and impact of the incident. The case is a reminder that AI systems deployed for customer support and account recovery can themselves become attack surfaces if their identity-verification logic can be manipulated. Teams building or operating AI-driven support automation should audit how those systems authenticate users before performing account-altering actions like password resets.
