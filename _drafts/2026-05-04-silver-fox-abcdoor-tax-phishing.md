China-based threat group Silver Fox launched campaigns targeting organizations in India and Russia using tax-authority-impersonating phishing lures, deploying a previously undocumented backdoor called ABCDoor alongside ValleyRAT and other malware families. The India campaign impersonated correspondence from the Income Tax Department; the Russia campaign followed a nearly identical playbook targeting Russian entities.

Over 1,600 socially engineered messages were distributed across both waves. Both campaigns followed a nearly identical attack chain, suggesting a templated operation running at scale. ABCDoor appears purpose-built for persistent access. Organizations in finance, government, and technology sectors in India and Russia should treat tax-authority email lures as high-suspicion, particularly during active filing periods.
