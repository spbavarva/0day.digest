Salesforce disabled the Klue Battlecards app integration after a security
incident at Klue, the competitive-intelligence vendor, on June 11 led to
abuse of OAuth tokens and exposure of customer data. The downstream
impact has hit other companies that integrated Klue into Salesforce,
including cybersecurity firms Huntress and Recorded Future. Organizations
using the Klue integration are unable to reconnect until further notice
and should review Salesforce access logs for anomalous activity tied to
the app's OAuth grants.
