SentinelOne's Week 16 cybersecurity roundup covers three notable stories. Authorities have taken down
the W3LL phishing ring, a well-known operation supplying adversary-in-the-middle phishing kits with
MFA bypass capabilities to criminal groups.

The AgingFly malware campaign has been observed actively stealing data from Ukrainian government
systems, representing continued nation-state exfiltration activity against Ukrainian institutions.
Separately, threat actors are exploiting a vulnerability in Nginx to hijack web servers in the wild.

Nginx administrators should prioritize checking for available security patches and reviewing server
logs for signs of unauthorized access or configuration changes. The SentinelOne post contains
additional detail and indicators of compromise for each of the three stories.
