Anthropic and California Governor Gavin Newsom's office have reached a deal
letting California state government agencies use Claude at half price.

The arrangement comes as Anthropic deepens its ties with California even as
it navigates a more contentious relationship with the federal government. No
further financial or technical details of the agreement were disclosed in
available reporting.
