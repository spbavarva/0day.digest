Anthropic's Mythos AI system has identified 23,000 potential vulnerabilities
across 1,000 open-source software projects. Anthropic reports that many
findings have already been confirmed as critical or high severity, with the
count expected to grow as scanning continues.

This represents one of the largest AI-driven automated vulnerability discovery
efforts disclosed publicly. OSS maintainers should expect inbound vulnerability
reports from Mythos-based tooling and prioritize triage for confirmed findings.
