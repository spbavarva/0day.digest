Novee researchers discovered an account takeover vulnerability in Pretalx, the
widely-used open-source CFP (Call for Papers) management tool. Successful
exploitation allowed attackers to take over reviewer accounts and approve any
conference talk submission with a 100% acceptance rate.

Pretalx is used by many major open-source and security conferences. Beyond
rigging submissions, an attacker could access private reviewer discussions and
sensitive submission data. Conference organizers running Pretalx should update
to the patched version immediately.
