Apple announced at WWDC 26 an Apple Intelligence-powered feature that
automatically detects and replaces weak or compromised passwords in Safari,
rolling out with iOS 27. The feature integrates with iCloud Keychain to
substitute breached credentials with strong generated passwords without
requiring user action beyond an initial approval prompt. This extends Apple's
existing Password Monitor (which flags compromised passwords) into an automated
remediation flow. The feature applies only to Safari on iOS 27 and later;
third-party browsers and password managers are unaffected.
