President Trump delayed signing a draft executive order that would have required US government
security reviews of AI models before public release, citing concern that the language would
impede AI development and competitiveness.

The delay leaves no federal mandate for pre-release AI security assessment in the US — a
significant governance gap as frontier models grow more capable of assisting with offensive
operations. No revised timeline or replacement order was announced.
