OpenAI disclosed GPT-Red, an internal automated red-teaming model built to
scale discovery of prompt injection vulnerabilities before tools are
deployed widely. OpenAI says GPT-Red is a strong red-teamer against which
"previous models are highly vulnerable," and that it's used to
adversarially train newer models, including GPT-5.6, against these
attacks. The approach reflects a broader industry shift toward using AI
systems themselves to find and close AI safety gaps at scale.
