Allen AI released OlmoEarth v1.1, a more efficient iteration of its open-weights Earth-observation model family, published via Hugging Face.
OlmoEarth is developed by the Allen Institute for AI (Ai2) and targets geospatial and Earth-observation tasks.
The v1.1 release focuses on improved efficiency over v1.0.
Full model weights and technical details are available on the Hugging Face model page.
