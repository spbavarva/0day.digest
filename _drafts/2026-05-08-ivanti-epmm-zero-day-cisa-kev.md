CISA added CVE-2026-6973, a high-severity flaw in Ivanti Endpoint Manager Mobile (EPMM), to its
Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog and issued a four-day remediation deadline for federal
agencies. The vulnerability allows an admin-privileged attacker to execute arbitrary code and has
been exploited in targeted attacks in the wild. Ivanti has released a patch. Federal civilian agencies
must remediate by the deadline; all other EPMM operators should treat this as urgent given Ivanti's
history of rapid weaponization.
