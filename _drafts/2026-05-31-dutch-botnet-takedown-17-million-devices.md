Dutch Politie and the National Cyber Security Center (NCSC) announced the takedown of a
botnet with at least 17 million infected devices — spanning computers, tablets, smartphones,
and IoT equipment. More than 200 command-and-control servers hosted in the Netherlands were
seized as part of the operation.

No TTPs or malware family name are available from the feed summary at this time. The scale
(17 million nodes) places this among the larger botnets publicly dismantled in recent years.
Further technical details are expected from Dutch authorities.
