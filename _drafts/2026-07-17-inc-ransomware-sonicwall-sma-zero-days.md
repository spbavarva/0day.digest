The Inc ransomware group is exploiting two zero-day vulnerabilities in
SonicWall's SMA (Secure Mobile Access) appliances. Chained together, the
flaws give attackers root-level capabilities on the affected devices,
turning a remote-access gateway into a foothold inside enterprise networks.
SonicWall SMA appliances are widely deployed for VPN and remote access,
making this a high-value target. Organizations running SMA appliances
should check for patches or mitigations and review for signs of compromise.
