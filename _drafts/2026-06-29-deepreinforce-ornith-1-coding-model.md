DeepReinforce released Ornith-1.0, a family of open-weights, MIT-licensed
models built on top of Gemma 4 and Qwen 3.5, targeting agentic coding tasks.
The lineup includes 9B and 31B dense variants alongside 35B and 397B
mixture-of-experts (MoE) configurations.

The release claims state-of-the-art performance among open-source models of
comparable size on coding benchmarks. It is DeepReinforce's first public
model release.
