Hackers accessed databases belonging to Spanish fast-fashion retailer Zara and stole personal
information from more than 197,000 customers. The breach was reported to Have I Been Pwned. No
technical details about the attack vector have been disclosed. Affected customers should monitor
for phishing attempts targeting their email and account credentials. HIBP subscribers whose email
addresses appear in the Zara dataset will receive breach notification emails.
