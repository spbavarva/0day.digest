Coca-Cola-owned dairy brand Fairlife has suspended U.S. production, including plants in Michigan, New York, and Arizona, following a ransomware attack. The company says it has not yet determined the full scope, nature, or impact of the incident. Fairlife's retail sales passed $1 billion in 2022, making this a disruption to a sizable food-and-beverage supply chain; further details on data impact should be watched as the investigation continues.
