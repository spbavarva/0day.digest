Anthropic has closed a $65 billion Series H funding round at a $965 billion
post-money valuation, nearing a $1 trillion market cap. The round is expected to
be the company's final private fundraise before a highly anticipated IPO. The
capital will support model development, compute acquisition, and international
expansion as Anthropic competes with OpenAI and Google in the frontier AI market.
The valuation signals continued investor conviction in Anthropic's safety-focused
approach even as the broader AI market sees intensifying competition.
