Researchers exposed Masjesu, a Telegram-advertised DDoS-for-hire botnet active since 2023 that compromises IoT routers and gateways across multiple CPU architectures. The service has operated largely under the radar until this public disclosure.

IoT operators should ensure firmware is current and default credentials have been replaced. The public disclosure provides defenders with TTPs and infrastructure indicators to detect Masjesu recruitment activity and ongoing C2 communications.
