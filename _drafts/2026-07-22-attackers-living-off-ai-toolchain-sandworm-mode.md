Researchers identified malware dubbed "Sandworm_Mode" that abuses trusted
AI tools and workflows to blend malicious activity in with legitimate AI
usage, making it harder to detect. By operating through channels defenders
already trust and don't heavily scrutinize, it represents a living-off-the-
land approach adapted for AI toolchains specifically. Limited technical
detail on the malware's mechanics has been published so far. Security
teams should treat AI-toolchain activity as a potential blind spot rather
than an inherently trusted signal.
