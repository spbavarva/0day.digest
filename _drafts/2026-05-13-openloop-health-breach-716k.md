Telehealth platform OpenLoop Health has disclosed a data breach affecting
716,000 individuals. The incident occurred in January 2026, with personal
information exfiltrated from its systems. OpenLoop provides virtual care
services, meaning the exposed data likely includes health-related PII subject
to HIPAA notification requirements. Specific data categories compromised were
not detailed in the initial disclosure. Affected individuals should monitor for
targeted phishing that leverages health data as social engineering lure.
Healthcare platforms remain high-value targets due to the combined exposure of
PII, health history, and financial data in a single breach.
