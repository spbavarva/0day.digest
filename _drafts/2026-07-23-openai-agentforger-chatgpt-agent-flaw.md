OpenAI patched a flaw dubbed AgentForger that could have let an attacker
create, insert, and remotely control an invisible autonomous AI agent inside
a victim organization's ChatGPT Agent environment. The vulnerability was
shared with researchers ahead of the fix being confirmed. Details on the
underlying mechanism and exploitation prerequisites were not specified in
the available summary. No evidence of in-the-wild exploitation was reported.
