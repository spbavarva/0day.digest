Anthropic launched Claude Tag, an always-on AI teammate integrated directly
into Slack. Beyond productivity gains, the feature is positioned as a way for
Anthropic to capture organizational context, institutional knowledge, and
enterprise workflow data over time. Organizations adopting Claude Tag should
weigh the data governance implications of an AI system with persistent
visibility into internal Slack communications.
