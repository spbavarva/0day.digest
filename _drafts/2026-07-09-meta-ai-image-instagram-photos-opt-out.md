Meta's new Muse Image AI model lets people generate AI content using
public Instagram photos and reels, including by @-mentioning specific
Instagram accounts to reference someone's public profile in generated
images. The feature is enabled by default rather than opt-in. Public
Instagram accounts should assume their posted photos may be usable as AI
generation input unless they opt out.
