CISA advisory ICSA-26-204-04 warns of vulnerabilities in Panduit IntraVUE
(versions up to 3.2.1a14) scoring a maximum CVSS of 10. Weaknesses include
plaintext password storage, a confused-deputy proxy issue, exposure of
sensitive system information, and inadequate encryption. CISA says an
attacker with access to the IT network could manipulate industrial control
devices without needing physical access, specialized insider knowledge, or
advanced tooling. Operators should apply patches and segment IntraVUE
deployments from general IT networks.
