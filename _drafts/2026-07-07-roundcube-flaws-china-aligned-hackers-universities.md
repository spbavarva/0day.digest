A suspected China-aligned threat cluster is exploiting patched Roundcube
webmail vulnerabilities, including CVE-2024-42009 (CVSS 9.3), against
physics and engineering departments at US and Canadian universities. The
campaign siphons credentials from compromised webmail accounts. Fixes for
the exploited flaws already exist, so this is an n-day patching gap rather
than a zero-day. Affected institutions should confirm Roundcube instances
are fully patched and review webmail logs for anomalous access.
