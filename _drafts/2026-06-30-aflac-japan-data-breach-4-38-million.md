Aflac disclosed a data breach affecting its Japan subsidiary, impacting
approximately 4.38 million policyholders. Attackers accessed the
policyholder portal multiple times between June 15 and June 25, 2026, and
stole personal and bank account information. Affected individuals should
watch for phishing or fraud attempts referencing the exposed personal and
financial details.
