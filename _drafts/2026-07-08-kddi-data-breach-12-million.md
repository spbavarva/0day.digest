Japanese telecommunications giant KDDI says a breach of an email platform
used by five internet service providers in the country exposed email
addresses and passwords belonging to more than 12 million people. KDDI has
not detailed the initial access vector. Affected users should change
passwords and watch for follow-on phishing or account takeover attempts
tied to the exposed credentials.
