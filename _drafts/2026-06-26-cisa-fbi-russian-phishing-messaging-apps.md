CISA and the FBI issued an updated public service announcement warning that
Russian Intelligence Services threat actors continue to target users of
commercial messaging applications through phishing campaigns. The advisory
updates a March 2026 alert on the same activity and includes recent
tactics, recommended mitigations, and samples of the phishing messages used
in the campaigns.
