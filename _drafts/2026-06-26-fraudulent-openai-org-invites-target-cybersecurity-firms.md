Threat actors are creating fake OpenAI tenants that impersonate legitimate
companies, then sending organization invites to employees at cybersecurity
firms. The apparent goal is to trick targets into submitting sensitive
company information through chats and projects inside the fraudulent
OpenAI organization. The technique abuses the trust users place in
OpenAI's organization-invite workflow rather than exploiting a platform
vulnerability.
