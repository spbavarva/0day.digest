Security firms coordinated the simultaneous takedown of all four
command-and-control channels used by GlassWorm malware. Disrupting all C2
infrastructure at once limits operator ability to reroute infected hosts to
backup servers, which is typically the immediate response to a partial
takedown.

Further technical details on the GlassWorm operation or affected victim
counts were not disclosed in the initial report.
