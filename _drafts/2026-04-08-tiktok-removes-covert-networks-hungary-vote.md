TikTok removed covert networks of fake accounts that posted and amplified
political content targeting Hungarian users ahead of a national election, aimed
at both opposition figures and the ruling Fidesz party. The company said the
networks used coordinated fake accounts to sow division across the political
spectrum.

The action reflects continued platform-level enforcement against coordinated
inauthentic behavior around major elections, and illustrates operations that
target multiple political sides rather than clearly favoring one.
