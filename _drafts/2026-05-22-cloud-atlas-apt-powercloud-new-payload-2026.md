Kaspersky GReAT published analysis of Cloud Atlas APT activity through the second half of 2025 and into early 2026. The group continues targeting public sector and diplomatic entities in Russia and Belarus with an updated toolset: PowerCloud (a new PowerShell-based payload), ReverseSocks, SSH tunneling, and Tor for persistence and C2. The updated tooling indicates active tradecraft development. Organizations in the targeted regions should review detections for these persistence mechanisms.
