Federal agencies updated an advisory detailing techniques used by Iranian
state-linked hackers to target programmable logic controllers (PLCs) from
Siemens, Schneider Electric, and Rockwell Automation. The update provides
additional information on the group's tactics against these ICS devices,
though specific new indicators of compromise weren't detailed in the
available summary. Operators of affected PLCs should review the updated
federal advisory and confirm exposed devices aren't reachable from the
internet.
