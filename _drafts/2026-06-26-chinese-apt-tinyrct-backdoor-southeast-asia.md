A Chinese-speaking advanced persistent threat group, tracked as
CL-STA-1062, has been linked to a new custom backdoor called TinyRCT. The
campaign targets government entities and critical infrastructure in
Southeast Asia, particularly state-owned enterprises in the energy and
government sectors. The activity was attributed by Palo Alto Networks.
