Krebs on Security reports that a cybersecurity startup offering millions
of dollars to acquire zero-day vulnerabilities in popular software is run
by a pair of convicted felons and far-right conspiracy theorists. Their
prior ventures reportedly included fake intelligence companies and a
now-defunct AI-based lobbying platform operated under assumed names. The
report raises questions about vetting and trust in the offensive
zero-day acquisition market.
