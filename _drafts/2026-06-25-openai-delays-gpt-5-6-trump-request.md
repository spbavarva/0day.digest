The Trump administration asked OpenAI to stagger the release of its next
model, GPT-5.6, over safety concerns. The Information reported that OpenAI
CEO Sam Altman told employees the company will release GPT-5.6 in limited
preview form, granting access to a small group of partners rather than the
general public, in response to the request.
