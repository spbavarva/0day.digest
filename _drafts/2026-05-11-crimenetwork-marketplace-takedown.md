German authorities dismantled the resurrected Crimenetwork cybercrime marketplace and
arrested its administrator. The second iteration of the German-language forum had grown
to over 22,000 registered users and more than 100 active sellers before the takedown.
Crimenetwork operated as a clearinghouse for stolen credentials, malware, and
cybercrime-as-a-service offerings.

The arrest continues a pattern of German law enforcement successfully targeting domestic
cybercrime platforms. Organizations that monitor dark-web credential exposure should watch
for data dumps previously sold through Crimenetwork appearing on other platforms following
the disruption.
