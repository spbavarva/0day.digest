Fortinet and Ivanti have released security updates addressing critical
vulnerabilities enabling arbitrary code execution and information disclosure.
Both vendors have a documented history of critical flaws that are weaponized
rapidly after disclosure — Fortinet and Ivanti CVEs regularly appear in CISA's
Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog within days of patch release. Specific
CVE identifiers and affected product versions were not detailed in initial
reporting; consult vendor advisories for scope and affected versions.
Prioritize patching any internet-facing Fortinet or Ivanti appliances above
other assets given the exploitation track record of both product lines.
