The FBI and CISA have updated a March advisory on a Russian-intelligence-linked
phishing campaign against Signal users. The operators have added a new step:
coaxing victims into handing over their Signal Backup Recovery Key, not just a
device-linking code. With the key, attackers can restore the victim's full
backup, read their entire private and group message history, and take over
the account outright. Unlike a linking code, the recovery key keeps working
until the victim rotates it, giving attackers a persistent foothold.
