Spanish police disrupted a cyber fraud ring responsible for an estimated
€140 million in losses. The group, based in the Iberian peninsula, carried
out a variety of cyberattacks and laundered proceeds through complex
financial networks. The action highlights the scale international fraud
operations can reach before being dismantled.
