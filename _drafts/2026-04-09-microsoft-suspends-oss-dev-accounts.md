Microsoft suspended developer accounts used to maintain multiple high-profile open source
projects without prior notification and no clear reinstatement process. The suspensions
are blocking maintainers from publishing new software builds and security patches for
Windows users.

The specific projects and accounts affected have not been publicly identified. The
incident raises supply chain concerns: security fixes cannot reach downstream users
while maintainer accounts remain locked. Organizations relying on the affected packages
for Windows deployments should monitor the situation and consider interim mitigations
until publishing is restored.
