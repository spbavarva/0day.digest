Security firm SOCRadar identified the FortiBleed campaign, in which attackers deployed custom sniffer tools on compromised Fortinet FortiGate firewalls to harvest authentication secrets. Fortinet has confirmed the campaign and disclosed that a database of more than 86,000 confirmed working credentials was assembled during the operation. The scale and the targeting of widely deployed firewall appliances make this a significant credential-theft event. Organizations running FortiGate devices should check for indicators of compromise, rotate credentials that traversed affected devices, and verify firmware is current.
