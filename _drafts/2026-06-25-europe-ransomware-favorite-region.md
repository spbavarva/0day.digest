After a global lull in ransomware activity, Dark Reading reports that
ransomware gangs are increasingly setting their sights on European Union
organizations and their suppliers. The piece frames this as a regional
shift in adversary targeting rather than a specific incident, with no
named victims or new TTPs in the summary. European organizations and their
supply-chain partners may want to revisit ransomware readiness given the
reported shift in focus.
