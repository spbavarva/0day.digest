Jacob Butler (23, Ottawa) was arrested in a US-Canada coordinated operation and charged with operating KimWolf, a DDoS-for-hire botnet assessed as a variant of the AISURU botnet that infected nearly two million devices worldwide. The US Department of Justice unsealed charges and is seeking Butler's extradition. KimWolf was sold as a commercial DDoS-for-hire service to criminal customers.
