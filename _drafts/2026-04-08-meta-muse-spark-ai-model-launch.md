Meta Superintelligence Labs launched Muse Spark, the first model release since
Mark Zuckerberg overhauled the company's AI efforts and established the new
division. The model now powers the Meta AI app and website in the US, with
rollout to WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and Messenger planned for coming weeks.

No security-specific details were disclosed. The launch marks Meta's first public
AI model release under the Superintelligence Labs banner following significant
investment and organizational restructuring.
