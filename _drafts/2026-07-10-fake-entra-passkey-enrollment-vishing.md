A threat actor tracked by Okta as O-UNC-066 is targeting organizations
across multiple sectors with voice-based social engineering that prompts
Microsoft 365 users to enroll a new Entra passkey, aiming to carry out
data extortion attacks. The group uses a panel-controlled phishing kit
built specifically to target the passkey enrollment process. Because the
attack abuses a legitimate authentication feature rather than stealing
existing credentials, organizations should train staff to treat
unsolicited passkey enrollment prompts as suspicious and verify such
requests through a separate channel before approving them.
