A hacker using the name "Misere" breached Tchap, the French government's sovereign instant-messaging platform, according to French officials.
Roughly 73,000 government accounts were affected, and the attacker claims to have stolen messages and user data from the platform.
French authorities have not detailed the initial access vector or confirmed the full scope of the stolen data.
Agencies using Tchap should watch for official guidance on credential resets and follow-up phishing attempts referencing the platform.
