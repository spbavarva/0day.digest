A takedown operation targeted infrastructure behind SocGholish, a malware
framework that uses traffic distribution systems (TDSs) to provide initial
network access to cybercrime groups, including the notorious Evil Corp.
TDSs route victim traffic through a chain of redirects to filter out security
researchers and uninteresting targets before serving a payload. The takedown
highlights TDS infrastructure as a high-value disruption point against
initial-access broker operations.
