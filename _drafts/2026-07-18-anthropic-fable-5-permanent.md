Anthropic announced via the @claudeai account that Claude Fable 5 will
become a permanent, included feature of all Max and Team Premium
subscription plans starting July 20, at 50% of usage limits. Pro and Team
Standard subscribers keep access to Fable via usage credits and will
receive a one-time $100 credit. The move reverses an earlier plan to make
Fable 5 available only through API pricing, reportedly a response to
competitive pressure from other recent frontier model releases.
