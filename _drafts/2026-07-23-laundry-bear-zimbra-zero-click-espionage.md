CISA and international partners are warning that Russian state-sponsored
group Laundry Bear (also tracked as Void Blizzard) is targeting
organizations running Zimbra Collaboration Suite email servers. The
campaign combines phishing with exploitation of a now-patched Zimbra
vulnerability to gain access to victim mailboxes. Targets reportedly include
Western government and commercial organizations. Zimbra admins should
confirm they're on a patched build and review the joint advisory for
indicators of compromise.
