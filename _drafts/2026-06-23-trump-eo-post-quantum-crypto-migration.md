President Trump signed an executive order on June 22 setting hard deadlines
for federal agencies to migrate high-value and high-impact systems to
post-quantum cryptography (PQC). Key establishment algorithms must transition
by December 31, 2030, and digital signatures by December 31, 2031. The order,
EO 14409, keeps national security systems on a separate migration track.
Federal agencies and their vendors should begin inventorying systems using
vulnerable public-key cryptography now to meet the deadlines.
