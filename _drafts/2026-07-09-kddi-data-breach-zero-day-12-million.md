Japanese telecom KDDI disclosed a data breach affecting approximately 12
million individuals. Attackers exploited a zero-day vulnerability in a
third-party system to gain unauthorized access to a KDDI email platform used
to support ISP operations. The vendor and software involved in the zero-day
have not been publicly named. No further technical detail was available at
time of writing.
