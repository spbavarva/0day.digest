Adobe's May 2026 security update cycle addresses 52 vulnerabilities across 10 products.
Many of the flaws carry arbitrary code execution potential, though none have been observed
exploited in the wild as of the advisory date. The affected products include widely
deployed creative and document tools used across enterprise environments. Adobe users and
administrators should apply updates through Creative Cloud or the Adobe Update Manager
and prioritize any products used to open untrusted files from external sources, such as
PDF readers and media tools.
