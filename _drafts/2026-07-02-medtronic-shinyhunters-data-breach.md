Medical device manufacturer Medtronic is notifying customers that their
personal data was exposed to an unauthorized third party in a breach
attributed to the ShinyHunters threat group. BleepingComputer reports the
notification but did not disclose the number of affected individuals or
the specific data types involved. ShinyHunters has been linked to a
series of high-profile breaches; affected Medtronic customers should
watch for follow-on phishing attempts referencing this incident.
