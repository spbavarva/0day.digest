Dozens of security researchers signed an open letter urging the US
government to reverse export restrictions on Anthropic's Claude Fable 5 and
Mythos 5 models. The signatories argue the ban undermines security research
and collaboration that depends on access to frontier models. The
restrictions primarily affect researchers and organizations outside the US
seeking to use or evaluate these models.
