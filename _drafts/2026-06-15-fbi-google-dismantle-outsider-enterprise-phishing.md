The FBI and Google dismantled "Outsider Enterprise," a phishing-as-a-service platform that operated more than 9,000 phishing sites.
The platform is estimated to have stolen nearly 4 million credit card numbers, causing roughly $1.9 billion in losses.
Details on the operators behind the service and any arrests have not been disclosed in this report.
The takedown removes a large piece of infrastructure used for payment card fraud.
