A new Chaos malware variant is targeting misconfigured cloud deployments in
addition to its traditional focus on routers and edge devices, adding a SOCKS
proxy capability that expands the botnet's utility for tunneling attacker
traffic. Darktrace researchers say the operator is actively expanding Chaos's
targeting infrastructure.

Cloud operators should audit for misconfigured exposed services and review
credential hygiene. Compromised cloud instances may be used as anonymizing
relays for further attacks, complicating attribution and detection.
