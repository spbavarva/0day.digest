Researchers showed that an Android app able to draw over other windows and
write to shared storage can slip hidden instructions to the AI agent
driving that phone, in text no human eye will ever see. From there, two
more steps let the same app run commands on the PC driving the agent. The
demonstrated attack chain, plus six related techniques, was shown to work
against five open-source mobile agent frameworks, including AppAgent and
AppAgentX.
