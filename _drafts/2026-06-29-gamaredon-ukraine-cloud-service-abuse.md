Russian APT group Gamaredon continued expanding its malware arsenal against
Ukraine throughout 2025, according to ESET. Researchers tracked 35 distinct
spear-phishing campaigns against new targets, most launched in the second
half of the year. The group has added new malware and is abusing cloud
services, likely for command-and-control or exfiltration. Primary targets
remain Ukrainian government and military entities. No IOCs are included in
the source summary; consult ESET's full report for technical indicators.
