Kaspersky GReAT's analysis covers Cloud Atlas threat actor activity through the second half of 2025
and into early 2026. The group continues targeting government agencies and diplomatic entities
primarily in Russia and Belarus, using a mix of new and established techniques to maintain
persistence in compromised systems.

New tools and payloads have been added to the group's arsenal since prior reporting. Organizations
in the targeted sectors should review Kaspersky's updated IOCs and TTPs for detection coverage.
