Craneware, a software provider used by more than 2,000 US hospitals,
disclosed that hackers gained unauthorized access to a subset of its data
environment. The Edinburgh-headquartered, London-listed company said the
stolen data includes employee and customer information, and it has brought
in outside forensic investigators. No details on the intrusion method or
timeline have been released yet. Given Craneware's reach across the US
hospital sector, downstream healthcare customers should watch for
notifications about affected data.
