Amazon has made an additional $5 billion investment in Anthropic as part of a circular deal
in which Anthropic has agreed to spend $100 billion on AWS infrastructure in return. The
arrangement deepens Anthropic's AWS dependency and mirrors similar reciprocal cloud-compute
agreements seen across the AI industry.

The deal follows Amazon's earlier multi-billion-dollar commitment to Anthropic and signals
that AWS is positioning itself as the primary compute provider for Claude model training and
inference at scale. For practitioners, this has implications for where Anthropic API traffic
is routed and which cloud region compliance requirements may apply.
