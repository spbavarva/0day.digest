The Gentlemen ransomware-as-a-service operation maintains and distributes
a dedicated EDR-killer toolkit, GentleKiller, to its affiliates. The
framework is built to impair roughly 400 distinct security processes
before the encryptor is deployed, and incorporates third-party tooling
alongside its own components. Defenders relying on EDR/AV alone should
assume affiliates will attempt to disable or blind those tools as a
first step, and should have tamper-protection and out-of-band monitoring
in place.
