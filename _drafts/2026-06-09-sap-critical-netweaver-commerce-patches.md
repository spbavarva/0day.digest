SAP released critical security patches for NetWeaver and Commerce products addressing flaws that could lead to sensitive information disclosure, memory corruption, and disruption of normal system operations.
SAP NetWeaver is a high-value target widely deployed in enterprise ERP environments; prior critical NetWeaver vulnerabilities have been exploited within days of disclosure.
No confirmed active exploitation reported at time of publication.
Organizations running SAP NetWeaver or SAP Commerce should apply the June patches immediately and prioritize these over routine maintenance windows.
