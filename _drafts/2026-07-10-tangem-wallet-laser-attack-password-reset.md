Researchers at Ledger's Donjon security team demonstrated that a
precisely timed laser pulse aimed at the chip inside a Tangem crypto
wallet card can reset the card's password to an attacker-chosen value,
without the old password or a backup card. Once reset, whoever performed
the attack gains full control of the wallet and can move out its funds.
The attack requires specialized laser equipment and physical possession
of the card, so researchers describe it as not an emergency for most
owners, and the flaw cannot be fixed with a firmware patch since it
targets the physical chip.
