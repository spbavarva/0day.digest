Anthropic has restored global access to Claude Fable 5 after the U.S.
Commerce Department lifted export controls it had imposed on Fable and its
more tightly controlled sibling, Mythos 5, roughly two and a half weeks
earlier. The controls were reportedly linked to jailbreak concerns. Fable 5
returned to Claude.ai, the Claude Platform, Claude Code, and Claude Cowork
on Wednesday, July 1. No new safety mitigations were described in the
restoration announcement.
