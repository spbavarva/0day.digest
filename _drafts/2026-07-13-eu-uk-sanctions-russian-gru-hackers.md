The European Union and United Kingdom jointly sanctioned dozens of
Russian individuals and entities in their first coordinated cyber
sanctions package, accusing Russia of coordinating a network of hacking
groups responsible for attacks across Europe. The action targets
GRU-linked military hackers and marks an escalation in joint EU-UK
response to state-sponsored cyber activity.
