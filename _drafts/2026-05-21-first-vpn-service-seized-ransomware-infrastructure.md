An international law enforcement operation seized "First VPN," a commercial VPN service that
ransomware operators and data theft actors used to anonymize their activity. Investigators linked
the service to ransomware deployments and exfiltration campaigns across multiple victims.

The takedown continues the pattern of operations targeting anonymization infrastructure rather
than individual actors. Threat groups will likely shift to alternative services or Tor, so this
disruption is unlikely to have lasting impact on the broader ransomware ecosystem.
