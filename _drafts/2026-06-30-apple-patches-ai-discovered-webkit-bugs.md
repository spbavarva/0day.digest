Apple released security updates fixing more than 30 flaws across iOS,
macOS, and Safari, including four WebKit memory-corruption
vulnerabilities that were discovered using AI tools — specifically
Anthropic's Claude and OpenAI's Codex Security. No active exploitation
was reported. The disclosure is a notable real-world example of
AI-assisted vulnerability research feeding directly into a major
vendor's patch cycle.
