Google expanded Binary Transparency for Android with a public append-only ledger that allows verification that Google apps installed on devices exactly match the binaries Google built and distributed. The initiative builds on Pixel Binary Transparency introduced in October 2021 and extends the same guarantees to the broader Android app ecosystem.

This is a direct countermeasure to DAEMON Tools-style supply chain attacks: by making the build-to-distribution chain publicly auditable, any tampering with official app binaries becomes detectable. Enterprise security teams should monitor Android security advisories as this program expands beyond Google apps to the wider ecosystem.
