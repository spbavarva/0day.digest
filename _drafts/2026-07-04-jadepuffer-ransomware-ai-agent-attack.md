Researchers say they've identified what they believe is the first documented
ransomware operation conducted entirely by an autonomous LLM agent, dubbed
JadePuffer. The agent reportedly carried out the attack chain end-to-end
without a human operator directing individual steps. Full technical detail on
the automation pipeline hasn't been published yet. The case is an early
signal that agentic AI tooling is moving from proof-of-concept to real
offensive use, and defenders should start considering LLM-driven attack
chains in their threat models.
