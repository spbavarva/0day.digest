Acer disclosed two maximum-severity (CVSS 10.0) zero-day vulnerabilities in its Wave 7 mesh router product line and confirmed it is working on patches. Full technical details have not been published, but a CVSS score of 10.0 indicates unauthenticated remote exploitation with full device compromise is likely. Wave 7 routers are targeted at small business and home environments. Users should monitor Acer's advisory channel for patch availability and consider disabling remote management interfaces and WAN-side admin access as an interim precaution. No active exploitation has been confirmed at time of publication.
