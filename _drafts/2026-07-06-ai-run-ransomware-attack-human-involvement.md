New details on last week's widely reported "first AI-run ransomware attack"
show that a human still chose the victim, set up the attack infrastructure,
and supplied stolen credentials, even though an AI agent handled the
technical execution. The finding tempers earlier headlines suggesting a
fully autonomous cybercrime debut. It's a useful data point for gauging how
close AI-assisted ransomware operations currently are to true autonomy.
