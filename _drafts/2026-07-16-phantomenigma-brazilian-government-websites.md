More than 20 Brazilian government websites were hijacked and turned into
malware delivery channels as part of a campaign dubbed PhantomEnigma,
uncovered by threat intelligence firm ANY.RUN. The investigation revealed
previously undocumented backdoor behavior and hidden infrastructure links
spanning multiple attack arms. Using legitimate government domains as a
distribution channel makes the campaign harder to detect via standard
reputation-based filtering.
