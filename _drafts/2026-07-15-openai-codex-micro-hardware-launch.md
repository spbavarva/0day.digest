OpenAI shipped its first piece of hardware: Codex Micro, a small
button-covered device designed to be used alongside its Codex coding
platform. It is unrelated to the separate AI-powered consumer device OpenAI
is developing with former Apple designer Jony Ive, which remains tied up in
litigation.
