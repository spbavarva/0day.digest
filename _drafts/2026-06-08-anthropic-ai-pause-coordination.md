Anthropic is calling for coordination among advanced AI labs that would allow the industry to collectively "pause" development if risks from frontier models grow too high. The proposal centers on a verification mechanism so that labs and rival nations can confirm that competitors have actually slowed or stopped their work, addressing the classic worry that unilateral caution just cedes ground to less careful competitors. No concrete commitments or governance structure have been announced yet.
