New AI startups in Asia are launching models positioned as Mythos-like
alternatives, offering similar capabilities without exposure to the export
ban affecting Anthropic's models in the region. The report frames this as a
market-share risk for U.S. AI labs, which may struggle to win back customers
in the region once local alternatives are established. The story is about
competitive and regulatory dynamics rather than a specific technical
capability jump.
