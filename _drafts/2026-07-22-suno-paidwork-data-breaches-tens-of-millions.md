Hackers leaked data stolen from Suno and Paidwork, two unrelated platforms,
affecting tens of millions of accounts combined. The leaked data includes
names, email addresses, phone numbers, passwords, and financial information.
No details on the intrusion method or timeline have been disclosed. Affected
users should rotate passwords and financial credentials and watch for
follow-on phishing or credential-stuffing attempts.
