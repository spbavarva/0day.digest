Chinese AI labs Moonshot and Alibaba both released new frontier models this
week — Moonshot's Kimi K3 and an updated Qwen release from Alibaba — which
the companies claim compete with the best offerings from OpenAI and
Anthropic at significantly lower cost. The rapid-fire releases add to
evidence that the gap between the US and Chinese AI frontier is narrowing.
Neither company has published independent third-party benchmarks
confirming the parity claims.
