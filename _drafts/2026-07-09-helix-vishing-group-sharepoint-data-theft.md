A new data-extortion group tracked as Helix is targeting SharePoint
environments using identity-focused attack methods rather than malware. Its
toolkit includes voice phishing (vishing), device code phishing, and MFA
abuse to obtain valid credentials and session tokens. Once inside, the group
focuses on data theft for extortion rather than deploying ransomware. The
techniques reflect a broader trend of attackers bypassing MFA through social
engineering rather than technical exploits.
