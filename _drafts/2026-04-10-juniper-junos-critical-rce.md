Juniper Networks has released patches for dozens of vulnerabilities in Junos OS,
including a critical-severity flaw exploitable remotely without authentication that
could allow a complete device takeover. Junos OS runs on Juniper routers and switches
widely deployed in enterprise and service provider networks. No evidence of active
exploitation was cited in the disclosure. Organizations using Juniper network equipment
should review the latest Juniper security advisories and prioritize applying patches for
the critical unauthenticated remote code execution vulnerability. Unpatched perimeter
network devices remain a common initial access vector in enterprise intrusions.
