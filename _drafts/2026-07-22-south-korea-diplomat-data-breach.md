South Korea disclosed that attackers breached the National Diplomatic
Academy's online education system and maintained access for roughly ten
months before detection. The stolen data includes personal information
belonging to current and former Ministry of Foreign Affairs employees,
including overseas diplomats. The government has not disclosed the
initial access vector, attribution, or the exact number of individuals
affected.
