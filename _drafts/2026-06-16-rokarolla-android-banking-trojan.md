Zimperium's zLabs documented Rokarolla, a new Android banking trojan targeting 217 banking and cryptocurrency applications with 137 remote commands. Operators can exfiltrate lock-screen PINs, read and send SMS messages to intercept OTPs, rewrite clipboard contents to redirect cryptocurrency payments, and disable Google Play Protect. The malware is distributed via fake TikTok and Chrome downloads, representing an escalation from conventional banking fraud to comprehensive surveillance and remote device control. Mobile security teams should push Rokarolla IOCs to MDM blocking policies and reinforce guidance to install apps only from official stores.
