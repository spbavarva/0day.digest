McAfee Labs flagged an active campaign, dubbed Silent Swap, that uses a
fake Google Notes browser extension to steal cryptocurrency. The clipper
malware stealthily replaces wallet addresses when a victim initiates a
transaction. It is delivered through unsigned installers observed in both
.NET and Golang variants.
