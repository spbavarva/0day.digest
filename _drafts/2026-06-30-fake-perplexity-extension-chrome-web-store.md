A malicious browser extension on the Chrome Web Store is masquerading as
the Perplexity AI answer engine. The fake extension intercepts users'
search traffic and collects their browsing information. The listing was
published on Google's official extension store, underscoring that store
review processes did not catch the impersonation before it reached users.
