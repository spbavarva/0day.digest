The ShinyHunters extortion gang compromised Vimeo in April 2026 and stole personal information belonging to over 119,000 users. The breach has been confirmed by the data breach notification service Have I Been Pwned. ShinyHunters has been responsible for numerous high-profile data thefts, often monetizing stolen data through sale or extortion. Affected Vimeo users should expect phishing attempts leveraging their exposed personal data and should change passwords, especially if reused elsewhere.
