A cyberattack against Instructure's Canvas learning management system knocked the platform offline for tens of
thousands of students worldwide during final exam season. Systems have since been restored, but the attack
vector and scope of any data exposure have not been publicly disclosed.

Canvas is one of the most widely deployed LMS platforms in higher education globally. An attack timed to the
peak exam period maximizes institutional disruption pressure — a common tactic in ransomware campaigns targeting
education. Organizations operating critical academic or SaaS infrastructure should review availability incident
response runbooks and assess dependencies on single-platform systems during high-stakes periods.
