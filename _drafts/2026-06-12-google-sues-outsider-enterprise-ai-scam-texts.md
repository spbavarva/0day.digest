Google has filed a lawsuit against an alleged Chinese cybercrime operation it
calls "Outsider Enterprise," accusing the group of using AI to scale a
scam-text campaign.

According to Google, the group sent roughly 2.5 million scam text messages
over a two-week period, scamming hundreds of thousands of victims.

The case illustrates how AI tooling is being used to generate and distribute
smishing content at scale. The lawsuit aims to disrupt the group's
infrastructure rather than address a specific technical vulnerability.
