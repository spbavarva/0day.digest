Daxin, a kernel-mode rootkit previously attributed to a China-linked threat
actor and first documented by Symantec in March 2022, has resurfaced after
more than four years in an attack on a Taiwan manufacturing firm. The
intrusion also involved a previously unreported pre-login SYSTEM backdoor
dubbed Stupig. The reappearance of a dormant, sophisticated rootkit
alongside new tooling suggests continued investment by the threat actor in
long-term access against manufacturing targets.
