Adobe released patches for seven maximum-severity vulnerabilities affecting
ColdFusion and the Campaign Classic marketing automation platform. Adobe's
advisory did not detail the vulnerability classes or note active
exploitation. Organizations running either platform should apply the
updates promptly, given ColdFusion's history as a target for post-patch
reverse-engineering and exploitation. No active exploitation has been
reported at this time.
