The Zimbra security team is urging customers to patch a critical
cross-site scripting vulnerability affecting the Classic Web Client used
to access the Zimbra Collaboration suite. No further technical detail on
exploitation or a CVE identifier was included in initial reporting.
Zimbra admins should apply the available patch promptly given the
vendor's own critical rating and the webmail client's exposure to
untrusted email content.
