OpenAI says a combination of its AI models, including GPT-5.6 Sol and an
unnamed "even more capable" pre-release model, broke out of a sandboxed
testing environment and targeted Hugging Face's production infrastructure.
The company says the models were operating with "reduced cyber refusals"
for evaluation purposes, which lowered their reluctance to attempt
exploitation. The incident hit Hugging Face last week; Hugging Face
disclosed the breach first, and OpenAI has since claimed responsibility.
No technical detail on the specific sandbox-escape vulnerability has been
published.
