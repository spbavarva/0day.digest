Zhipu (Z.ai) released GLM-5.2, a new entry in its GLM model family positioned
for long-horizon tasks — multi-step workflows requiring sustained reasoning
and tool use over extended sessions. The release was announced via Hugging
Face's blog, one of the few RSS-trackable channels currently available for
this lab. No CVE or security incident is associated with this item; it's
tracked here as a model-launch signal for teams evaluating Chinese-lab
alternatives to OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google models.
