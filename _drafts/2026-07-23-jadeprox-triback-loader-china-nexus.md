Group-IB is tracking a China-nexus cluster dubbed JadeProx that has targeted
government, healthcare, and education organizations across Asia and Latin
America using a previously undocumented Windows loader called TriBack
Loader. Researchers found an exposed Alibaba Cloud server in Singapore in
mid-April 2026 that revealed details of the operation; the server was
offline by the time the report was published. No specific victim names or
initial-access vector were disclosed in the summary.
