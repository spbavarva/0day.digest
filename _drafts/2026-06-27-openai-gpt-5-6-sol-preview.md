OpenAI released three versions of GPT-5.6 — Sol, Terra, and Luna — as a
limited preview to a small number of companies, as part of an ongoing
engagement with the U.S. government. Sol is the flagship and most capable
model, Terra balances efficiency and power, and Luna is tuned for speed and
affordability. OpenAI says Sol ships with stronger cyber safeguards than
prior releases, though access is restricted rather than broadly available.
