Google Cloud announced new VPC Service Controls (VPC-SC) capabilities aimed
at securing agentic AI workloads. As enterprises put autonomous AI agents
into production, those agents connect across many tools and datasets,
making network-level, destination-based perimeters important for data
protection. The new capabilities are designed specifically for agentic
workloads, extending VPC-SC's existing role as a boundary control for cloud
resources.
