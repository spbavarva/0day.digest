Law enforcement and private-sector partners, working under Operation
Endgame, took down 106 SocGholish command-and-control servers and domains.
The action cleaned up roughly 15,000 WordPress websites that had been
compromised by the SocGholish fake-update malware loader. SocGholish is
typically used as an initial-access vector, tricking visitors into
downloading fake browser updates that deliver follow-on malware, including
ransomware. The takedown disrupts a long-running distribution channel used
by multiple affiliates. Site owners should still check for residual
malicious plugins or injected JS even with the C2 infrastructure down.
