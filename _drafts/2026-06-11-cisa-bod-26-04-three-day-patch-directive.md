CISA issued Binding Operational Directive 26-04, requiring Federal Civilian Executive Branch agencies to patch critical, actively exploited vulnerabilities within three days.
The directive also requires agencies to review and update their vulnerability management policies with a focus on entries in CISA's Known Exploited Vulnerabilities (KEV) catalog.
The three-day window is a significant tightening from prior remediation timelines and reflects the shrinking gap between disclosure and exploitation.
Private-sector organizations may want to use the new directive as a benchmark for their own KEV-driven patch SLAs.
