Citrix patched six NetScaler vulnerabilities, including a new
denial-of-service issue dubbed the "HTTP/2 Bomb" attack and a high-severity
information disclosure bug described as CitrixBleed-style. NetScaler has
been a repeat target for real-world exploitation in past CitrixBleed
incidents, so the naming is meant to flag similar risk. No active
exploitation of these specific flaws was reported. Citrix customers should
apply the patches promptly given NetScaler's history as an attacker entry
point.
