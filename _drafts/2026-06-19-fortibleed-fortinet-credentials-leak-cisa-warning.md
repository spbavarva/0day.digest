A data leak dubbed "FortiBleed" exposed nearly 74,000 Fortinet firewall and
VPN credentials, according to CISA. The agency is urging Fortinet customers
to secure their devices in response. The exposure covers a large share of
internet-accessible Fortinet firewalls and VPNs, raising the risk of
unauthorized remote access. No detail yet on whether the leaked credentials
are being actively used in follow-on attacks. Fortinet admins should treat
any device that hasn't rotated VPN/firewall credentials as potentially
compromised and do so immediately.
