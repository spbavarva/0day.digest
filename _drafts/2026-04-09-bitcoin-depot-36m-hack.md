Bitcoin Depot, operator of one of the largest Bitcoin ATM networks in the US, confirmed
that attackers stole $3.665 million in Bitcoin from its crypto wallets after breaching
its systems last month. Hackers transferred more than 50 BTC by compromising credentials
used to access the company's wallet infrastructure.

The method of initial credential compromise has not been disclosed. The incident was
reported in a regulatory filing. Bitcoin Depot has not indicated whether customer funds
were at risk or whether the stolen BTC has been frozen or traced on-chain.
