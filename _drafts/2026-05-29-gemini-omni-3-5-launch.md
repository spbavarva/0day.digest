Google debuted Gemini Omni and Gemini 3.5 at I/O 2026, publishing nine demonstration videos showcasing their capabilities. Gemini Omni extends multimodal handling while Gemini 3.5 targets incremental reasoning and task performance improvements.

The releases continue Google's cadence of model updates across the Gemini family, competing directly with OpenAI's GPT-5.5 and Anthropic's Claude 4 tier models. No security-specific capabilities or safety evaluations were highlighted in the announcement.
