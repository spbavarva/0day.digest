Google released Nano Banana 2 Lite, also known as Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite
Image (`gemini-3.1-flash-lite-image` in the API), described as the fastest
and cheapest Gemini image model. Simon Willison's hands-on testing found
improved output quality over earlier Nano Banana models, though the model
still misspells text within generated images in some cases. No
security-relevant details were disclosed.
