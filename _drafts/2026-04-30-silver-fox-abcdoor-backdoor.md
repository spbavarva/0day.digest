The Silver Fox APT group is conducting a campaign against companies in Russia and India by impersonating tax authorities to deliver malware. The campaign deploys ValleyRAT — a known remote access trojan — alongside a newly identified backdoor called ABCDoor, suggesting active tooling development by the group.

Tax-themed lures are a recurring technique for Silver Fox, which has historically targeted East Asian organizations and financial institutions. The introduction of ABCDoor alongside established tooling signals continued investment in custom malware infrastructure. Security teams operating in Russia and India should treat unexpected tax authority communications as high-risk phishing vectors and ensure endpoint detection covers ValleyRAT indicators of compromise.
