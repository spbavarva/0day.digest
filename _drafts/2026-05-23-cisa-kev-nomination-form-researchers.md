CISA launched a public nomination form enabling security researchers, vendors, and industry partners to formally submit vulnerabilities for consideration in the Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog. Previously, additions to the KEV were driven entirely by CISA's own monitoring. The change should accelerate catalog coverage of exploited vulnerabilities by incorporating community-sourced intelligence from practitioners who observe active exploitation before CISA does.
