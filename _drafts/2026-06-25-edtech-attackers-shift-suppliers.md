Attackers are increasingly targeting the software suppliers that
educational institutions rely on rather than schools directly, according to
Dark Reading. Compromising an edtech vendor gives attackers a single point
of entry into many downstream school districts, raising the stakes for
supply chain security in a sector that historically under-invests in it.
