Palo Alto Networks and SonicWall both released patches for high-severity vulnerabilities that
allow attackers to modify protected resources and escalate privileges to administrator level on
affected appliances. Both vendors produce widely deployed network perimeter devices — firewalls,
VPN gateways, and SD-WAN appliances.

Privilege escalation to admin on these devices can give attackers full control over network
segmentation and traffic policy. No active exploitation has been confirmed per the summary, but
security appliance CVEs are frequently targeted shortly after public disclosure. Organizations
should prioritize patching on an expedited schedule.
