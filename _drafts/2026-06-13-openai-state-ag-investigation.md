Multiple US state attorneys general have opened an investigation into OpenAI.

The states involved have not been disclosed. The inquiry reportedly spans a
range of topics, including OpenAI's advertising policies and its handling of
users' health-related data.

No findings or enforcement actions have been announced, and the scope and
timeline of the investigation remain unclear.
