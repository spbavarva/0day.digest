Runway introduced a Media Router, a tool that automatically selects the best
available image, video, or audio generation model for a given request.
Developers can prioritize quality, speed, or cost, and the router picks a
model accordingly rather than requiring manual selection. The launch comes as
the generative media landscape grows increasingly crowded with competing
model providers. No pricing or model-list details were included in the
announcement summary.
