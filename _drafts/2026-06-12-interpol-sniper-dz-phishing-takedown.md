An INTERPOL-led operation, codenamed "Operation Ramz," disrupted Sniper Dz, a
phishing-as-a-service platform that Group-IB says had operated for roughly a
decade.

The effort ran between October 2025 and February 2026 and involved
authorities from 13 countries across the Middle East and North Africa. It
resulted in 201 arrests, including the platform's primary operator, known as
"Guedz."

The takedown removes a long-running source of ready-made phishing kits used
by lower-skilled threat actors.
