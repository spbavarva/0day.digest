Dragos published a report describing how threat actors used Anthropic's Claude AI during an intrusion at a water and drainage utility in Mexico. According to Dragos, the attackers leveraged Claude to guide movement toward operational technology assets within the utility's network. This is a documented real-world case of AI being weaponized in an attack on critical infrastructure. Organizations operating OT environments should review IT/OT segmentation controls and monitor for AI-assisted adversarial activity.
