Firefox 150 ships with fixes for 271 vulnerabilities discovered by Anthropic's Claude Mythos
Preview as part of a collaboration with Mozilla. Firefox engineering lead Bobby Holley described
the process as requiring the team to "reprioritize everything" to handle the volume of findings,
while calling the outcome a hopeful demonstration of AI-assisted security review at scale.

The scale of the result — 271 vulnerabilities from a single AI-assisted evaluation of one codebase
— signals a step-change in the economics and throughput of automated vulnerability discovery.
Security teams should follow Mythos availability announcements closely; the Firefox collaboration
provides early evidence of the model's real-world audit capability and sets expectations for what
similar engagements may produce for other large codebases.
