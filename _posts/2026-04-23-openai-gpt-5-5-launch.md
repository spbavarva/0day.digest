OpenAI released GPT-5.5, described as its "smartest and most intuitive to use model yet" and
positioned as the next step toward agentic computer-based workflows. The model excels at coding,
research, and data analysis across tools, following GPT-5.4 which launched last month. OpenAI is
maintaining a rapid monthly model iteration cadence in 2026.

The security-relevant dimension of each capability jump: improved code reasoning makes models more
effective at automated vulnerability analysis — both for defensive tooling (code review, fuzzing
assistance) and as a potential capability uplift for adversaries building AI-assisted attack
toolchains. No specific safety incidents or capability concerns were disclosed at launch.
