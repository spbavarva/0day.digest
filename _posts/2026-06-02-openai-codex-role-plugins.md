OpenAI released six new Codex plugins targeting specific professional roles: data analytics, creative production, sales, product design, equity investing, and investment banking. Each plugin bundles job-specific integrations, instructions, and context to allow Codex to approximate the workflows of professionals in those fields. The plugins are available within the Codex app. The release signals OpenAI's intent to expand Codex from a software engineering tool into a general-purpose AI workforce product covering non-technical knowledge work.
