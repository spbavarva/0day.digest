Anthropic released Claude Opus 4.8, its latest flagship model, shipping with a
new capability called Dynamic Workflows designed to coordinate swarms of subagents
for complex multi-step tasks. The company also emphasized improved honesty, training
the model to better acknowledge uncertainty rather than confidently produce incorrect
answers. Opus 4.8 is available via the Anthropic API; Claude.ai access timing was
not specified. The Dynamic Workflows feature targets developers building agentic
applications requiring orchestration across many parallel agents.
