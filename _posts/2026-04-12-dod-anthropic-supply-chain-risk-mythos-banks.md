The U.S. Department of Defense has formally classified Anthropic as a supply-chain risk,
a designation with direct procurement and contracting implications for federal agencies.
Despite that warning, unnamed Trump administration officials are reportedly encouraging
financial institutions to pilot Anthropic's Mythos language model.

The contradiction between a DoD supply-chain designation and executive-branch promotion
signals an unsettled federal posture toward Anthropic technology. Security and compliance
teams at banks or federally-adjacent organizations should review any existing or planned
Anthropic integrations against applicable vendor-risk and federal contracting requirements.
No technical vulnerability is involved; this is a procurement and regulatory risk story.
