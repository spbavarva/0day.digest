Discord has migrated all users to end-to-end encrypted messaging by default, bucking a recent trend: Instagram and TikTok both announced they are removing E2EE from their platforms. Discord's move strengthens privacy protections for its large developer, security, and gaming communities.

No user action is required. For organizations using Discord for team communication, this raises the baseline security posture of those channels. The shift also highlights the diverging approaches major platforms are taking to user privacy under regulatory pressure.
