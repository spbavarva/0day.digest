AWS has expanded IAM Identity Center with multi-Region replication support and custom vanity domain
routing for access portals. Organizations can now replicate their IAM Identity Center instance
across multiple AWS Regions to improve resilience and reduce latency for globally distributed
workforces.

Custom domain support enables organizations to front the access portal with their own vanity URL
without requiring changes to existing application integrations. The feature is relevant to security
and identity teams managing large-scale AWS SSO deployments who need regional failover for their
identity broker.
