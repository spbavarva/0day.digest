Adobe patched seven maximum-severity (CVSS 10.0) vulnerabilities across
ColdFusion and Campaign Classic. The ColdFusion fixes address flaws that
could lead to arbitrary code execution, privilege escalation, arbitrary
file system read, and security feature bypass. No active exploitation has
been reported. Given the CVSS 10.0 ratings and ColdFusion's history as an
attacker target, self-hosted deployments should prioritize patching over
the normal maintenance cycle.
