Home security giant ADT has confirmed a cyber intrusion after the ShinyHunters extortion group
threatened to publish stolen data unless a ransom is paid. ADT acknowledged the breach and said
a "limited set" of customer and prospective customer data was stolen.

ShinyHunters is a prolific threat actor with a history of large-scale data theft (Ticketmaster,
Santander, AT&T). ADT serves millions of residential and commercial customers in North America,
making the breach scope potentially significant. Full disclosure on affected data categories and
customer counts has not been provided. Customers should monitor for follow-on phishing using
home address or contact details.
