Google announced Google AI Threat Defense, an automated security system embedded in Google Security Operations that uses AI agents to detect, prioritize, and contain AI-powered threats.
The system follows a four-step framework: Prepare, scan and prioritize, remediate, and monitor — designed to match the speed of AI-accelerated attackers.
AI agents handle continuous monitoring and routine triage tasks, freeing security teams to focus on investigation and response rather than alert volume.
This is Google's first integrated AI-versus-AI defensive product available to enterprise Security Operations customers on GCP.
