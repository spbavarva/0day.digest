Google has announced a commitment of up to $40 billion in Anthropic through a combination of cash
and cloud compute — one of the largest single AI investments on record. The deal follows the limited
release of Anthropic's Mythos model, which is specifically positioned for cybersecurity use cases.

The investment reinforces Anthropic's position as a primary AI infrastructure partner for Google
Cloud customers. From a security tooling perspective, Mythos-powered capabilities are likely to
see broader availability through Google Cloud's security product line. The scale of the compute
commitment also signals continued acceleration in AI capability development, compressing the timeline
for AI-assisted offensive and defensive security tools.
