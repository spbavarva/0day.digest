IBM released the Granite 4.1 family of LLMs in 3B, 8B, and 30B parameter sizes under the Apache 2.0 license. The Granite team published a detailed write-up on the training process; Unsloth has already released 21 GGUF quantized variants of the 3B model, ranging from 1.2 GB to 6.34 GB.

The Apache 2.0 license allows unrestricted commercial use, making Granite 4.1 a viable option for enterprises that require open-weight models for data sovereignty, on-premise deployment, or cost reasons.
