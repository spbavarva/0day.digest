Researchers found portions of Claude Code's CLI source on a public S3 bucket
belonging to a third-party contractor. Anthropic confirmed no production keys
or customer data were exposed and has since taken the bucket offline. The
incident is a reminder: vendor-side misconfigurations are still your problem.
