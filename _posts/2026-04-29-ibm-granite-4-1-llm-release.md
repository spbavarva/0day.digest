IBM published Granite 4.1, an updated family of large language models, on Hugging Face. The Granite model family is IBM's enterprise-focused LLM line designed for code generation, reasoning, and business application tasks. The Hugging Face post details the architecture and training approach behind the 4.1 release.

Practitioners evaluating open-weight LLMs for enterprise deployment — particularly in regulated industries where IBM's enterprise support and licensing model is relevant — can review Granite 4.1 model cards and benchmarks on Hugging Face. The release expands options in the increasingly competitive open-weight frontier model space.
