OpenSSL released a security update patching seven vulnerabilities, including one data leakage flaw. The remaining six issues are primarily denial-of-service risks. No active exploitation has been reported for any of the issues.

Apply the update through your normal patching cycle. OpenSSL's ubiquity across TLS stacks makes timely patching standard practice even without confirmed active exploitation. Check the OpenSSL security advisory for affected versions and CVE identifiers.
