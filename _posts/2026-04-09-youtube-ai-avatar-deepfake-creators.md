YouTube Shorts is rolling out an AI avatar feature that allows creators to generate
realistic video clones of themselves for content on the platform. The launch continues
YouTube's broader generative AI expansion even as the platform struggles to contain
AI-generated deepfake scams and impersonation content.

No security controls beyond terms of service are described. Privacy advocates and
security researchers have raised concerns that normalized creator deepfake tooling
could lower the barrier for non-consensual deepfakes and fraud. The feature reflects
a wider trend of platforms adding generative video capabilities while relying on
policy enforcement rather than technical safeguards to prevent misuse.
